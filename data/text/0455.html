- Finance, Accounting, and Operations

- Government Services

- Global Services

Established in New York in 1990, Open Systems Technologies is one of the largest, privately held, staffing companies in the world. Job Description

An up and coming technology firm is looking for strong Data Scientists for their Machine Learning team

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Innovating around new algorithms and models to solve new problems
  • Development and testing of the core matching algorithms, as well as automatically evaluating and refining these algorithms
  • Prototyping, development and testing of various components
  • Porting and development of core algorithms and analysis methods for large-scale cluster deployment

REQUIRED EXPERIENCE

  • Strong math/statistics/machine learning background
  • MS/PhD in Computer Science, Mathematics or related discipline (or equivalent experience)
  • Experience with machine learning librarires (scipy, scikit-learn, pandas, Graphlab, etc.)
  • Experience designing and implementing new algorithms
  • Python coding experience
  • Linux and Mac OS experience
  • Team player

DESIRED EXPERIENCE

  • Successfully built large-scale data processing and analysis pipelines
  • Familiarity with cutting edge cluster computing toolkits such as hadoop or spark
  • Parallel programming experience
  • NLP and text mining experience
  • Database hands-on experience (SQL or noSQL)
  • Experience working in a software development team
  • Experience working in a startup
