We are seeking an accomplished Senior Network Engineer to contribute to all functions relating to the planning, implementation, operation and maintenance of all internal corporate networking and telecommunications infrastructure.

You are passionate about driving innovation and have demonstrated success with large scale business transformation projects. You enjoy partnering with internal teams and providing operational direction and support in close collaboration with management and key stakeholders. Most of all, you enjoy working with great people who are changing an entire industry.

Responsibilities

* Design, install, configure, document and maintain internal network infrastructure.

* Troubleshoot and repair network-related problems collaborating with IT operations team, third parties and key stakeholders.

* Ensure that network links and equipment are properly monitored and that alerts are addressed in a timely manner.

* Collaborate with IT and Director of Information Security to establish, monitor and enforce network access policies.

* Create and satisfy stringent SLAs associated with Internet Connectivity, Wide Area Network, VPN Connectivity and Wireless Connectivity.

* Monitor and review services and service providers supporting IP Telephony and Video Conferencing technologies.

* Create, maintain and improve documents supporting CIT network processes and procedures.

* Plan, test and respond to Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery events.

* Actively participate in scheduled "Off-Hour Operations", "Weekend Work" and "On-Call Rotation Shifts" to provide 24 x 7 support.

* Up to 10% Travel may be required to support global branch offices.

Qualifications

* 7+ years experience as a Corporate IT Network Engineer.

* High level of proficiency in the following routing protocols: RIP, OSPF, EIGRP and BGP.

* Expert level knowledge with Routers, Switches, and Firewalls.

* Expert level understanding of TCP/IP, including; DNS and DHCP.

* Hands-on experience with Cisco / Juniper (JunOS) network equipment.

* Strong performance tuning (QoS) and capacity planning, traffic analyzation (e.g. wireshark) experience.

* Experience with Infrastructure or SaaS based VoIP Technology/IP Telephony

* Experience with various monitoring solutions such as NAGIOS or Solarwinds

* Working Knowledge of wireless technology using Ruckus or equivalent service provider

* Working knowledge of Video Conferencing technology using BlueJeans or equivalent service provider

* Proven track record identifying and championing appropriate new technologies; ability to quickly adapt changes

* A self-starter who can prioritize work in a fast-paced and dynamic tech environment.

* A team player who can collaborate across all departments and with all levels.

* Must possess good analytical problem solving and troubleshooting skills.

* The ability to lift and handle packages/hardware weighing of 50 pounds or more.

* Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Information Systems or equivalent experience.

Desired Skills

* Experience at multinational corporation dealing with network service providers such as British Telecom in UK, or NTT in Japan

* Professional level Cisco/Juniper Networks certification (e.g. CCNP, JNCIP)

* Ability to design and implement LAN/WAN using MPLS or Metro Ethernet

* Audio Visual conference room technologies (e.g. Lifesize, Crestron)

* Automation and scripting
