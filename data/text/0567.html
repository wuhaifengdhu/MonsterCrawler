Primary work will focus on progressing the Par Provider Agreement Validation project. This will require gathering requirements, data analysis, building reference data, integrating new data and new logic into an existing SAS job (or rewriting it if needed), and working with multiple departments to validate errors and get them corrected. Secondary responsibilities will include learning about work done by the Data Quality Consulting team, and participating as needed/desired on their other projects. Weekly team meetings and training sessions will continue through the internship.

Excellus BlueCross BlueShield, headquartered in Rochester, NY, is part of a $6.6 billion family of companies that finances and delivers health care services across upstate New York and long term care insurance nationwide. Collectively, the enterprise provides health insurance to about 1.5 million members and employs nearly 6,000 New Yorkers.
