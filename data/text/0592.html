MANAGER, WEB ANALYTICS (OMNITURE) - 21408 New York, NY

Ralph Lauren Corporation is a leader in the design, marketing and distribution of premium lifestyle products in four categories: apparel, home, accessories and fragrances. For nearly 50 years, Polo’s reputation and distinctive image have been consistently developed across an expanding number of products, brands and international markets.

Position Purpose and Scope
The Manager, RLE-NA supports the RalphLauren.com US e-commerce business within the Ralph Lauren Corporation. Reporting to the Senior Director of Data Science, RLE-NA, this individual will be a key contributor to the success of our fast-growing retail businesses.

Key Responsibilities

Leading the development of actionable consumer insights to enhance the digital customer experience and drive conversion for the RalphLauren.com web site
Functioning as a power user of Adobe Omniture Site Catalyst & Discover and other analytics tools Creating, maintaining & delivering various dashboards & reports for key stakeholders
Conducting ad hoc analysis for internal partners as requested, including in depth funnel and conversion analysis
Incorporating industry web analytics best practices and methodologies (e.g., testing and behavioral targeting)
Combining web analytics with customer insights derived from the Global Customer Database
Supporting the maintenance and development of the web analytics platforms
Collaborating with internal and external partners to develop performance measurement plans for new product launches or campaigns; define targets and deliverables; and communicate performance results to key stakeholders and leadership team
Supporting Digital Marketing and Site Merchandising teams and developing strong partnerships with cross-functional and inter-company peers

Requirements
Strong analytical abilities
Demonstrated critical thinking and leadership skills
Understanding of business financials, ROI, & profitability
Excellent verbal and written communication skills; ability to present complex information in a easy-to-understand manner with clear recommendations based on data insight
Team-player; ability to work with all business divisions in a proactive manner and drive results
Adapts easily to changing situations and demonstrates flexibility in juggling priorities
Ability to provide and articulate Omniture tracking and implementation requirements
Bachelor’s degree
Minimum 1 year in a leadership role
Minimum of 2 year of Site Catalyst/Omniture experience; DTM experience a plus
At least 4 years previous work experience, ideally within the eCommerce and/or Digital Marketing space
SAS/SQL experience not required, but considered a plus
Exemplary analytical capabilities
