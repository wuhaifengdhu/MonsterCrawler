The candidate will work on many aspects of the research process including building and maintaining financial models, performing financial analysis and due-diligence and undertaking a wide array of industry analyses and competitive assessments.

The role offers considerable intellectual freedom to focus on analyzing consumer spend data from the Envestnet Yodlee platform and derive in-depth insights on financial and operational performance of companies based on the aggregated consumer spend data.

Primary Responsibilities:

* Initiate internal coverage of several sectors including consumer retail, subscription services and restaurants

* Conduct data driven research on public entities using diverse information sources including filings, news, market research and internal datasets

* Model and analyze company financials

* Work closely with data science and account management to support new sales and renewals from a research perspective

* Support the investor client base to marry fundamental analysis with insights from both unstructured and structured data sets

Education and Skills:

* Bachelors degree in economics, finance, business, accounting, STEM or related

* MBA a plus

* CFA a plus

* Series 7 certification or related a plus

* Strong qualitative and quantitative acumen combined with strong written and verbal communication skills.

* Ability to think broadly and creatively about a range of strategic issues affecting a business.

* Ability to make decisions under tight deadlines, at times with incomplete information.

Experience:

* 3 years of experience as a quantitative driven fundamental equity analyst at a buy-side or sell-side financial institution

* 1+ years' experience with programming and databases. SQL / Python / R

* Familiarity financial analysis, model building, DCF analysis, comps, M&A analysis, GAAP and sell side reports

* Complete fluency with Excel including pivot tables

* 1+ years of experience with Bloomberg or similar system

* In-depth exposure to a specific industry via monitoring company, news, and events

* You should possess a passion for the equity markets and the global financial markets broadly

* Experience with consumer-related sectors such as e-commerce, retail, restaurants and subscription based services is a strong plus.

* Proven ability to work well on teams, self-motivated, showing initiative, a positive attitude and willingness to work hard.
