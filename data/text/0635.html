WHO ARE YOU

The Data Science team leverages data, technology, and quantitative methods to build products that enhance the analytics capabilities of the Commercial Solutions organization. The Business Analyst will be responsible for partnering with Growth Strategy, Inventory, and Sales Operations on behalf of the Data Science team to gather business requirements, translate them into actionable tasks, and ensure that they meet the business' demands. The Analyst will also be responsible for assisting with ad hoc requests, providing analytics on Data Science product usage and success, as well as documenting best practices and methodologies.

This role will be best suited for someone who is highly organized and has a strong business acumen. Demonstrated experience analyzing large data sets in Microsoft Excel and SQL is a must. The right candidate will have meticulous attention to detail, strong organizational skills, and individual work ethic coupled with a team-collaboration mentality.

Qualifications

* BA or BS degree, preferably with a quantitative focus

* 2-3 years of professional work experience

* Highly organized with a demonstrated ability to work in a fast-paced, multi stakeholder environment

* True problem solver with proven ability to translate underlying business needs into actionable items

* Document methodologies and business processes. Ensure documentation is current.

* Fluency in MS Office, proficiency in SQL, and basic experience with Tableau or another data visualization tool
