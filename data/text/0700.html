WHO WE ARE

ContextMedia:Health improves health outcomes for patients, providers and life science companies. We provide technologies incorporating hardware, software and media content that educates, empowers and activates people living with a chronic condition to improve their quality of life. With presence in 25,000 healthcare practices nationally, we continue to scale 265% YOY and have been recognized by Forbes, Crain's and Inc for our culture, business growth and innovation.

We are a group of creative problem-solvers - with hustle and grit in our DNA - who fight until we win, and do so collaboratively. We believe in communication with transparency, purpose before self and ownership over outcomes.

WHO WE NEED

The Marketing Sciences team embraces the challenges of measuring behaviors and outcomes within the complex eco system of healthcare and positive health outcomes. With more than 25K physician practices serving more than 125K HCPs, data and analytics is a foundational team passion. The Director of Data Sciences will dive into a complex world of network, product, and provider data in order to power predictive modeling, program optimization, and performance measurement for ContextMedia:Health. You will be the champion for data governance and best practices working closely with the IT, product, and commercial teams as we evolve and expand our data and technology tools and infrastructure. In addition, you will guide our road map and vision for data sciences as you grow and develop a world-class data science team. If you fancy yourself equal parts theory and practice while embracing the challenges of statistical modeling, optimization techniques, and machine learning then this is the opportunity you have been dreaming of.

As a leader for this team, your success will be determined by the innovation and efficiency to which you use to drive our organization forward. You will be responsible for collaborating with internal stakeholders around our data architecture and work to ensure internal users have access to robust and effective data and metrics. As acumen around analytics and data visualization continues to develop, so must company adoption of tools and big data solutions. You will be expected to take ownership over adoption and each and every outcome that aligns with business objections. This will require you to take initiative, bring a diverse voice and to be fearless in trying new ways to enhance our services.

We are looking for an experienced data and analytics professional who can dive into the technical while guiding and advising our growth through actionable insights derived from advanced modeling and measurement. You must be a problem-solver, exhaustive, and results-centric. We strongly believe in white-glove service for our member physician practices and want someone who has a passion for setting the performance bar high, enjoys the autonomy and responsibility of owning fast growth, and thrives in an energetic team environment.
