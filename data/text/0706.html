FACULTY VACANCY ANNOUNCEMENT

The Computer Science Department of the City College of New York is seeking outstanding candidates for a tenure-track position at the Assistant Professor level.

The target area is Data Science as broadly defined, including machine learning, natural language processing, data mining, information retrieval, big data analytics and applications in areas such as bioinformatics, genomics, and natural and social sciences. Examples of subfields of particular interest within machine learning include applications in computer vision and image analysis, biomedical computing, and computational linguistics .

The successful candidates will be expected to be actively involved in research, teach both undergraduate and graduate level courses, mentor undergraduate and graduate students, and contribute broadly to the academic life of the department. The Assistant Professor will be responsible for teaching, research and guidance duties in Data Science. He/she will also share responsibility for committee and department assignments including administrative, supervisory, and other functions.

For information about the Grove School of Engineering and the Computer Science Department please see http://www.ccny.cuny.edu/engineering/index.cfin.

QUALIFICATIONS

Ph.D. degree in Computer Science, or equivalent, and n outstanding academic or industrial record are required. Also required are the ability to teach successfully, interest in productive scholarship or creative achievement, and ability to cooperate with others for the good of the institution. The successful candidate must demonstrate a strong commitment to excellence in undergraduate and graduate teaching and the ability to attract significant research funding. Must meet ICRA employment eligibility requirements for appointment.

The City College of New York has a strong institutional commitment to the principle of diversity. In that spirit, we are particularly interested in receiving applications from a broad spectrum of people, including women and under-represented groups. Upon request, reasonable accommodations will be provided for individuals with disabilities.

COMPENSATION

CUNY offers faculty a competitive compensation and benefits package covering health insurance, pension and retirement benefits, paid parental leave, and savings programs. We also provide mentoring and support for research, scholarship, and publication as part of our commitment to ongoing faculty professional development.

HOW TO APPLY

Visit www.cuny.edu, access the employment page, log in or create a new user account, and search for this vacancy using the Job ID or Title. Select "Apply Now" and provide the requested information.

Applicants must include a cover letter and curriculum vitae (CV), the names and contact information for three references, and a statement of research and teaching goals. PDF format is preferred.

CLOSING DATE

Open until filled with review of applicants to begin February 1, 2017.
