The Center for Marketing Science Innovation is a new team within Facebook focused on understanding consumer's response to media for applications in advertising research and product development. In service of this goal the Neuroscientist will work with vendors and internal Facebook partners to analyze data outputs from consumer neuroscience research and translate findings into meaningful results for advertisers.We seek an expert in neuroscience and data science with experience influencing the advertising industry and analyzing complex data sets. This team is instrumental in informing Facebook's overall product strategy, advertising strategies recommended to Facebook clients, and new measurement platforms used across the advertising industry. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in the media industry, research methods and statistics. Experience with implicit, biometric, and neuroscience research methods strongly preferred. This position is full-time and based in New York, NY.

Responsibilities

* Define research projects which will influence the advertising industry's approach to biometrics and brand measurement

* Partner closely with Facebook Marketing Science and UX research and other internal teams for joint project collaboration

* Work with Academia to lead collaborative papers

* Assess validity and rigor of research vendors by evaluating methodologies, analyzing data, and reviewing literature

* Analyze participant-level and aggregate-level biometric data for industry findings

* Deliver industry findings to internal and external partners and industry leaders

* Work with Facebook researchers and data scientists and outside vendors to develop new methodologies to research brand and product measurement

Minimum Qualifications

* PhD in Neuroscience, Signal Processing, Engineering or similar technical field with 3+ years' experience in applied research

* Experience in a range of research and measurement methods, including EEG, GSR, heart rate variability, eye tracking, facial coding, and survey analysis

* 8+ years of experience in signal processing and statistical analysis

* Experience in data analysis of at least 3 of the following: EEG, GSR, heart-rate variability, eye tracking, facial coding, survey

* Experience in Matlab, Python, R, SPSS

* Experience in solving problems and measurement prototyping

* Experience with statistical analysis of data

* Experience in presenting research to industry leaders and communicating analysis and results to any audience

* Collaboration and communication skills for working in multidisciplinary teams
