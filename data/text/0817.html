Software Guidance & Assistance, Inc., (SGA), is searching for an Business Analyst for a contract assignment with one of our premier clients in New York, NY.

Responsibilities :
  • Capture, build, connect and prioritize Key Risk Indicators originating from UT relevant controls (trading and non-trading) from various platforms
  • Provide an integrated view into the overall risk coming from various KRIs and allow management to focus on high risk KRIs, users, desk etc.
  • Use comprehensive Risk Models on top of KRIs, employee behavior and historical trends to create a risk profile for traders & trader groups
  • Provide meaningful insight on anomalous behavior, trend and correlation across KRIs and traders
  • Learn, adapt, reduce false positives and improve risk models with minimal human intervention through machine learning algorithms
  • Present and visualize KRI and UT risk data in an intuitive and user friendly way
  • Project work will span all phases of development from user interaction, business and data requirements gathering, relationship building with external business and technology teams, help with testing, and some production support.

Required Skills :
  • Minimum 5 year experience in Business and Data analyst role
  • Knowledge of fixed income & equities trading
  • Trade Floor experience
  • Experience with full software development life cycle
  • Knowledge of Relational Databases
  • Experience using various testing strategies Experience in creating BRD & test scripts
  • Experience with Statistical Analysis and Data Science
Preferred Skills :
  • Knowledge of data science concepts
  • Jira
  • Tableau
