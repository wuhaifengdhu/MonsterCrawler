Senior Data Engineer at Enterprise Select

New York, NY

Report this job

About the Job

Enterprise Select has partnered with a rapidly growing and well-funded startup company to help staff their ambitious data initiatives. We are looking for a senior level Data Engineer who is proactive, self-directed, and who will be a valuable addition to this fast-growing team.


This company is bringing data science and AI to personal finance, and providing tremendous savings and benefits to its users. They are backed by the tech industry’s top VCs and are growing quickly. If you are passionate about being a major player and building a meaningful product alongside a team of all-stars, we’d like to hear from you!


WHAT YOU’LL DO
  • Design and engineer robust, scalable application software architecture for use with mobile application
  • deploy and maintain application infrastructure to optimize operational efficiency
  • Ensure data pipeline provides clean organized, aggregated data into databases for use by Data Scientists
  • Develop, construct, test and maintain architectures, such as databases and large-scale data processing systems
  • Perform Extracting, Transforming and Loading (ETL) of very large datasets from multiple sources using tools such as Hadoop or Hive
  • Draw conclusions from data and recommend actions


WHAT TO BRING
  • 3+ years in data analysis or related field
  • 3+ years building, scaling and maintain data pipeline and systems
  • Strong SQL, NoSQL skills
  • Strong Python & database scripting experience
  • Understanding of data structures and algorithms
  • Master's degree in Computer Science or Statistics desired


Not only will you be working with the industry’s best and brightest on a cutting-edge product, you will also be working for a rapidly growing pre-IPO company that really takes care of its employees. You will receive extremely competitive compensation, equity, and a full suite of amazing benefits.



If you are interested in this or other related opportunities, please get in touch today and let’s talk about how we can be the bridge to your ideal career opportunity!
