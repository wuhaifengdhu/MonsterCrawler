As the O&G Digital Senior Staff Technical Product Manager located at the Oil & Gas Technology Center (OGTC) in Oklahoma City you will work with the O&G Digital team, the OGTC Digital Leader and cross-disciplinary technical teams to lead the end-to-end delivery of software/analytics prototypes. You will work with product managers, researchers and customers to define the specifications, and then work with UX, architects, researchers, data scientists and developers to lead the development efforts.
The OGTC is GE O&G’s research division developing the next generation digital industrial technologies for product offerings by O&G Digital. These prototype solutions will span the OGTC program areas of Reservoir Performance, Well Construction & Production Solutions, and Oilfield Facilities & Services and you will be coordinating multiple prototypes at the same time. As Senior Staff Technical Product Manager, you will:
  • Work with OGTC technical staff and O&G Digital Product Managers to translate the long-term product strategy & roadmap into definitions for prototypes with actionable features – serving as the key junction between the technical research staff and the software development teams
  • Lead the end-to-end development of software and analytic prototypes by leading a fast-paced global research team of software developers working closely with UX, architecture, analytics researchers, data scientists, mechanical, electrical, petroleum and chemical engineers
  • Multitask and manage multiple projects in a dynamic cross-functional applied research organization establishing technical feasibility and driving new product offerings
  • Work with multiple stakeholders (internal & external) to define the overall scope of work and meet the quality metrics needed to monitor the performance of the ongoing work
  • Lead the project team by managing daily activities, resolving issues and insuring team integration across disciplines to deliver projects on-time and on budget
  • Oversee definition, execution, integration and deployment of end user features in conjunction with technical architecture and non-functional requirements
  • Collaborates with technical teams to decompose features into consumable user stories and act as the Agile product owner responsible for managing the spring backlogs, release plans and short-term roadmaps
  • Communicate progress to senior leadership and remove barriers with regards to staffing, resources and program support
  • Transition successful software prototypes to production development teams at GE O&G Digital for commercialization
  • Clearly and professionally display the ability to work with internal clients and SW/design teams, create and maintain global design systems, and further the GE brand through a variety of research oriented efforts
  • Bring a strong working knowledge of a pragmatic Agile process and quality software development methods, in both small-scale and large-scale development efforts.
  • Instill quality and efficiency across research teams, eliminating duplication, and leveraging product and technology reuse.
