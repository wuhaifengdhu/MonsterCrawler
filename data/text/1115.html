As a Senior Director of Data Science with Optum, you will apply your superior analytical ability, creativity, and judgment in analyzing, developing and implementing algorithmic, technical and software solutions to complex requirements. You will contribute significantly to the design, testing, development, and / or support of Optum solutions and / or systems with special emphasis on technological excellence by providing scientific and technical direction, as well as leadership in the future development of technologies supporting Optum solutions.

As a Senior Director of Data Science with Optum, you will influence decisions that affect the overall success of projects and directly impacts financial performance, image, schedules, and customer satisfaction. You will be recognized internally and externally (by the scientific / technical community and by customers) as a technical leader.

Primary Responsibilities:

* Defines, recommends, and implements algorithms, software technologies and software components that will achieve a high level of innovation, competitive advantage and customer satisfaction

* Continuously develops the technological or scientific expertise required to design innovative and competitive software by:

* Performing research activities, including research leading to publications / presentations in PIC* or PIC - targeted journals and / or conferences, or related patents (before publication)(*) PIC : List by OPTUM research of the journals & conferences of excellence

* Contributing to, and staying current with, the academic community

* Leads the development of some product work - packages in a multi - functional professional cross - site context, contributing to project planning & management activities for those work - packages

* Thoroughly understands and effectively executes existing software development processes and procedures and develops new approaches as appropriate

* Ensures accurate and thorough documentation is maintained for the project

* Scope of work encompasses either multiple components, or a highly complex component, of a software product or solution and a specific technological / scientific domain of expertise

* Advises other professionals on complex technology and / or software product issues related to own discipline

* Ensures design and code development incorporates latest technologies and maximizes resources. Makes decisions and recommendations on priorities, design changes, improvements, and problem resolution. Creatively invents and / or improves software development tools, processes and procedures

* Effectively uses teamwork and group dynamics skills to lead design of new algorithms and technologies, and to lead development of software engineering solutions

* Positively contributes to a high - morale / high - performance team culture, leading by example

* Communicates effectively with functional / division management and customers

* Represents Optum technical excellence as an active member of the academic or technical community

* Provides on - going scientific and technical guidance and leadership for software development projects within the team or functional area

* Understands and supports the mission and vision of the function

* Actively participates in defining and deciding upon objectives which support the changing business needs and foster innovative solutions

* Recognized as a technical authority within the functional area, team or department

* Accountable for completion of team or departmental functional area technical objectives results and activities and or projects involving multi - functional teams on or ahead of schedule while achieving or improving cost and quality objectives

* Decisions have a direct and significant effect on project / team objectives, and directly impact customer satisfaction, cost, quality and expense

* Technical leadership and mentoring role requires ability at least to join the core team at least once a year if remote

* Assumes additional responsibilities as assigned

To be successful in this role, you will have experience in the following areas:

* In - depth professional knowledge of software development, system / product design, and integration; typically acquired via significant professional experience. Applies this knowledge to solve complex software engineering problems within the business unit

* Significant active and current scientific / technological knowledge and curriculum (PhD or research positions, publications and qualified academic contributions)

* Has recognition by peers of the scientific contributions he / she can bring to OPTUM technologies

* Analytical ability, creativity and judgment in analyzing, developing and implementing algorithmic, technical and software solutions to complex requirements

* A scientific mindset to move from the problem to the idea, and an engineering mindset to move from the idea to the software artifact

* Ability to work in flexible office conditions; may be exposed to demands of travel, and / or irregular work hours

* Scientific / Academic activity requires attendance at one to two technical conferences (as a speaker, panelist or workshop coordinator)

Be a leader at an industry-leading company. Join us.

Required Qualifications:

* 10 years of leadership experience (including leading and mentoring technical team members, and budget responsibility)

* 5 years of service development (REST, API, Microservices)

* 5 years of experience with the fundamentals and practical application of Machine Learning & Al

* 3 years of experience with one or more of the following Machine Learning and Cognitive technologies: IBM Watson, SAS, Amazon ML, Google Cloud ML, Azure ML Studio

* Thorough understanding of core Machine Learning methodologies: Regression, Classification, Clustering, Matrix Factorization, Predictive Analytics, Natural Language processing, Decision trees, Support Vector Machines, Neural Networks / Deep Learning

* 3 years of experience creating prototypes in R, Python, Scala, Java or similar stack

* 3 years of experience with retrieval and manipulation processes utilizing: SQL, Hive QL, Python, Hadoop, R, Unstructured Data

Preferred Qualifications

* Advanced degree in computer science or related field

* PhD

* Ability to work effectively in a cross - functional team

* Demonstrated initiative and strong ownership of deliverables

* Healthcare experience

* Strong software design and architecture skills with an eye toward avoiding and reducing technical debt

* Experience in multiple collaborations with research, implementing assets into solutions

* Regular presenter at conferences and / or guest lecturer

* Experience conducting professional negotiations
