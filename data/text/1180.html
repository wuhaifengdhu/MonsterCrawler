Reporting to the Head of Customer Analytics, this person will provide advanced analytics leadership for the Enterprise Research & Analytics (ERA) Customer Analytics team and key internal Client Partners. The Advanced Analytics Manager will lead cross-enterprise and cross-business line analytics efforts. They will provide an integrated analytic point of view on work occurring across the Customer Analytics team. They will build strong and effective relationships across ERA and the Enterprise to determine analytics needs, predict trends, and ensure business questions are being answered in the most appropriate manner, in service of acquiring, deepening, and retaining precious U.S. Bank Customer and Client relationships.

In addition, this is a management role in which the Manager will need to feel comfortable "rolling up their sleeves" and leading the analytics effort of their projects. They will feel at ease querying our database and building models, conducting sophisticated quantitative analyses and presenting their findings as clear actionable insights for the business that creates specific and targeted views of the customer, and enables revenue growth.

They will have a strong willingness both to Learn and to Teach, and will have a passion for leading change across a matrix organization and driving Employee Engagement. They will simplify complexity by successfully utilizing data and analytics tools & techniques to enable better, more customer-centered decisions across the Enterprise. They will be expert in advanced analytical tools and statistical techniques to separate "signal from noise" in order to derive insights that will help answer key business questions.

Basic Qualifications

* BA in a quantitative field such as Math, Economics, Engineering, Statistics or related field required. Master's Degree preferred.

* Ten plus years of experience in the field of advanced analytics, data science, predictive modeling, marketing analytics

* Expert user of advanced analytics tools & techniques - SQL or SAS.

* Expert user of analytics data visualization tools such as Tableau

* Expert user of PowerPoint (Tableau Story is a plus)

Preferred Qualifications

* Proven track record of driving Analytics into actionable business outcomes

* Strong Leadership skills

* Exceptional written and oral communication skills

* Demonstrated ability to influence change across a complex organization.
