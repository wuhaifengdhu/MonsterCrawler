Primary Responsibilities:

* Own an end to end process

* Develop functional, market level, and / or site strategy, plans, production and / or organizational priorities

* Identify and resolve technical, operational and organizational problems outside own team

* Product, service or process decisions are most likely to impact entire functions and / or customer accounts

* Identify project needs and opportunities with the marketing team

* Provide work direction for data analysts utilizing SAS, SQL and R

* Manage the process of delivering analytics and insights tied to Medicare and Retirement Marketing division

* Responsible for front end requirements gathering, answering statistical modeling questions, reporting, and providing visualizations and presentations to clients in marketing

* Provide individual contributions in rigorous statistical modeling, fast programming, and reporting

Required Qualifications:

* 4 or more years of experience as a database analyst specifically with large databases

* 4 or more years of experience utilizing SAS

* 4 or more years of data analytics, predictive analytics, and / or data science

* Strong communication skills in connecting with internal customers across the organization

* Demonstrated ability to manage an analytics team of both onshore and offshore professionals

* Ability to prioritize deliverables and disseminate complex technical specifications into visualizations understandable to nontechnical marketing business partners and customers across the organization.

Proficient user skills in MS Access, Excel, PowerPoint, and Word

Proven organizational skills with the ability to be flexible and work with change

* Bachelor degree in Mathematics, Statistics or similar field

Preferred Qualifications:

* Experience working with Oracle and DB2

* Business Intelligence tool implementation and management such as Tableau or Domo

* Experience working with statistics to perform analysis, data modeling, and predictive behavioral modeling

* Healthcare industry experience

* Master's Degree in mathematics, statistics, or other analytical based degrees.
