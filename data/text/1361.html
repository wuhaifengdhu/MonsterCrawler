Sprout Social's marketing team is looking for an experienced conversion optimization professional to join the team. The Sprout Social platform is used by more than 17,000 brands around the world. Companies like Dropbox, Zendesk, Google, and Evernote rely on Sprout to create stronger relationships with their customers through social media. You will drive conversion and increase customer acquisition growth by creating compelling and highly converting web experiences

You will be responsible for managing the creation and optimization of all of Sprout Social's marketing websites. The ideal candidate is someone obsessed with improving customer experiences that can drive an optimization strategy, build effective cross-team collaboration, and manage rapid A/B testing to continuously improve.

Responsibilities

* Develop strategies for improving website conversion rates for acquiring new customers

* Understand needs and barriers to conversion for visitors to Sprout Social web properties

* Analyze web metrics and customer activity to identify the highest impact opportunities

* Prioritize and manage website initiatives from stakeholders across the organization

* Continuously run A/B and multivariate tests to iteratively improve conversion

* Influence and educate a team of digital marketers with cross-functional responsibilities as well as design & development teams dedicated to marketing websites

* Work closely with design, development, data science and product marketing teams

* Collaborate with the marketing team to strategize, plan, and execute campaigns with appropriate metrics to meet goals

Desired Skills & Experience

* 5+ years experience in a marketing or optimization role focused on improving conversions

* Strong analytical abilities and extensive experience with A/B testing

* Proficiency with statistics preferred

* Experience using tools like Google Analytics, CrazyEgg, Hotjar, Maxymiser, VWO or Optimizely

* Ability to present data, findings, and recommendations that are appropriate for senior level management

* Any SEO, SEM, HTML, CSS, Javascript, UX or copywriting skills a plus

* Self-driven, results orientated with a positive outlook, and a clear focus of high quality and business profit

* Excellent organizational skills and ability to manage multiple simultaneous projects and resources

* Strong attention to detail
