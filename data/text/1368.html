Responsible for defining business use cases at enterprise level beyond ERP processes around analytics and implementation of analytics projects from a business technology solutions perspective. Guides the team in the creation of a comprehensive ERP BI technology solution portfolio and implementing best practices to transform the organization to adopt next generation analytics platform. Ability to define BI solution patterns for the purposes of creating reusable assets across the organization. Anticipates IT and industry future directions and relates trends and best practices to future Enterprise applications analytics requirements and projects. Responsible for providing strategic direction on underlying technical/development tools and architecture recommendations to support Enterprise Analytics across the organization. Interfaces across the enterprise, acting as a visionary to proactively assist in defining direction for the use of ERP BI technologies. Capable enough to apply analytical framework of mind to conceptualize new ideas and thoughts and develop technology solutions that empowers business with actionable analytical insights to provide a competitive advantage.

Duties and Responsibilities:

Adhere and contribute to development methodologies, standards, and best practices in analytics.

Develop technical architecture designs which support a robust solution taking into account the user requirements, technical requirements, etc.

Deliver project related documentation during the different phases of the project per requirements

Monitor regulatory guidelines/laws and emerging industry standards to determine impact on enterprise solution architectures and technology strategic direction.

Experience designing and building complex, high performance platforms to support BI analytics on HANA application platform.

Consistently work with offshore and onsite resources to deliver top class BI solutions.

HANA Analytics including HANA Side car /HANA Live solutions with BOBJ suite of tools

HANA Data provisioning using SAP LT Replication Server (SLT),DXC or CDS

Advise management on using the right Applications/tools/approach for next generation analytics platform

Application Optimization for HANA platform

Position Requirements/Qualifications:

Bachelor's degree or equivalent required

Experience with development using SAP BW, CDS, BOBJ, Fiori, HANA tools – 10 years plus

Minimum 3-4 Years' experience with HANA Analytics platform

Experience implementing LSA Architecture, HANA Live and HANA Smart Data Access

Understanding of HANA database architecture

Experience working in Agile and Best Practice methodologies

Industry experience in Pharma or Bio-Medical or validated environment.

Proven ability to effectively comprehend industry requirements and configure/enhance the HANA Platform based solution to support/satisfy the requirement

Demonstrate knowledge of Big Data Architecture, Data Warehousing, Advanced Analytic, or Data Science implementations.

Preferred:

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science/or related field including a business management minor/coursework

Experience in HANA Platforms and SAP BW 7.4 or later

Experience in working with various core SAP modules is highly desirable.

Experience exposing BW queries in Fiori tiles using SAP UI5.
