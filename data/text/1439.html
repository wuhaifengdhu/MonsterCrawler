Abbvie's Advanced Scientific Computing and Informatics (ASCI) group develops novel, innovative data science platforms to accelerate the drug discovery and development process. We are seeking highly skilled, full stack software developers that would like to contribute to the mission of discovering the next generation of novel medicines. The ideal candidates will be intellectually curious and highly collaborative. Exposure to a relevant scientific computing domain such as Bioinformatics or Cheminformatics is helpful, but not required.

* Experience in architecture, design, and development of full stack applications with technologies including but not limited to JavaScript, Angular JS, D3JS, Bootstrap, Python, C#, .NET Framework, Oracle, SQL Server.

* Experience in developing service oriented back-ends, including familiarity with REST, JSON, XML, XSLT, CORS, OAUTH.

* Experience deploying solutions across multiple platforms, including Windows and Linux.

* Comfortable working in a team or alone.

* Strong troubleshooting skills.

* Self-learner.

* Experience with Pipeline Pilot, TIBCO Spotfire, Neo4j, Oracle Application Express, Informatica or other Enterprise Level Data Integration Platforms a plus.
