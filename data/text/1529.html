The Manager, Advanced Analytics Capability Program Management role within the CSCM organization will take accountability for project managing and prioritizing various aspects of the Advanced Analytics capability at Discover. The Manager will interface with business leaders and teams across the organization to provide analytical solutions on large, high value projects, leveraging new analytic and data-driven technologies, processes and techniques. This fast paced position will offer significant exposure and accountability to leaders including Directors and VPs. This role requires strong leadership and communication skills as the Manager will need to partner with functions and lines of business across the company. The individual will participate in strategic discussions to ensure projects are managed correctly and completed on time. This position requires a highly motivated individual with strong results leadership and people leadership

Key Responsibilities

* Partner with stakeholders across the company, including Business Technology, to manage various aspects of Advanced Analytics capability development
* Manage multiple concurrent projects in the planning, initiating, controlling and implementation phases and within defined SLAs
* This position will be responsible for full project lifecycle implementation - translating business needs into technical requirements
* Control a project's scope and mitigate issues while partnering with resources from both the Business and Business Technology to deliver the desired solution
* Lead negotiation with business partners to deliver solutions, evaluating and controlling project scope to deliver maximum project value within reasonable timeframe
* Develop Strategic Capabilities by means of managing technology-based initiatives
* Secure commitments and monitor progress of deliverables from engaged resources
* Manage communication and interactions to ensure timely and efficient project implementation
* Provide PMO analysis for decisions and enhancements to existing processes
* Present findings and recommendations with supporting data to business partners and management
* Lead the project team to define project requirements and identify impacted business and technology teams
* Manage prioritization of projects based on divisional and corporate goals
* Develop team capabilities by exhibiting leadership by example
* Promote a risk-aware culture, ensure efficient and effective risk and compliance management practices by adhering to required standard and processes
Skills
Required Skills

* Bachelor's Degree in a related field
* 5 years' experience in financial services industry, technology, finance, analytics, project management
* Experience working on large technology and/or Data Science projects
* Experience must include excellent analytical and problem solving skills
* Successfully managed projects and the full life-cycle project management
* Strong communication (oral and written), interpersonal, presentation and organizations skills. Must be able to demonstrable the ability to successfully interact with internal and external business partners.
* A high level of initiative and critical thinking to formulate business issues into actionable solutions

Desired Skills

* Experience with deployment and/or production capabilities on a distributed environment (e.g. Hadoop)
* Lean Six Sigma and/or demonstrated process improvement experience a plus
Promote a risk-aware culture; ensure efficient and effective risk and compliance management practices by adhering to required standards and practices.
