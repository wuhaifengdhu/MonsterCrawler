JOB DESCRIPTION:

The Predictive Data Analyst will be an integral member of a new and dynamic corporate Quality team based environment to support and promote the Quality Intelligence function at Abbott.

The Data Analyst will apply a working knowledge of machine-learning, statistics, data mapping, modeling and programming that support Predictive Analytics activities. The Data Analyst will work on a variety of projects that support Abbott’s global businesses while operating within the framework of established policies, procedures and guidelines. The analyst will apply a working knowledge of data mining, statistics Machine Learning and programming skills to understand the external quality and regulatory environment and internal data to support ongoing business needs.

Core Job Responsibilities:

Responsible for compliance with applicable Corporate and Divisional Policies and procedures.

Develop data models, algorithms and programs that support the overall strategy for data analytics/predictive analytics with focus on identification of potential issues before they occur and providing intelligence about industry trends and issues.

Create visualization techniques for output of data models and other analysis

Interact with all operational divisions of Abbott, across multiple levels of the organization, and successfully navigate demands across functions. Provide analytical expertise throughout the organization and support key goals and initiatives.

Comprehend the compliance, quality and regulatory requirements of Abbott's businesses and define pathways to support achievement of objectives.

Partner with analytics functions of other Corporate-level groups (e.g., Public Affairs, Security, Legal, Marketing) to develop an overall strategy and roadmap for analysis of data using predictive analytics.

Develop KPIs to measure predictive analytics outcomes, including improvements and related product and process improvements.

Lead global teams and provide direct business support Improve the effectiveness of the Abbott Quality System, including monitoring, trending and analysis of Abbott compliance performance Act as a change agent for incorporating best practice methods

Supervisory / Management Responsibilities:

Direct reports could include 0-3 individuals

Position: Specialist Good Manufacturing

Specific tasks may include:

·     Providing timely analysis of external and internal quality and regulatory, including the analysis of underlying trends and other time-series concepts for key markets

·         Creating models for predictive analytics utilizing data science techniques

·         Constructing and maintaining various data mapping and algorithms based on changing environment

·         Performing statistical analysis

·        Participating in scenario analysis and simulations of key business drivers

·         Contributing to department reports and communications by providing qualitative and quantitative input

·         Responsible for creating and updating data sets in support of the business decision making process

·         Assist other team members in analyzing data and other ad-hoc projects

Requirements and Desired Skills

A successful candidate possesses either a Master’s or Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science or comparable technical degree.

The candidate should have experience in classification models and preferably be proficient in R and RStudio to complement quality and regulatory skills. Candidates with a degree in a related field will be considered provided there is a demonstrated understanding of Data Analytics, model building and application. Prior work experience in a quality, regulatory or economic/financial forecasting role is preferred.

In addition, a successful candidate should possess the following skills and characteristics

·         A strong interest in global quality and regulatory environments along with the curiosity and desire to develop their personal skill set

·         The ability to critically review and analyze external and internal reports and data, and to summarize key information into clear and concise viewpoints

·         Experience working with data from multiple data sources

·         Experience in open sources tools is required (e.g., Python, Hadoop, Processing, D3, Raphael, R, Ruby, MySQL

·         Knowledge of machine learning classification techniques including decision trees and random forest

·         Strong written skills, including the ability to effectively communicate economic concepts and themes to a general business audience

·         The ability to work independently on individual projects while contributing to the overall team as we collaborate on various reports, models and economic platforms
