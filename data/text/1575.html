Zurich is currently looking for an AVP, Analytic Architecture Manager to work out of our North American head-quarters in Schaumburg, Illinois.

A cornerstone of Zurich's strategy is to create competitive advantage through distinctive risk insights that help our customers better understand and protect themselves from risk and help us make better risk selection and pricing decisions. Zurich's Predictive Analytics team tackles fresh and exciting challenges that require innovative techniques, tools and data. The Analytic Architecture team is a core part of moving insights to impact.

The Analytic Architecture Manager will be responsible for the Zurich North America Research Data Lake. The candidate will:

* Partner with ZNA IT to manage and enhance the Data Lake

* Keep up to date with development in new analytic tools and techniques and define the roadmap for ongoing enhancements

* Work closely with Predictive Analytics project teams to apply new analytic tools and techniques

* Drive ongoing training on new analytic tools and techniques

* Enhance critical capabilities to accelerate the deployment of analytic solutions

* Build core capabilities to improve access to data in close collaboration with the Data Architecture team

* Manage a team of data scientists and architects, providing appropriate training, coaching, and development. Responsible for performance and talent management

Basic Qualifications:

* Bachelor's Degree and 8 or more years of experience in the Business In Insurance, Operations Training and/or Change Management area

OR

* High School Diploma or Equivalent and 10 or more years of experience in the Business In Insurance, Operations Training and/or Change Management area

AND

* 3 or more years managing a team of people

* Enterprise-wide project management experience

Preferred Qualifications:

* Master's degree in computer science, statistics, applied math or other related quantitative degree program

* Strong analytical thinking and problem solving skills

* 3 or more years of people development experience

* Solid project management and organizational skills to work on multiple priorities, establish and meet deadlines as well as manage the productivity vendors and project teams

* Ability to establish systems level architecture of the solutions, define subsystems and interfaces, and allocate development resources.

* Experience structuring large complex data sets to support automated retraining of analytics

* Experience with Hadoop/Spark ecosystem and cloud implementations

Imagine working for a company that truly cares about their employees, customers, stakeholders, and communities they serve.

Imagine working for a values-driven organization that has the ambition and desire to be the best global insurance provider in the world.

Zurich is that place where 55,000 employees across 200 countries and territories are all focused on helping people and helping companies protect what is truly most important to them. We are a values-driven organization that takes pride in the work that we do every day and we have the ambition to be the best global insurer in the world.
