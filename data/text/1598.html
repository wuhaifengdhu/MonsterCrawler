The Project Manager, Advanced Analytics Capability Program Management role within the CSCM organization will be responsible for project managing various aspects of the Advanced Analytics capability at Discover. The Project Manager will interface and partner with functions and lines of business across the organization to provide analytical solutions on large, high value projects, leveraging new analytic and data-driven technologies, processes and techniques. This fast paced position will offer significant exposure to leaders including Directors and VPs. The individual will be responsible for ensuring projects are managed correctly and completed on time. This position requires a highly motivated individual with strong leadership and communication skills.

Key Responsibilities

* Partner with teams across the company, including Business Technology, to manage various aspects of Advanced Analytics capability development
* Lead multiple concurrent projects in the planning and implementation phases and within defined SLAs
* This position will be responsible for full project lifecycle implementation - helping to translate business needs into technical requirements
* Collaborate with resources from both the Business and Business Technology to deliver desired solutions. Evaluate and control project scope to deliver maximum project value within reasonable timeframe
* Monitor progress of deliverables from engaged resources
* Provide PMO analysis for decisions and enhancements to existing processes
* Present findings and recommendations with supporting data to business partners and management
* Lead the project team to define project requirements and identify impacted business and technology teams
* Promote a risk-aware culture, ensure efficient and effective risk and compliance management practices by adhering to required standards and processes
Skills
Required Skills

* Bachelor's Degree in a related field
* 3 years' experience in financial services industry, technology, finance, analytics, project management
* Experience working on large technology and/or Data Science projects and the full life-cycle project management
* Experience must include excellent analytical and problem solving skills
* Strong communication (oral and written), interpersonal, presentation and organizations skills. Must be able to demonstrable the ability to successfully interact with internal and external business partners.
* A high level of initiative and critical thinking to formulate business issues into actionable solutions

Desired Skills

* Experience with deployment and/or production capabilities on a distributed environment (e.g. Hadoop)
* Lean Six Sigma and/or process improvement experience a plus
Promote a risk-aware culture; ensure efficient and effective risk and compliance management practices by adhering to required standards and practices.
