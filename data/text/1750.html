As VP of Sales you will be responsible for acquiring and retaining customers for our data science products and services. You will be charged with identifying and evaluating potential growth opportunities, building and driving relationships with key internal and external stakeholders, standing up and building out the software sales team, territory planning, providing market research and analysis, and helping shape the go-to-market strategy for our products and services in a perpetually evolving environment.

Your responsibilities will cut across all departments at Civis Analytics. You must be able to work well with others who approach problems from different perspectives, but you will be expected to challenge and improve the way we operate as we mature. This role will report to the CEO and is based in our Chicago headquarters.

We value creativity, hard work and on-the-job-excellence and offer competitive compensation and benefits packages. In compliance with federal law, all persons hired will be required to verify their identity and eligibility to work in the United States.

Minimum Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in business, finance,marketing, computer science, or a related subject, or equivalent work experience
  • 7-10+ years of sales experience, of which 3+ are CEO-level relationship sales for a technology company
  • Experience leading 5-10 employees or other large sales teams
  • Proven track record of leading and driving successful sales plans and meeting or exceeding targets
  • A robust knowledge of the software industry, including demonstrated market research and analysis
  • 5+ years of relationship and account management experience
  • Outstanding communication skills (written,verbal, and presentation)
  • Willingness to travel and work longer hours and some weekends as necessary
  • A sense of humor

Preferred Qualifications

  • MBA or advanced degree
  • SaaS sales experience
  • Strong familiarity with analytics
