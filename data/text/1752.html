Digital Marketing Analytics Manager: Sears & Kmart Formats

Manager in Digital Marketing & Analytics is responsible for contributing to optimization of digital marketing budget in collaboration with Digital Marketing Execution team for both Sears and Kmart formats.
Manager will work with Data Science team towards development of efficient algorithms with clear line of sight into the activation and operational parameters of digital media buying landscape. Manager will translate the business needs as articulated by media buyers into actionable recommendations for digital channels.
Manager will also participate in company-wide initiatives to find efficient media spend allocations across the entire marketing spectrum, including but not limited to non-digital channels.



Responsibilities:
- Participates in the development and implementation of a turnkey digital marketing optimization platform across Sears and Kmart formats
- Works with Digital Marketing Execution team leads in various channels, e.g. Affiliates, Paid Search, Data Feeds, Social, to identify opportunities for marketing efficiency
- Partners with Web Analytics to develop modules to enhance operational efficiency of digital media buyers
- Develops working knowledge of in-house Customer Lifetime Value Estimation tool, and contributes to feature enhancement and integration with digital marketing targeting capabilities
- Contributes to Data Science initiatives through statistical and machine learning algorithm design and coding
- Consults on strategy towards productionizing Data Science solutions
- Builds and manages relationships with external vendors to ensure deliverables meet organization's needs
- Acts as a liaison between Data Science and Marketing Execution leads to deliver insights for in-market activation
- Collaborates with Data Science team in tracking and optimization of digital campaigns across the entire digital marketing spectrum
- Uses available system and manpower resources to optimize the process and performance of digital marketing
- Willingness and ability to learn Big Data Tools and programming languages, e.g. BigQuery, SQL, R, Python
- Reports to Divisional Vice President of Digital Marketing & Analytics within Online Business Unit

Skills/Experience Requirements
- Statistical and/or Machine Learning Algorithm Design and implementation
- Experience in partnering with external stakeholders towards large-scale implementation of solutions
- Understanding of Big Data tools and platforms to deliver automated solutions to drive marketing efficiency
- Ability to craft algorithms for efficient analysis of individual clickstream data from digital log files
- Capability to translate insights from marketing optimization platform to actionable recommendations for media buyers for both digital and non-digital channels
- Concise design of project scope and managing deadlines
- Expert knowledge of at least one statistical programming tool between R/SAS/Python, and working knowledge of SQL/BigQuery


Background/Requirements

  • Minimum Master's degree in a quantitative field such Statistics, Economics, Computer Science
  • Minimum 5 years of experience in statistical modeling and application of machine learning
  • Working knowledge of SAS, R, Python, SQL and expertise in at least one scripting language
  • Strong communication skills
  • Experience with analysis of large scale data on the order of millions
  • Track record of building scalable strategies and executing strategies with passion, precision, and excellence
  • Creative thinker who can generate innovative ideas and solutions and partner effectively with marketing teams
  • Strong project management and organizational abilities
  • Interpersonal and relationship building skills, experience in dealing with multiple and diverse internal stakeholders, and interacting with business and technology partners
