The Information Management Analyst at Health Care Service Corporation (HCSC) is responsible for the development of an information architecture strategy that provides improved capabilities for a specific business portfolio or enhances major portions of the existing information processing and delivery environment. The Information Management Analyst applies his/her technical and non-technical skills in developing, communicating, and leading an information architecture vision for their respective portfolio.

Operating within the Enterprise Architecture Services team, the Information Management Analyst will translate the client's business requirements into specific systems, applications or process designs, integrate their architecture within a new or existing application or data platforms, and act as an advocate for the client's business objectives.

JOB REQUIREMENTS:

* Bachelor Degree in MIS, Computer Science, Mathematics, Information Services, or Data Science.

* Technical data experience, including combinations of: data modeling/analysis, ETL development, and reporting/analytics delivery.

* Experience with a relational database platform, preferably designed and constructed for different consumption patterns.

* Familiarity with NoSQL data platforms.

* Familiarity with Hadoop data platforms, such as an understanding of the Big Data architecture, and minimally, usage of Apache Hive.

* An ability to communicate conceptual, logical, and physical technology.

* Strong communications skills, with the ability to present your ideas to both technical and non-technical audiences in an organized manner.

* Strong business acumen, strategic and analytical thinking, root cause analysis, and creative problem solving ability.

PREFERRED JOB REQUIREMENTS:

* Healthcare industry expertise
