Be part of the team that powers social good!

Corporate IT and infrastructure is an exciting group focused on keeping Blackbaud's internal systems and website up and running effectively. Together, we build out data centers, protect our data, stay up-to-date on the latest cloud technologies all while ensuring Blackbaud's employees have the tools and equipment they need to do their jobs.

We are seeking exceptional people that are passionate about giving back and impacting nonprofits, foundations, corporations, educational institutions, and the individual change agents who support them. See an opportunity that fits your background? We want to hear from you, apply today.

About Blackbaud:

Blackbaud is the world's leading cloud software company powering social good. Our mission is to provide software, services, expertise and data intelligence that empowers and connects people to advance the social good movement. Focused on fundraising, financial management, CRM, marketing and more. We work with over 35,000 organizations, helping them realize their goals, fund their missions, manage their operations, and develop long-lasting supporter relationships. Our customers are passionate about making the world a better place, and we're inspired by the opportunity to help them.
