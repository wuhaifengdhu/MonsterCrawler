About Our Team

Customer success is our #1 priority at Salesforce. We are looking for an energetic, creative, and execution focused individual to take our customer journeys to the next level! What is a customer journey, you ask? Our 'journeys' act as a virtual success manager, providing prescriptive, guided paths to help customers get started, achieve business outcomes, or solve challenges. Journeys include multiple engagement channels to get customers the resources and guidance they need to be successful.

The Customer Success Journeys team is looking to add an experienced data insights analyst to our growing organization. Our team is focused on creating automated and scalable customer success programs that help increase engagement and adoption of the Salesforce product.

The data insights analyst will play a key role in enabling our internal stakeholders to make data driven decisions based on insights. You will work with the business to understand requirements, create reports and dashboards to assemble the data, analyze the data and provide recommendations based on the results.

Key Responsibilities:

* Work with internal stakeholders to understand their program goals, processes and KPIs through detailed data analysis and partnership with SMEs.

* Document requirements and translate business problems into questions that can be understood by data engineers and data scientists.

* Think through complex problems and make actionable recommendations based on the data.

* Analyze large amounts of data and provide key business insights across multiple data sources (Excel, Oracle, Hive, etc.).

* Present complex and diverse data sets in the form of a story to business stakeholders.

* Continuously derive insights by analyzing data and understanding business processes.

* Identify key data points that the business should be evaluating on an ongoing business.

* Develop ad hoc reporting/data insights to address immediate business needs and present findings in a business centric fashion.

* Create and automate repeatable processes to report out regularly on KPIs

* Apply critical communication skills to ensure your analyses and insights are understood and appropriately utilized.

* Create business process flows for various manual data loads and automate wherever possible

* Create data visualization dashboards to enable self service data insights

Experience/Skills Required:

* 2-4+ years experience as a Business Analyst or Consultant in a mid to large sized organization with demonstrated leadership driving projects in a matrix organization.

* Consultative approach, including ability to translate customer needs into low level technical requirements and process flows for consumption by the technical team.

* Ability to "tell a story" with the data by honing in on specific and actionable insights.

* Present back actionable recommendations to business problems in a clear and concise manner.

* Analytic thinker that is willing to make decisions cross functionally in a fast paced, dynamic environment.

* Exceptional attention to detail to deliver correct conclusions and solid recommendations.

* Strong interpersonal skills with demonstrated ability to present technical content to general audiences.

* Solid organizational skills, attention to detail, and ability to prioritize a varied workload with multiple stakeholders.

* Demonstrate strong problem solving skills, process-driven mindset, and resourcefulness.

* Advanced Excel skills.

* Working knowledge of SQL: Oracle, MySQL, SQL Server, Redshift, etc.

* Working knowledge with one or more business intelligence or visualization tools such as Tableau, Qlik, Business Objects is a plus.

* Experience working with Scrum teams and Agile Methodology is a plus.

* Experience with the Salesforce.com platform and related cloud or web-based technologies is a plus.
