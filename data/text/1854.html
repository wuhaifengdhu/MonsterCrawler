Raytheon Applied Signal Technology is a diversified intelligence and defense company that develops systems to provide integrated sensor and signal processing products in support of intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance.

In this position you will work on complex technical problems and provide solutions. Application of advanced technical principles, theories and concepts will be necessary while contributing to the development of new principles and concepts and ensuring proper setup, configuration, and functionality of systems being deployed.

The candidate will work with other staff in our various customer offices for the protection of our country. The successful candidate will be able to generate briefings and present material clearly to team members, customers, and Raytheon Applied Signal Technology management as necessary.

Required Skills

* A minimum of 8 years systems engineering experience with SIGINT systems

* Experience in five (5) of the specializations within the last 8 years

oSignal Analysis

oModern Tele-communications System and Networks

oTelephony Switching System

oCommunications Systems

oCellular/Personal Communications Systems

oTDM/PCM Multiplexing / Modulation /Switching Systems

oPacket Switching System

oSystems Engineering

oOperating Systems

oAdvanced Protocols

* A minimum of 7 years development and integration experience in SIGINT systems

* Minimum of 5 years experience in at least four (4) of the Engineering Subsystem Specializations

oSwitching and Survey

oCircuit Processing

oPacket Processing

oDistribution

oFiltering & Selection

oMonitoring

oAnalytic Tools

* Minimum of 5 years experience with the functionality and inter-processing of packet- and circuit-based HW & SW solutions

* Minimum of 8 years experience in performing and providing technical direction for the development, engineering, interfacing, integration, and testing of complex hardware/software systems. This experience should also include definition of requirements and allocation of functions, conduction of systems requirements reviews, development of Concept of Operations (CONOPs) and interfacing standards, definition of architecture, technical/non-technical assessments, and end-to-end flow analysis

* Minimum of 7 years experience in a leadership position providing technical supervision to other engineering personnel; solving varied technical/scientific problems; and generating and reviewing designs for technical accuracy, completeness and appropriateness in light of objectives, budget, and schedule.

* US Citizen

* Existing TS/SCI with Full Scope Poly

Required Education:

* 8 Years, with a B.S./B.A. in Engineering, Science, or Mathematics or 6 years with MS/MA in Engineering, Science or Mathematics or 4 years with PhD in Engineering, Science or Mathematic

U.S. Citizenship status is required as this position needs an active U.S. Security Clearance as of day one of employment.

This position and any offer associated with it are contingent on clearance acceptance from the customer. Failure to obtain customer acceptance will cause the offer to be rescinded.
