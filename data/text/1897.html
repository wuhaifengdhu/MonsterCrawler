Northrop Grumman Mission Systems Sector (NGMS) is looking top talent to join our Mission Assurance Data Analytics, Tools, and Metrics team as an Information Governance Lead 5 at our Baltimore, MD location. Work location flexible, full-time telecommuting option available.

This position is responsible for leading Information Governance for NGMS and requires a solution-based orientation, unwavering ethics and values, and the demonstrated ability to be a trusted partner. A successful candidates must demonstrate a proven track record of solving complex problems and developing a variety of solutions.

Additional responsibilities include:

Leads the development of Information Governance strategy and key initiatives.

Effectively collaborates with ES & CSO on Information Governance strategy development.

Identifies various data sources across the sector.

Implements policies for access and use of those data sources and develops a system for sector-wide data sources availability.

Develops an organization of data and system owners.

Employs relevant COTS products

MAQEA

Basic Qualifications:

Bachelors Degree in a related STEM field.

14 years of related Data Science experience with Bachelors Degree, 12 years with Masters Degree, and 9 years with PhD, including 7 years of Information Governance experience.

Demonstrated success implementing Information Governance.

Experience developing and deploying information systems.

Broad understanding of business processes.

Knowledge of NGMS functional organizations.

Strong communication skills.

Experience with Information Governance Tools

Demonstrated experience with enabling and executing change in large organizations

Preferred Qualifications:

Masters or Ph.D. in STEM field

5 years of Data Analytics work

Ability to obtain a TS/SSBI Clearance, or an active TS/SSBI Clearance.
