Description


CSRA seeks an software developer to join a team supporting the NASA Center for Climate Simulation (NCCS) (http://nccs.nasa.gov). The NCCS supports high-end computational modeling of Earth science, space science, and exploration providing a key resource in the effort to increase understanding of Earth's climate system, natural and human influences on climate and consequences for life on Earth, and to advance space science and scientific exploration beyond Earth.

The NCCS is dedicated to providing scientists and engineers with high-end computing and data resources, simulation tools, and analytics specifically designed to carry out critical NASA missions and to make new scientific discoveries with a focus on the support of climate and data intensive science.

Essential Job Functions

  • Maintain and update the NCCS website in Drupal
  • Update content as required
  • Support the migration of an existing website currently hosted in WordPress to Drupal
  • Update internal web portals for staff use
  • Maintain Daily Status Report dashboard
  • Collaborate with the web developers at the NASA Advanced Supercomputing Division at NASA Ames
  • Support web based user surveys
  • Support promotion of development features to production through the use of version control (Git)
  • Designs, develops, troubleshoots, debugs, configures and maintains website(s) for internal and external communications for the company and/or for external customers and clients.
  • Ensures website(s) is available to the desired audience with appropriate links and security.
  • Develops, assesses and communicates website usage and security policies and procedures.
  • Designs web page layout, graphics, color schemes and infrastructure to maintain a cohesive website based on the organization's communications strategies and goals.
  • Researches and evaluates new related technologies.

Qualifications

  • BS or equivalent + 0 yrs experience
  • Experience with Drupal, including Content Types, Blocks, Modules, and Views
  • Experience with PHP, SQL, Javascript, HTML, CSS and Perl
  • Experience with mysql RDBMS, Git
  • Familiarity with Wordpress
  • Basic Linux skills required, preferably Debian and Centos 6/7, with administrative background a plus
  • Familiarity with Apache on a Linux system; understands basics of configuring Apache
  • Familiarity with Java, Objective-C, and Python a plus
