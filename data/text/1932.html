Sr. Manager, Advanced Analytics

Develop and lead team of statistical analytic and data science experts to support multiple retail and wholesale business functions. Manage statistical modeling and data mining projects to enable key initiatives in the areas of promotions, targeted marketing, customer segmentation, financial and demand forecasting, assortment, program measurement, and test designs.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities include but are not limited to the following:

* Develop and maintain strategic partnerships across multiple business units and digital marketing groups

* Serve as Sr. Analytics consultant bringing innovative thought leadership and providing methodological guidance to internal clients as well as external consultants to solve complex and multifaceted business challenges

* Coach, train and mentor and manage internal and external analysts and data scientists

* Partner with functional business leads to create and deliver insightful, meaningful and actionable analyses to drive measurable results

* Work with functional analytics leads to define business problems, develop appropriate methodologies and analytic frameworks, define data requirements, perform statistical analyses, interpret results and produce actionable business insights that lead to measurable business and consumer experience performance improvements

* Responsible for managing project resources, timelines, budget and quality of deliverables

* Develop, own and maintain production analytics processes, standards and best practices

* Serve as subject matter expert on internal and external data and be a strong advocate of data quality, quality and master data standards and provide training and consultation to functional business unit partners

Technical Attributes of Ideal Candidate: include but are not limited to the following:

* 8+ years of experience developing and leading consumer engagement and/or marketing analytics teams, preferably in a retail or CPG company

* Strong statistical background with hands-on and leadership experience in many of the following techniques: generalized linear modeling techniques (e.g., linear regression, logistic regression, etc.), time series analysis, mixed modeling, clustering, factor analysis, decision tree (e.g., CHAID and CART), survival analysis, design of experiments

* Proven skills and experience using a variety of statistical analytics tools (SAS, R, SPSS, Knime, Matlab, etc.)

* Proven skills and experience using a variety of data management and data visualization techniques and tools (SQL, SAP Analytics, Tableau, Business Objects, Alteryx, etc.)

* Proven skills and experience performing data discovery and statistical analysis in a Big Data environment using a variety tools and techniques (HDFS, Map Reduce, Hive, Python, Spark, etc.) – Experience in SAP Hana environment preferred

* Attention to detail and time management to deliver high quality work for multiple projects while meeting deadlines. Proven ability to learn and expand in a fast-paced environment

* Demonstrated ability to translate complicated statistical modeling concepts in an easy-to-understand manner in order to effectively communicate the value and benefits of the statistical findings to business users and senior executives

* Excellent written and oral presentation skills

* Demonstrated ability to foster strong client relationships

* Experience managing cross functional teams with a primary role supporting multiple teams of analysts

* Demonstrated ability to foster ideas and growth among a team of analysts

* Advanced degree in quantitative field (Mathematics, Statistics, Economics, Finance, Engineering)

Behavioral Attributes of Ideal Candidate: include but are not limited to the following:

* Team Player. Loves being part of a team, both leading and serving; demonstrated deep commitment to colleagues

* Humble & Hungry. Confidence without arrogance; driven to continually "up their game" regardless of prior successes

* Unparalleled Integrity. In good times and bad, lives the UA Way; takes ownership for mistakes; delivers tough messages directly; doesn't "back-channel"

* Action Oriented. "Can do" and "does do" attitude; authentically engaged with people and situations

* Accountable. Commits to stretch goals and delivers; holds self to highest performance standards

* "Gets" Under Armour. Passionately embraces the challenge and effort to keep UA unique; committed to go the extra mile to build/protect the brand
