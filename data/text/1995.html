**Qualifications**

**JOBSUMMARY**

Provides day-to-day pricing and statistical modeling expertise. Supports programming and analysis for RevenueManagement and Sales Systems. Participates in the development of new and creative business solutionsto pricing, revenue management, reservations and sales challenges. Participates at the organizational level todeliver business solutions and within the team on analytical orresearch-oriented activities to serve our business in an innovative andimpactful manner.

**CANDIDATE PROFILE**

**Education and Experience**

**Required:**

+ Graduate degree in Operations Research or related field(Management Science, Economics, Statistics, Mathematics, or Engineering)
+ 1-2 years experience in industrial or academic research;applying analytical and programming skills against complex mathematicalmodeling and/or systems development
+ 3 years experience in software development

**Preferred:**

+ PhD Operations Research or related field (noted in required)
+ Understands complex, discrete data sets with subtle underlyingbiases
+ Experience with of price elasticity models, pricing response models,market segmentation, forecasting, optimization, simulations analyses, and othermethodologies.
+ SAS, R programming, including SAS/HPF, SAS/ETS and macros. Knowledge and experience with SASadministration for multiple users.
+ Proficiency with Linux environment.
+ Familiarity with optimization software (CPLEX, Gurobi), Python,Java, C/C
+ Good communications skills and ability to work within a team.
+ Knowledge of relational databases and object-orienteddevelopment environments.
