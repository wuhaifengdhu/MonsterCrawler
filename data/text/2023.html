**Qualifications**

JOB SUMMARY

The Vice President of Customer Experience (CX) Analytics serves as the leader of the CX Analytics discipline reporting to the Chief Customer Experience Officer.CX Analytics is charged with developing and utilizing state-of-the-art analytic approaches to generate business intelligence about customer attitudes and behaviors across all customer-facing touch points along the B2C and B2B customer journeys.

**CANDIDATE PROFILE**

EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE PREFERRED

+ Bachelor s degree required; advanced degree in data science, mathematics, statistics, or related discipline preferred.
+ Twelve or more years of work experience in Sales & Marketing Analytics, or a related discipline, demonstrating progressive career growth and a pattern of exceptional performance.
+ Experience working within large, distributed customer and marketing-led organizations.
+ Experience leading teams with advanced modeling skills (regression, neural networks, machine learning, social/text analysis, etc.).
+ Diverse and up-to-date experience using data science methodologies and technologies to generate practical business solutions.
+ Solid track record of business leadership

KEY SKILLS

+ Ability to influence change and develop strong interpersonal relationships with team members, and key stakeholders
+ Proven ability to lead cross functional teams in support of specific business priorities
+ Skill and experience distilling a set of data and translating findings into innovative, profitable, executable, and operationally feasible solutions
+ Experience leading projects to deliver against major initiatives.
+ Experience managing projects through a highly matrixed and relationship-based organization
+ Team player that works well with counterparts from various functions/departments and has the ability to influence the work of counterparts without direct reporting accountability
+ Strong ability to manage multiple stakeholders
+ Must possess strong knowledge and practice of core principals in brand strategy, conceptual design, execution
+ Exceptional skill in problem solving and leveraging resources to optimize capabilities

CORE WORK ACTIVITIES

Operational Analytics-Marketing and Media

+ Provide analytic support to the Global Marketing and Global Brand Management organization to help evaluate the performance and effectiveness of marketing programs and investments, and provide business intelligence to inform strategic direction (e.g., Marketing Media, B2B Marketing Spend, Social Media, and Campaign application and impact, ongoing analytic engine for digital marketing Orchestration targeting program)Operational Analytics-Digital, Distribution, and Sales
+ Provide analytic support to the Global Sales, Distribution and Digital organizations to help evaluate effectiveness of digital services, distribution strategy programs, and B2B sales programs; and, provide business intelligence to inform strategic direction (e.g., Marriott.com, Voice, OTA, B2B Sales, Digital Services Initiatives)Platform and Special Project Analytics
+ Provide advanced analytic support and predictive/propensity models for complex multi-channel programs, platform evaluations, and strategic planning
+ Develop and manage KPIs and analytics to monitor and manage the health of the full customer ecosystem
+ Lead analytic support for Omni Channel CRM
+ Serve as expert COE on complex analytics projects supporting broader BMSC and Continents as needed
+ Manage Decision Engine that drives personalization across all customer touch points

**Collaborate with key leaders of areas critical to the development transformative customer experiences**

+ Establish a network of internal partners to gain cross-functional leadership perspectives.
+ Establish a trusting relationship with Brand, Marketing, Digital, IT, Operations, and Global Design.
+ Partner closely with CXI leadership team (Consumer Insight, CRM and Data Strategy, Analytics, and Customer Experience Strategy) to ensure the ongoing effectiveness of Marriott s customer experience innovation engine.

**Steward excellence in talent management and organizational capability**

+ Hire, retain, and continually develop diverse, high caliber talent that makes a strong, positive impact on the organization.
+ Setting and maintaining high standards for team and individual performance.
+ Develop effective organization structures and operational processes
+ Providing timely coaching and feedback.
+ Making and rewarding distinctions in performance.
