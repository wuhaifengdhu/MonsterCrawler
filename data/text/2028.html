**JOB SUMMARY**

The purpose of the Customer Data Strategy (CDS)team is to support the enterprise data strategy and be the bridge between ITand the business, building the data and platforms to support reporting,analytics, CRM, and personalized marketing. The CDS team is responsible for integrating customer data, includingprofile, transactions, interactions, third party, survey, and analyticallyderived data into a robust customer profile that will support our CRMefforts. The Senior Manager will be akeep player in defining this customer profile and determining how to integratecustomer data to support strategic programs. He or she will also lead the analytics supporting our personalizedmarketing programs, managing and contributing to the process to used advancedanalytics to predict customer behavior and target marketing communications andcustomer treatments across channels. Thesenior manager will become an expert in existing data sources and will takepart in the development of new data about our customers. Finally, the senior manager will leadprojects to develop analytically derived models of customer behavior and deploythem in production systems to contribute to the customer profile, managingexternal consultants and collaborating with different departments at Marriott.
