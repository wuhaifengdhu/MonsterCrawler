SATCOM Systems Engineer, Mid

Key Role:

Join a team of satellite communications, systems engineers, and subject matter experts from other fields, including cost analysis, data science, DoD acquisition, and contracts, supporting acquisition, engineering, analysis, sustainment, and management of communication related systems within the DoD. Collaborate with colleagues to promote synchronization and synergies across multiple programs, including commercial satellite communications (SATCOM), SATCOM gateway sustainment, and DoD Teleport. Assist senior colleagues and clients by contributing thought leadership and subject matter expertise towards holistically addressing DoD interests and issues. Participate in authoring a range of engineering and related deliverables, including high level architectures, concepts of operations, requirements development and decomposition, implementation guidance, program management plans, acquisition support documents, analysis and recommendation documents, and other assigned products. Support technical coordination across multiple government groups. Interact frequently with peers in presenting one's own progress and contributing feedback on teammates' draft work products. This position is located in Annapolis Junction, MD.

Basic Qualifications:

-2+ years of experience with systems engineering gained on IT, networking, or communications systems projects and programs

-Experience with SATCOM practices

-Secret clearance

-BS degree

Additional Qualifications:

-3+ years of experience with the design or integration of satellite communications architectures, systems, and networks, including physical and data link concepts, such as satellite beam coverage areas, link budgets, modulation, forward error correction, or multiple access

-Experience with DoD systems acquisition process and documents

-Experience with contemporary DoD or commercial satellite or terrestrial communications systems

-Possession of excellent oral and written communication skills

-BS degree in Engineering, CS, Mathematics, or a related field preferred; MA or MS degree a plus

Clearance:

Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information; Secret clearance is required.
