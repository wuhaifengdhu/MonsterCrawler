Data and Analytics are increasingly important to the performance and success of most companies. At Micron, we are using data and analytics to make better and faster decisions, and to create competitive advantage. As a member of the IT Enterprise Analytics & Data (EAD) team at Micron, you will work in one of the most exciting and innovative fields, making a difference every day.

As an Agile Coach in the IT EAD team, you will apply your extensive knowledge of Agile to help teams employ the latest technologies and methods for putting data to work in the areas of business intelligence, data management, data science, big data, advanced analytics, and more. You will have an opportunity to grow your career in this expanding field.

The Agile coach position is responsible for helping teams develop their Agile skills in order to rapidly become self‐sufficient and benefit from Agile principles in their team practices. More specifically, you will support teams/projects by participating with the team in an immersive and hand-holding approach to implement or improve the application of Scrum/Scrumban throughout projects, and facilitates the adoption of best practices.

Responsibilities:

* Acting in Lead and/or Supporting coaching roles

* Providing training and mentoring for teams, programs, and department

* Working with the Agile Center of Excellence and IT Leadership

* Helping with Agile transformation activities as possible and required

* Working on an Agile Coaching team using Agile practices, building coaching plans for teams, and assisting in implementing tools, artifacts, and documentation based on various industry standard practices

* Act in a self-directed way, while also working effectively as part of a coaching team and COE to help ensure adoption goals and metrics

* Help develop the coaching skills of other Agile professionals in the organization

Required Experience:

* 10+ years working in the IT industry

* 6-8+ years of Agile experience in any role

* 4-6+ years of experience as a Scrum Master

* 2+ years working as a Team, Program, or Enterprise Coach

Additional Desirable Knowledge and Experience:

* Leading and detailed participation in Agile transformation activities

* Deep experience with Scrum, with additional experience in Scrumban (preferred) or Kanban

* Agile in the data warehousing and analytics domain

* Leading and implementing XP engineering practices

* Experience with JIRA and Confluence

* Experience with Agile ALM tools (e.g., VersionOne, Rally)

* Working with the software development lifecycle from a people, process, and tools perspective in an Enterprise setting

* Large enterprise experience is desirable, although small organization experience is great too

* Experience implementing DevOps is strongly preferred, including CI, TDD, Automation, working with deployment pipelines, working with both Delivery and Ops teams and understand what models to use with each

* Excellent articulation of ideas as well as presentation and verbal communication skills

* Ability to research and explore new ideas and convert them into useful outcomes

* Ability to work independently and to be accurate, efficient, and organized

Education and Certification:

* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent required

* Master's Degree desirable

* Certified Scrum Master or Certified Scrum Professional is desirable

* Other IT Certifications in process or tools desirable
