We are looking for a Chief Scientist with a strong background in organic chemistry and NIR spectroscopy, and knowledge of intelligent predictive computational models.

Tasks

  • Lead a project, fully hands-on, to develop tools for the creation of algorithms to predict nutritional food information including food quality, safety, and ripeness
  • Lead a project to improve accuracy and efficiency of the existing algorithms used for intelligent computational predictive food analysis
  • Publish scientific discoveries and validation reports in scientific peer-reviewed journals
  • Engage in outside scientific presentations to promote work developed
  • Help file patents and liaison with patent lawyer
  • Lead or co-lead the following: algorithm team, food testing team, food scanning team, data validation team
  • Exchange ideas and strategies with Scientific Advisors, Head of R&D, Head of IT, and CEO
  • Help set up food lab in the Arizona offices
  • Help hire the team members

Desired Skills & Experience

  • PhD in computational chemistry, bioinformatics, computational biology, or a related discipline
  • Expert on near infrared (NIR) spectroscopy and its applications
  • Strong background in chemometrics
  • Knowledge of organic chemistry
  • Experience with data science, probability theory, computational mathematics, NIR chemometrics, food testing, and validation methods
  • Strong knowledge of machine learning and its application in food science and chemistry
  • Proficiency in data analytics
  • Proficiency and experience with the Linux environment
  • Experience with food testing procedures in a large commercial food laboratory is a plus
  • Understanding of Food Science is a plus

Personality

  • A critical thinker who likes solving challenging research problems and has a can-do attitude
  • Excellent interpersonal skills, i.e., an effective team player
  • Excellent communication skills, i.e., capable of communicating complex ideas efficiently
  • Comfortable working in a highly dynamic fast paced work environment
