Looking for an environment that encourages collaboration, making an impact and where you'll contribute fresh ideas? Drive your engineering future with us. At ViaSat you'll work with top technical talent who are always striving to stay ahead of the curve. ViaSat is a leader in providing practical solutions for difficult communications problems. ViaSat continues to grow at a record pace, and a key to our growth and stability has been our flexibility in serving both commercial and military markets. Join ViaSat's team and play a key role in continuing our growth and leadership in the communications field.

Our culture empowers us to do things that other companies would find very difficult, if not impossible. It is uniquely ViaSat and, intentionally, not like any other company. As a new graduate we understand that seeking your dreams is your ambition….and achieving them is ours.

In this role you will be responsible for developing cutting edge data visualization solutions for internal users. In this role you will be given the flexibility to select and use new and innovative solutions and tools for improving the overall efficiency of the engineering and manufacturing test teams. You will be responsible for planning, defining, developing, testing, and deploying cutting edge data visualization tools. We need more effective solutions for automating testing, storing and recovering large amounts of test data, plotting and complex analysis of test data, as well as many more challenging tasks. You will be given the opportunity to not only determine where and how we can improve our overall efficiency through better software tools and solutions, but develop and deploy the solutions.

Requirements

* Bachelors Degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering or related discipline

* Desire to work in an engineering support role

* US Government position. US Citizenship Required

* Ability to travel up to 10%

Preferences

* Software tool development desire

* Data science courses

* Exposure to Python, Jupyter, Pandas, JQuery, HTML, UI Design, C#, SQL, Jira, GitHub

* Experience with automating test equipment a plus

* Understanding of RF testing a plus

Our Advanced Microwave Products group is located in Tempe, Arizona, also known as the "high tech oasis of the silicon desert". Tempe is a lively, up-beat town located southeast of Phoenix and is in close proximity to the world-famous upscale town of Scottsdale. Arizona is also a lovely winter haven and is the golfing and resort Mecca of the Southwest. With a solid work force and an ever-expanding family-based community, The Phoenix metro area has arts, entertainment, all major league sports, and a growing electronics industry that continues to flourish.

Are you ready to take the next step in your Engineering career where you will thrive and enjoy coming to work? Then submit your resume. It only takes a few minutes and could start you on your new path to a fulfilling career at ViaSat. We look forward to hearing from you.
