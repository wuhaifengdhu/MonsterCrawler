Analytic Data Scientist
Creates solutions for the business by recommending ways to bring data to life to solve business problems.
We are a shared service for the entire company and operate in several key areas:
Business Analytics:
-Using advanced modeling and simulation techniques to optimize the performance of our business units.
-Forecasting, Attribution modeling, Text mining, Spatial analytics, and other advanced techniques are applied to solve real-world business problems.

Marketing Analytics:
-Using advanced modeling to understand consumer behavior to both enhance customer experiences as well as improve our marketing program execution and design.
-Utilize formalized program evaluation methodology to determine marketing program effectiveness. Conduct customer level modeling to customize communication and marketing offers.
Skills Required:
- Strong collaboration and communications skills
- Strong problem formulation and problem solving skills
- Candidates should have experience with tools in each of these areas (not necessarily all software packages, but some from each group: - Data gathering skills/tools (e.g. SQL, Hadoop, Teradata)
- Modeling skills/tools (e.g. R, SAS, Matlab, etc.)
Skills Preferred:
- Strong understanding of analysis and computational complexity and ability to programmatically solve problems
- Strong capabilities on Test Design and Measurement
Experience Required:
- Strong academic background with graduate training in Statistics, Economics/Econometrics, Operations Research, or related fields.
Experience Preferred:
-Survival Analysis applied to consumer behavior, or warranty data
Education Required:
- Masters degree from accredited university
Education Preferred:
- PhD/ABD preferred with a strong background in data sciences
