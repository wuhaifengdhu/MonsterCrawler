About the Company

Based in Utah, Idaho, and Arizona, Progrexion leads the credit repair industry with groundbreaking technologies and services that help consumers access and understand information contained in their credit reports, verify whether that information is fair, accurate and substantiated, and correct inaccuracies with individual creditors, other data furnishers and the national credit bureaus.Â  Progrexion technology and services are used by Lexington Law, an independently-operated law firm, and CreditRepair.com, its wholly-owned subsidiary.

Description

The Customer Experience group at Progrexion is looking for a summer intern to help collect, clean, organize and analyze data related to employee performance and retention. In this role, they will work with a team of senior data scientists who will take responsibility for advising and supporting their work.

The ideal candidate will have some experience in analysis and data management with priority given to those with proficiency in SQL, R, Python or a combination of the three. This position will require the ability to set and maintain short-term priorities and deadlines.

Responsibilities

  • Collect, clean, and organize internal data related to employee performance and retention.
  • Use statistics to characterize data and identify outliers.
  • Conduct preliminary analyses regarding drivers of performance.
  • Set and communicate project-specific priorities and deadlines.

Requirements

  • Active interest in data analysis and management, with experience a plus
  • Curiosity and a willingness to learn (including from mistakes)
  • Attention to detail
  • Responsible and reliable; able to set and meet deadlines.
  • Fluent in English

About Progrexion

Progrexion is a technology enabled consumer services business that leads the way in credit repair.
