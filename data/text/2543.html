About the Agency

JOB DESCRIPTION: GEOINT Analysts (Data Science) develop and apply methods to identify, collect, process, and analyze large volumes of data to build and enhance GEOINT products, processes, and systems. They conduct data mining and retrieval, and apply statistical and mathematical analyses to identify trends, solve analytical problems, optimize performance, and gather intelligence. They visualize information using a range of tools (e.g., GIS, R), develop scripts and algorithms, create explanatory and predictive models, and conduct comparative analyses to address complex military and intelligence problems.

Duties

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION: The candidate will leverage contemporary data analytic software and approaches to discover, prepare, manipulate and normalize large unstructured data sets. The candidate should have the ability to use mathematical concepts and techniques to scientifically estimate probabilities complex analysis issues; understand concepts associated with structured data and relational databases; understand the relationship between the data collected for a real-world problem and the required structure of a relational databases to help solve that problem; have the ability to conceptualize the relationship between a database structure and the requirements for analyzing and visualizing those data both temporally and spatially.

The candidate should understand how to write scripts for modeling processes, for knowledge/source capture, and conduct hypothesis testing. The candidate should understand how to use available tools, such as Arc GIS, Excel, Tableau, SPSS, R, Python, or other programming and statistical packages to analyze and visualize data. The candidate should be able to visualize data both temporally and spatially to assist in data integrity checks, ask the next question, and display analytical assessments from diverse customer sets. The candidate should understand the data enough to know which tools to use and how to use them creatively; and communicate technical data to non-technical individuals.
