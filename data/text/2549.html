Description


As a member of Information Security’s Cyber Intelligence & Analysis team, you will be part of a team of analysts dedicated to increasing situational awareness of the operating environment, analyzing threats against it and determining potential impacts for the purpose of influencing decision-making and driving behavior change to reduce liability and risk to the business. These insights allow for the identification and prioritization risk and add immediate, demonstrable value to the business.
This role is as exciting as it is challenging. The primary goal of our Data Scientist is to leverage data to support cyber intelligence throughout the strategic, operational and tactical spaces to identify current and emerging threats that when coupled with risk assessments provides increased fidelity into business risk. This goal necessitates an understanding of data science and familiarity with information security functional areas.
You should have a background or knowledge of data analysis, visualization, and interpretation. A foundation in computer science, modeling, and statistics is desired. An understanding of data management, systems analysis, and system life-cycle phases are recommended. Knowledge business intelligence, data mining, inventory systems, log management systems, and social networks is desirable.

Responsibilities:
  • Provide expertise in data analytics and algorithm development supporting the integration and analysis of diverse data sources.
  • Develop machine learning, data mining and statistical algorithms for pattern recognition and anomaly detection.
  • Develop statistical and predictive analytic models for large, diverse data sets.
  • Improve upon current methods for the automated processing and exploitation of large data sets.
  • Design, develop and advance new methods to extract information from diverse data sources using flexible querying methods, innovative visualization, and data aggregation/integration, data mining and analytical techniques.



Boeing is the world's largest aerospace company and leading manufacturer of commercial airplanes and defense, space and security systems. We are engineers and technicians. Skilled scientists and thinkers. Bold innovators and dreamers. Join us, and you can build something better for yourself, for our customers and for the world.


Qualifications:


Basic Qualifications:

  • 3 or more years of experience designing, developing and driving reporting and data analytics solutions

  • 3 or more years of experience with data analytic technologies (NoSQL, GraphDB, Neo4j)
  • 1 or more years of experience with Machine learning and data formats (JSON, XML, etc)
  • 1 or more years of experience with applied data sciences techniques, including but not limited to, machine learning approaches, data visualization, and statistical modeling.
  • 1 or more years of experience in a hands on role performing advanced predictive analytics using tools like Python, R, or SAS.
  • Experience writing SQL queries to obtain data from multiple source systems.
  • 1 or more years of experience using data mining methods, such as clustering and anomaly detection, to understand data patterns and select appropriate predictive techniques.
  • Willing and able to obtain a DoD Secret Security Clearance
  • 1 or more years of experience working with international information technology and information security standards (e.g. ISO/IEC 2700X, NIST SP800-53, ISO/IEC 15408, ISO/IEC 20000, CobiT)


Preferred Qualifications:
  • Experience with network graph analysis, CAPEC, ATT&CK, or Kill Chain analysis
  • Experience with relational databases is preferred.
  • Experience with US government information assurance and C&A standards (e.g. DIACAP, NIACAP, NIST SP800-37, FIPS, DoDI 8500.2) is desired
  • Experience with Hadoop and/or Linux
  • Experience with any AmazonWeb Services


Typical Education/Experience:
  • Technical bachelor's degree and typically 5 or more years' related work experience or a Master's degree or an equivalent combination of education and experience. A technical degree is defined as any four year degree, or greater, in a mathematic, scientific or information technology field of study.


Security Clearance:
  • Current and active DoD Secret Security Clearance preferred

    This position requires an active (or ability to obtain) U.S. Security Clearance, for which the US Government requires US Citizenship.
