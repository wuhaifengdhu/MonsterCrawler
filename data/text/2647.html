Description:

Duties and Responsibilities:

The Senior Recruiting Manager will report to the VP of Talent Acquisition and will be responsible for the execution and delivery of recruiting services for the National and Cyber Solutions (NCS) Business Group. The NCS Business Group is comprised of over 1,300 engineering, data science, cyber security and signals intelligence experts. The talented NCS staff contribute to every step of the intelligence lifecycle and leverage an agile development methodology that provides rapid prototyping, quick reaction, operational support and solutions to a diverse customer base. The NCS Business Group supports some of our nation's most pressing mission objectives.

Position responsibilities:

The successful candidate will be responsible for developing and implementing recruiting strategies and delivering services appropriate for the Business Group's business model. Will manage and develop a team of recruiting professionals, providing leadership, planning, and project coordination, ensuring the effective and efficient operation of the recruiting function is in place. Concurrently, ensures the recruiting function is aligned and integrated with the overall CACI recruiting strategies. The selected candidate will also contribute to enterprise wide recruiting strategies and activities, including the implementation of company-wide recruitment programs.

Additional responsibilities will include: ensuring the BG's hiring practices are in compliance with government laws and CACI policies and procedures; partnering with the BG HR Director to ensure the achievement of recruiting goals and human resources objectives; meeting regularly with BG leadership to understand the goals and objectives of the BG; coordinating and collaborating with recruiting colleagues to enterprise-wide recruiting programs; and reviewing and analyzing recruiting metrics to assess effectiveness of recruiting efforts, making adjustments to recruiting levels as needed. The selected candidate will also oversee and support the diversity goals and objectives of the enterprise.

Required EDUCATION & EXPERIENCE:

Bachelor's degree and minimum of 12 years of related experience in staffing and/or human resources to include 5+ years of full-life cycle recruiting experience. Requires 3+ years of experience in a leadership/supervisory role, serving as a full-life cycle recruiter and leading recruiting initiatives may substitute for formal supervisory experience.

* Experience supporting federal government contracts, in-depth knowledge and experience supporting various contract types to include, Firm Fixed Price, Time & Materials, Cost Plus) as well as non-IDIQ and IDIQ single or multiple award contracts.

* Experience recruiting and/or managing recruiters that support hiring in the fully cleared markets is required, requires extensive knowledge of the DoD and Intel markets.

* Experience reviewing and analyzing recruiting metrics to assess effectiveness of recruiting efforts and assist in managing recruiters.

* Experience supporting an organization that delivers leading edge solutions in the areas of Cyber Intelligence Solutions, Digital Signal Processing, and Mission Application Development is a plus.

* Computer proficiency in standard Microsoft Office applications is required, as is proficiency with one or more automated recruiting systems.

Desired Experience:

* Well versed in recruiting marketing trends

* Experience conducting executive level searches

* Familiar with Taleo and Workday

* Active security clearance is desired

Company Description:

CACI employs a diverse range of talent to create an environment that fuels innovation and fosters continuous improvement and success. Join CACI, where you will have the opportunity to make an immediate impact by providing information solutions and services in support of national security missions and government transformation for Intelligence, Defense, and Federal Civilian customers. A Fortune magazine World's Most Admired Company in the IT Services industry, CACI is a member of the Fortune 1000 Largest Companies, the Russell 2000 Index, and the S&P SmallCap600 Index. CACI provides dynamic careers for over 20,000 employees worldwide.
