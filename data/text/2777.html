Job Duties and Responsibilities ManTech International Corporation will be hiring experienced and skilled Research Analyst to provide Business Intelligence Support Services to assist the U.S. Government Customs Border Patrol (CBP) officers and border enforcement personnel.

The chosen candidate will look to effectively and efficiently help to provide analysis in identifying cargo, individuals, conveyances that may present additional risk to the United States.  They will conduct terrorism analysis and global assessments that convey changes in terrorism threats and identify emerging threats, develop and evaluate CBP-wide intelligence-based targeting rules and intelligence driven special operations, and coordinate and enhance analysis and target efforts.

As Research Analyst, the candidate for this position will provide intelligence analysis support as part of an integrated agile team that will be responsible for using, developing, maintaining, and enhance models, tools, and software for:

•    Intelligence Analysis-Counter-terrorism-Network Analysis
•    Predictive Modeling
•    Threat Research Support
•    Visualization
•    Query Support


POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES:
  • Deliver analysis for the identification and analysis of transnational criminal organizations, their future actions, and their methods.
  • Search and analyze intelligence data, synthesize intelligence information into a relationship or threat model data, and infer the confidence level and completeness of the information.
  • Research additional sources such as trade, travel, criminal activity, and money movement to isolate actors, activities, and networks that represent threats to the US security or legal trade and travel.
  • Provide analytical support for the development of analytics models to identify networks, predict future threats, and isolate and evaluate threat scenarios.
  • Work in data science teams that include Intel analysts, data and visualization experts, and software developers.
  • Construct of dossiers for specific threats.
  • Prepare documentation validating reasoning, certainty, and completeness of synthesized information.

QUALIFICATIONS:
  • From HS & 7-years to a Master's degree and 1-year of experience.
  • Demonstrated expertise in understanding large, complex transaction data, with the ability to synthesize information from large amounts of data.
  • Experience with Intelligence Analysis.
  • Experience in community and threat Identification.
  • Experience presenting work to both technical and non-technical audiences.
  • Experience evaluating uncertainty in relationship data.
  • Experience working in law enforcement or classified intelligence analysis environment.


Minimum Clearance Level: U.S. Government DOD - Top Secret security clearance AND/OR DHS - CBP.
