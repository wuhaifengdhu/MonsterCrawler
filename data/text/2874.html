Description:

CACI is seeking a candidate with strong problem-solving skills, excellent communication skills, an ability to work under pressure on key business and highly technical dynamic environments within the Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency (DARPA)!

As a DevOps Engineer, you will be responsible for driving the continuous process improvement, and collaborate effectively with cross-functional operational and development teams to solve problems and implement new solutions. Specifically, the candidate will help define and guide the platform initiatives to maintain consistency across deployment models in areas such as operational excellence, launch readiness, and the adoption of best practices. You will perform system and software installs, as well as, provide support and maintenance for systems utilized by a team of software developers and data scientists. You will be responsible for creating contingency plans and responding to service outages for system malfunctions. You must be willing to troubleshoot, resolve computer hardware and software problems, and support a multi-contractor team environment.

Required Qualifications:

* 4+ years hands-on Linux System Administration Installing and configuring software, patches, and upgrades

* Troubleshooting issues, working with developers and other team members

* Knowledge of shell scripting for troubleshooting and automation of routine tasks

* Overall understanding of network topologies, network concepts, and hardware

* Must be able to provide communication, both written and verbal to team members, project partners, and customers

* Communication will take the form of documentation, incident reports, project statuses, etc

* Typically requires bachelor's degree or equivalent and five to seven years of related work experience

Desired Qualifications:

* Experience working in a dynamic research environment

* Experience using Atlassian products (JIRA, Confluence)

* Capability to configure and troubleshoot network equipment, and identify and resolve hardware/software/network malfunctions Puppet Shell scripting System Vulnerability Scanning tools Nagios

Clearance Requirement: This position requires the ability to obtain/maintain a Top Secret/SCI U.S. government security clearance.

Company Description:

CACI employs a diverse range of talent to create an environment that fuels innovation and fosters continuous improvement and success. Join CACI, where you will have the opportunity to make an immediate impact by providing information solutions and services in support of national security missions and government transformation for Intelligence, Defense, and Federal Civilian clients. A member of the Fortune 1000 Largest Companies and the Russell 2000 Index, CACI provides dynamic careers for approximately 15,000 employees working in over 120 offices worldwide.
