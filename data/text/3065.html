Interesting?? Scroll down for more

Not an exhaustive list of responsibilities, but an overview:

  • You need to support the development and implementation of customer management and customer reporting tools for executive management decisions.
  • You will be generating ad hoc customer analysis and research.
  • Make recommendations for improving existing processes, products, and practices.
  • Manage and monitor customer behaviors, outcomes, feedback and relationships of these things to business performance and customer lifecycle/profitability.
If this has piqued your interest, please check if you have the following:
  • Experience manipulating data warehouses as a resource for analytical work and Excel Reporting.
  • 2 years of analytical experience; preferably in marketing analysis, advanced analytics, or consulting.
  • Bachelor’s degree with quantitative major such as math, applied math, or data science.
  • You must be proficient in MS Office Suite.
