Capital One

It's audacious work at a massive scale and, hey, we think it's pretty rewarding.

Small Business Banking

We believe Small Businesses are the heart and soul of what drives our communities and economy forward with their passion and dedication to their work and customers. Beyond our customers, they are our family, friends and neighbors. We're hell-bent on reinventing the way Small Businesses successfully manage their money, engage with their bank, and build meaningful financial lives. It's audacious work at a massive scale.

Our group is relatively new to Capital One so we don't shy away from challenging the status quo of process, policy, and the sacred cows inherent in a financial institution. Our inspiration is our small business customer — their ingenuity, scrappiness and relentless optimism for the future.

The Role

Capital One's Small Business Bank is launching a digital first Spark branded platform that will provide a one stop shop of banking and business management services to small and medium-sized businesses. The product is built with the end customer in mind, including simple pricing, integration with third party complementary services, and customer friendly policies.

The digital segment team is focused on driving the overall strategic vision and business intent for growing Capital One's Small Business customer base, specifically within online and mobile digital channels. The team is responsible for developing product, pricing, and servicing strategies designed to grow the business while optimizing the customer experience.

Specifically, the Director's responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

* Analytical leadership: Lead comprehensive data analysis (e.g., product pricing, P&L forecasts, competitor benchmarks, market outlooks, etc.) to build a solid fact base for strategic decisions

* Customer focus: Drive customer management campaigns (e.g., cross-sell and relationship deepening) and push the customer experience agenda

* Strategic leadership: Develop business strategies that will drive growth, profitability, and competitive success for Capital One in the face of shifting customer and regulatory behavior

* Team leadership: Manage cross-functional teams. Act as a coach and mentor to associates with a goal of developing and retaining talent at Capital One

* Partnership: Work closely with colleagues across Capital One, including: IT, Sales, Operations, Finance and others

Basic Qualifications

* Bachelor's degree in any quantitative field (business, math, economics, finance, statistics, science, engineering) or military experience

* 4+ years experience in Business Management

* Superior analytic skills; strong understanding of advanced statistical techniques

* Excellent communications, both written and verbal. Ability to explain complex issues and convey assessments in concise, simple, and crisp terms

* Strong teamwork, relationship building, and influencing skills

* Solid people leadership experience: Ability to build and leverage the capabilities of a high-performing team, as well as business partners across the enterprise

Preferred Qualifications

* Masters/Doctorate Degree in any quantitative field (business, math, economics, finance, statistics, science, engineering)

* Experience working with product teams to design/build financial products

* Experience in the financial services industry, specifically Small Business banking and payments

Location: McLean, VA (preferred); San Francisco, CA
