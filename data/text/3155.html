Senior Hadoop Engineer

Marketo provides the leading engagement marketing software and solutions designed to help marketers develop long-term relationships with customers their – from acquisition to advocacy. Marketo is built for marketers, by marketers and is setting the innovation agenda for marketing technology.

The Opportunity

Every day, there are hundreds of millions of prospective customers being processed by our product and this number is growing rapidly. Marketo captures and acts on billions of marketing events a day which feeds into multi-petabyte Hadoop clusters.

Marketo is a fast-paced environment that focus on delivering customer value which maintaining a high bar of operational excellence. If you think you are up for the challenge and can raise the bar, then take the leap and join the Marketo team!

We are looking for talented and motivated engineers to help build the next generation marketing automation platform to power Marketo.

Your role:

* Build upon our new data infrastructure to enable more engineers and data scientist to quickly build new applications.

* Build libraries and tools to improve the production readiness of our near realtime Spark streaming, Spark ETL, HBase and Druid infrastructure. You will use cutting edge technologies to scale our clusters to thousand jobs and millions of queries a day.

* You will be working with a team of stellar talent to build out the next wave of innovation for our marketing automation and analytics offerings.

Responsibilities include:

* Work on a Hadoop environment where you will work closely with production operations and systems engineering to design and implement tools to leverage our multi-terabyte data lake.

* Create tools and automation frameworks to enhance production stability, availability, efficiency and reliability of Marketo's data infrastructure.

* Design and participate in the development and delivery of operability-related features such as system health monitoring, diagnostics, repair, and other self-healing automation.

* Influence and create new designs, architectures, SDKs tool for large-scale distributed data processing.

* Engage in service capacity planning and demand forecasting, software performance analysis and system tuning.

Desired Skills and Experience

* BS or MS in Computer Science or related field and/or related experience.

* 5 years of experience on Hadoop infrastructure stack (Ex: HDFS, MapReduce, HBase, Spark, Oozie, YARN, Zookeeper, etc).

* Good programming skills in one or more of Python, Go, C++ or Java/Scala

* Demonstrable expertise designing and implementing system health and performance monitoring tools

* We are open to new college graduates with relevant internship experience.

Preferred Qualifications

* Good foundation in distributed systems concepts.

* Last but not least: Believe that iterating quickly, while building for long term is the only path forward.
