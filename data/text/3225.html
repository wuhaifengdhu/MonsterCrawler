SUMMARY

Voya is looking for an inspiring, intelligent, and data-driven leader to join Voya and head up our Data Engineering organization. The Director Data Engineering will be a part of the leadership team responsible to envision and build overall growth strategy for data architecture, engineering, and analytics for the organization. Responsible for creating and leading world class Data Engineering team responsible for building the next generation of our data platform and products.

PRINCIPAL RESPONSIBILITIES

Direct responsibility for managing our Application DBA team, Data Tools, Big Data and Data Innovation team. Passionate about big data and data-driven engineering, naturally curious about any problem where data can be applied and analyzed..

* Build a culture of learning and innovation with the ability to learn new data technologies / practices and spearheading adoption within the organization

* Build a vision for data infrastructure at Voya to help the business insights, product analytics, data science and all business functions to use data to drive decisions and build new products

* Bring a passion to stay on top of technology trends, experiment with and learn new technologies, participate in technology communities and act as an internal mentor to users of these platforms

* Collaborate with executive team and cross functional leaders to explore and leverage new data technologies to build products and improve our operations

* Facilitate the establishment and execution of the Strategic roadmap and vision for information delivery and management; including the data warehouse, big data, BI & analytics, content management and data management

* Architect target architecture and strategy for key business initiatives.

* Apply business strategy while driving technology strategy, balancing short term and long term needs to ensure that the Data engineering architecture can scale and evolve accordingly

* Experience leading transformation from traditional to next-gen data-analytic technology solutions

* Provide technical leadership in design, implementation and support of the core Data Engineering services that are highly reliable, flexible, scalable and cost effective

* Develop, support, and communicate core data engineering policies, standards, guidelines and procedures

* Establish proper monitoring, measuring and reporting of service performance against Service Level Objectives, customer satisfaction and financial efficiencies

* Ensures delivery success with strong focus on customer service and partnership development

* Manage all levels of support for implementations and operations

* Improves, optimizes, and identifies opportunities for improved development and delivery

* Supports continuous process improvement and innovation

* Manage risks, assessing gaps/opportunities, and implementing action plans to mitigate risks, when applicable

* Effectively communicate project status to management and other stake-holders as appropriate

* Hire and grow the data engineering organization

* Lead data engineers to architect and deliver the solutions that fulfill the Business information needs and align with the information vision and strategy

* Manage, develop, coach, motivate, energize, and inspire members of the Data Engineering Team.

* Provide advice, guidance, direction, and authorization to carry out major plans and procedures to ensure schedule attainment, process adherence, and performance and budget targets are met

* Build trusted relationships between Data Engineering Team and all other Voya IT teams.

* Promote a culture of collaboration and teamwork across organizational boundaries.

* Manage relationships with key third partners (e.g., Cloudera, Oracle, Tableau).

* Work with technology vendors to ensure that all platforms and software are working at optimal levels and explore the use of new features to increase its value.

EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE

* Bachelor's degree and/or Master's degree in Computer Science

* 10+ years of industry experience of increasing responsibility running engineering teams

* Deep knowledge on building with, integrating, and operating big data or Database technologies

* Significant experience building scale-out data platforms and services for the full data lifecycle

* Prior leadership experience managing a number of development teams concurrently as well as multiple, competing priorities simultaneously

* Multidisciplinary experience overseeing software development, program/project management and QA

* Experience architecting and operating large scale systems

* Candidates must think and innovate at scale

* Experience with two or more vendors in any of the following areas including; SQL Server, Oracle , Hadoop, SAS, Tableau, SAP Business Objects, and other Analytics Vendors with BI components

* 5+ years managing organizations larger than 20+ people while still remaining in touch with the critical details

* 5+ years directly managing organizations of engineers

* 5+ years interacting regularly and directly with senior executives

* Superior communication and analytical skills, including strong ability to identify and solve ambiguous problems

* Proven ability to lead cross-functional teams through influence versus direct management

* A history of strong relationships building both internally and externally.

Critical Skills

At Voya, we have identified the following critical skills which are key to success in our culture:

* Customer Focused: Passionate drive to delight our customers and offer unique solutions that deliver on their expectations.

* Critical Thinking: Thoughtful process of analyzing data and problem solving data to reach a well-reasoned solution.

* Team Mentality: Partnering effectively to drive our culture and execute on our common goals.

* Business Acumen: Appreciation and understanding of the financial services industry in order to make sound business decisions.

* Learning Agility: Openness to new ways of thinking and acquiring new skills to retain a competitive advantage.
