Req ID: 40301BR POSITION SUMMARY The Data and Digital Project Manager will be responsible for supporting all aspects of a digital or data governance project or program, from planning and development through implementation. Projects are expected to range from design and implementation of governance processes and controls to managing review and governance of discrete data-dependent and digital initiatives. This position will drive results on a highly collaborative basis, supporting the digital, consumer, and privacy legal teams and coordinating with a variety of functional areas, including the Data Sciences organization. Fundamental Components: Managing all aspects of a project or program, such as planning, coordination, development, and implementation, including prioritizing work, resources, and time Engaging in a highly collaborative process, including managing relationships across multiple functional areas Ensuring that the end state of the project and/or program meets law/compliance objectives and that all deliverables and due dates are met in support of digital and data governance objectives Supporting relevant functional areas by recommending appropriate actions, strategies, and/or alternatives to meet business needs Developing innovative solutions for governing digital and data initiatives that simplify and improve upon quality and delivery of support services to the Consumer Health & Services organization, Data Sciences, and other businesses, functional areas, and governance stakeholders Maintaining and developing an effective documentation system, including tracking progress and results of project reviews and developing accessible tools to streamline and standardize governance processes Maintaining or developing a working knowledge of digital and privacy compliance requirements and risk issues BACKGROUND/EXPERIENCE desired: 5-7 years Project Management experience desired Experience with legal and compliance issues preferred, particularly related to privacy& HIPAA Proven project management skills, including independently driving results and working collaboratively across functions Highly organized, ability to develop, maintain, and readily access tracking and documentation systems Experience Visio, Sharepoint EDUCATION The highest level of education desired for candidates in this position is a Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience.
