Join our team to work in a dynamic business environment with friendly and supportive colleagues. You'll help develop and implement forecasting models and tools used to manage multi-billion-dollar sales and maintenance contracts. You'll be supporting and enabling the decision-making process driving these business deals. And you'll be challenged to perform your best in this fast-paced atmosphere.

You will work as a forecasting model manager in the Pratt & Whitney Engine Services organization as part of the Development Analytics group. The Development Analytics group provides forecasting models and other analytical tools to support the sales and maintenance activities of the Commercial Engines & Aftermarket business, encompassing a significant portion of Pratt & Whitney's $14 billion in total revenues in 2015.

We will teach you how to use and develop models in our state-of-the-art Monte Carlo simulation system running on a high-performance computing platform. It would be helpful, but not required, if you already possess a basic familiarity with statistical modeling. To develop thorough models, you will learn to account for the impact of contractual obligations, maintenance practices, and technical performance of the engine in order to optimize value for both P&W and the customer. In addition to model development, you will be involved in the many of Development Analytic's studies and projects to further our understanding of the commercial aftermarket business. These studies often require working across PW organizations (Engineering, Programs, Finance, Sales). Most importantly, we're looking for your ability and willingness to learn, perform and drive projects to completion.

This position is an excellent opportunity if you are a technically-focused person looking to broaden your experience with more business exposure.
