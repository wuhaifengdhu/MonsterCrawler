Data Science Product Manager

Primary Responsibilities & Objectives

* Identify the available data, including internal and external data sources, and leverage new data collection processes

* Apply statistical and predictive modeling techniques to build, maintain, and improve on decision systems

* Lead the discovery process with internal stakeholders and customers to identify business requirements and translate them into product requirements

* Define and prioritizes the roadmap and product backlog

* Gather data analysis requirements across different Ellie Mae products

* Coordinate with the development teams to deliver enhancements and bug fixes

* Work with legal, sales, customer service and customers to understand issues enhancement requests

* Maintain up-to-date knowledge regarding mortgage industry and technology trends as well as the Ellie Mae product line and competitor products

* Provide assistance to support and sales teams in order to assist customers with detailed product questions, acting as top tier for complicated support escalations

* Responsible for product delivery, adoption and growth

* Evangelize product area among customers, prospects, and colleagues

Qualifications, Skills and Education

* 3-5 years of relevant quantitative and qualitative research and analytics experience.

* At least 2 years' experience working on Analytics and Big Data products as a product manager

* 8-10 years' experience working in the tech industry

* Solid knowledge of statistical techniques.

* Proficiency in statistical analysis, quantitative analytics, forecasting/predictive analytics, multivariate testing, and optimization algorithms.

* Must have experience developing detailed Analytics product requirements

* Ability to analyze and clearly communicate business requirements as a key input to the product development process

* Detail-oriented and able to manage multiple projects simultaneously

* Experience working with cross-functional teams, including product management, legal, development, quality assurance, support, marketing and sales

* Self-motivated as well as a strong team player

* Must have excellent written and verbal communication skills

* Bachelor degree in mathematics, statistics or computer science; Master degree preferred
