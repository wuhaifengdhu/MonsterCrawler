Data Scientist

Unisys - Carlsbad, CA 92009

Posted: 3/18/2017

Position Overview (overview will vary depending on level):

Data scientist for medium sized ($10M USD or 15 project team members) or large projects ($20M USD or 25 project team members) including complex, performance-intensive, enterprise-class solutions. May participate in smaller projects in an oversight or design capacity. Ensures that the proposed solution meets the client's requirements, is architecturally complete, and all architectural risks are quantified for the project. Leads client engagements with in-depth portfolio knowledge and implementation experience. Ensures that the solution aligns with and utilizes the portfolio offerings that result in profitable revenue growth. Acts as a primary technical interface to the client and a leadership role developing business opportunities and ensuring positive financial outcomes for engagements and business development activities. Assumes a leadership position within the architect community and effectively utilizes the Unisys collaboration infrastructure to improve solution quality and project team effectiveness. Effectively promotes the use of Unisys standard processes and methodologies.


Responsibilities: (responsibilities vary depending on level):

  • Identifies opportunities for new or follow-on business and assists in creating change orders in the analytics and application areas
  • Leads advisory services, strategic planning activities and business case development within the assigned Unisys portfolio areas. Mentors junior data architects on advisory services delivery.
  • Leads strategic consulting presentations to clients including features, implementation approach, technical requirements, impacts, and benefits.
  • Assumes a leadership position on and uses an understanding of Unisys Portfolio Resources for new business opportunities.
  • Performs responsibilities including Portfolio solution proposal management, solution design, solution review, risk analysis, proposal preparation and client presentations.
  • Provides technical oversight for technical estimates created with standards tools, portfolio Work Breakdown Structures, statements of work and industry standard estimating techniques.
    Analyzes and evaluates client requirements against portfolio offerings and performs build vs. buy analyses.
  • Architects a complete solution using appropriate portfolio offerings with design elements that allow integration into client environment. Directs the activities of junior data architects assigned to business development solution design.
  • Refines and optimizes data architectural models and reviews the architectural artifacts produced by junior architects. Creates architectural prototypes as required.
  • Coordinates data science resources on all sized projects to develop the solution architecture.
    Maintains knowledge of data science technologies, industry trends, standards and design techniques.

  • Guiding the full life-cycle of Big Data and Advanced Analytics solutions, including:
  • Maintaining a current knowledge of the Big Data Analytics technology industry and Federal IT marketplace and determining the impact of emerging policies and technologies
  • Developing use case scenarios to support/enable utilization of Big Data Analytics solutions
  • Performing vendor landscape and technology assessments (Hadoop, NoSQL, distributed RDBMS, In-memory databases, BI / Reporting, analytical

  • Rapid prototyping to demonstrate value of Big Data use cases
  • Designing and architecting Big Data and Analytics solutions to deliver highly scalable, high-performing, distributed applications
  • Supporting business development activities to articulate Unisys’s Big Data Analytics capabilities and help shape and communicate proposed solutions to client executives and architect
