Job Description

About the role

ARM designs the technology that sits at the heart of the connected world, from smartphones and tablets, to servers and infrastructure and into the internet of things.  Information is ARM's second most valuable asset, after its people. The data science team is part of the rapidly growing Information Management group, which ensures this information gathered, stored and used wisely. We are dedicated to developing cutting edge data-driven applications, aiding data-driven decision-making and fostering a data science community.

We have recently expanded the team from Cambridge UK, to the Bay Area and are looking for talented data scientists to help us shape the future of data science at ARM. If you are curious, excited about turning data into insights and keen to drive the business forward, we want to hear from you.

Your primary focus will be on consulting with business stakeholders, performing statistical analysis and building high quality machine learning systems. This is a global role, working with people around the world on both engineering and business projects. You will work closely with teams in IoT, business strategy, marketing, high performance computing and research.

What will I be accountable for?

  • Working with the data science team and business stakeholders to understand complex business problems and invent, prototype and deploy solutions

  • Validating and tracking performance of deployed models

  • Designing, analyzing, and interpreting the results of experiments

  • Processing, cleaning, and verifying the integrity of data used for analysis

  • Driving the collection of new data and the refinement of existing data sources

  • Sharing your statistics and machine learning expertise with the ARM data science community

Job Requirements

What skills, experience, and qualifications do I need?

  • Degree in a quantitative discipline (mathematics, statistics, computer science, physics, economics, life sciences, etc.) or equivalent experience

  • Ability to rapidly prototype and deploy data science solutions in Python

  • Ability to gather and manipulate both small and large datasets using SQL and NoSQL queries

  • Applied statistics skills, such as hypothesis testing, regression, classification and clustering

  • Excellent understanding of machine learning techniques and algorithms. A few of our favourites are Gaussian processes, Bayesian hierarchical models, random forests and LSTMs

  • The ability to communicate clearly with business and engineering teams with a focus on driving impact

  • Experience with (or willingness to learn) common data science toolkits such as pandas, scikit-learn, TensorFlow, NLTK, networkx, H2O, Spark

  • Experience with (or willingness to learn) data visualisation tools and web technologies, such as Tableau, D3.js, etc.

Nice to have

  • Ability to develop and deploy web-apps (Flask, Django, Javascript, Docker, etc)

  • Good understanding of software development workflow, such as Git usage, unit testing, software design and architecture principles

  • Experience with any of the following:

    • natural language processing

    • Bayesian statistics

    • multi-objective optimisation

    • reinforcement learning

What are the desired behaviours for this role?

At ARM, we are proud to have a set of behaviours that reflect our unique culture and guide our decisions, defining how we work together to defy ordinary and shape extraordinary. These behaviours are assessed as part of the recruitment process:

  • Partner and customer focus – Your tendency to put your partners and customers first in all that you do by understanding their needs and striving to achieve win-win solutions.
  • Collaboration and communication – Your ability to forge lasting relationships which are built upon mutual trust, open communication and sharing of information and success.
  • Creativity and innovation – Possessing a passion for driving continuous improvement through spotting opportunities and seeking the views of others.
  • Team and personal development – You take time to drive your own development, whilst also encouraging team members and partners to do the same.
  • Impact and influence – You listen to different perspectives, evaluate, persuade and carefully shape your work to deliver truly impactful results.
  • Deliver on your promises – You demonstrate a can-do attitude and you drive to overcome obstacles, acting with a sense of urgency because you’re passionate about what you do.
