Bio-Rad Laboratories has an open Senior Data Scientist position in business analytics. The successful candidate will be highly specialized in the open-source Big Data tools and techniques of Data Science. This position will play a critical role in helping transform the company into a business that differentiates and competes on information and analytics; serve key stakeholders across the Global Supply Chain, Global Commercial Operations, Marketing and Sales, Finance and Customer Support organizations; decompose business pain-points into analytical solutions that deliver iterative insights and incremental value; and identify opportunities where machine learning can deliver global business value via forecasting, recommender systems, automated decision support and customer segmentation and targeting. As a member of the global business analytics team you will have access to an exciting mix of tools like Hadoop, Spark, Hive, Impala, Presto, Drill, Nifi, Python, RStudio-Server, RShiny-Server, Jupyter, Zeppelin, Git/GitHub, RDBMS, NoSQL, Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure and PowerBI.

What you’ll be doing:

  • Identify, experiment and test new ways to deliver value from combinations of operational data, customer data and third-party data sources
  • Transform complex datasets both structured and unstructured into business insights
  • Build and manage data flows that combine internal and external data sources, develop custom ETL/ELT processes that invoke data profiling and data cleansing best practices and provision high-quality data for analysis, modeling and dashboarding
  • Perform descriptive, exploratory and inferential statistical analysis as well as supervised and unsupervised machine learning
  • Utilize Cloud based platforms such AWS and Azure to architect solutions
  • Agile-style, iterative MVP development seeking feedback from business
  • Stay on the cutting edge of technology and Data Science
  • Serve as Data Science representative for the global business analytics center of excellence
  • Coach and assist business partners along the journey from Excel style analytics to advanced Big Data style analytics
  • Strong user focus and ability to be consultative to deliver unique approaches and analytics solutions

What you need for this role:

  • Master Degree or PhD in statistics, computer science or mathematics or similar quantitative field with at least 1 year work experience
  • Candidates with Bachelor’s Degree in statistics, computer science or mathematics with at least 3 years experience and tangible results in Data Science will be given special consideration
  • Broad knowledge and awareness of Apache Software Foundation tools and incubator projects
  • Demonstrated passion for Data Science through participation in open-source or hobby projects
  • Natural curiosity and a strong passion for empirical research and problem solving
  • Ability to work in collaborative and cross-functional teams and environments
  • Programming
