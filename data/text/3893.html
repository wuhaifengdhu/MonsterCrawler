Roche Sequecing Solutions Santa Clara, is a fast moving, highly technical team of people with the ambitious goal of disrupting Next Generation DNA Sequencing. Our technology fuses active biological and solid state elements to bring single molecule based sequence detection to scale. Developing this unique platform requires expertise across a range of fields: Software Engineering, Data Science, Information Theory, Biophysics, Biology, Chemistry, Protein Engineering, Digital and Analog Circuit Design, and Electrochemistry, just to name a few.

As a Senior Data Scientist / Software Engineer in our Data Science and Analytics Group, you will have the opportunity to work with experts in each of these fields, all of whom are focused on the common goal of bringing our sequencing platform to market. Your role will involve a wide variety of software development activities that drive towards this goal. The main objectives of this role:

* develop code that provides actionable insight through analysis of vast quantities of sequencing data generated by our prototype instruments

* integrate and streamline new algorithms into an evolving pipeline

* support the growing cloud infrastructure needs of our Data Science group and work with dedicated IT/Dev-Ops groups as needed, to ensure appropriate resources are available

Responsibilities Include:

* Collaborative Software Engineering in Python

* Cloud infrastructure support for Data Science and Machine Learning group.

* Install, configure and maintain linux environments for reproducible R&D

* Monitor host and application performance, resolve issues

* Who you are

You're someone who wants to influence your own development. You're looking for a company where you have the opportunity to pursue your interests across functions and geographies. Where a job title is not considered the final definition of who you are, but the starting point.

Skills

* 3+ years collaborative software engineering experience

* 2+ plus years scientific programming experience in Python, especially numpy / pandas / scikitlearn / matplotlib.

* Experience with distributed computing or cloud implementation

* Experience with large-scale use of cloud computing for massive data

* Familiarity with HPC clusters and tools

* Broad knowledge and experience leveraging open-source tools for systems administration

* Knowledge of modern cloud-deployed technologies including Github and AWS

* Experience with automation/configuration management using tools like Ansible, Puppet, Chef or equivalent

* Working skills in Bash, Python, or other scripting language

Background

* Master's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or equivalent industry experience

* 3+ years industry experience

* 2+ years experience in supporting Linux environments

* Desire to work as part of a team in a startup-like environment
