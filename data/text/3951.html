Research Engineer - Computer Vision (Augmented Reality, up to $180k)
San Francisco, CA


This company is in the augmented reality space, and is revolutionizing the retail industry with its technology.  It has raised a large round of funding from top tier investors, and is rapidly growing.

Job Responsibilities:
-    Working with a talented team of software engineers, mathematicians, and physicists to bring state of the art computer vision and machine learning technologies to market at scale.
-    Improving and advancing the company’s products and technologies using methodical research techniques.
-    Applying machine learning and computer vision principles to improve the company’ recommendation engine
-    Developing and owning projects from ideation to rollout.
-    Using large proprietary datasets to conduct computer vision experiments
-    Applying the latest available techniques to improve facial feature detection, including skin tone, landmark detection, and gender.

Qualifications:
-    Strong collaboration skills, a good team player
-    Experience applying machine learning and computer vision principles to real world data
-    Good knowledge of Computer Science, math and statistics fundamentals (algorithms and data structures, meshing, sampling theory, linear algebra, etc.)
-    Knowledge of data science technologies such as R, Octave, Python, pandas, scipy,  numpy, matplotlib, etc.
-    Experience developing elegant, high performance code with C++ and/ or Python
-    Either a Ph.D. or Master's degree in a STEM discipline, or equivalent industry experience.
