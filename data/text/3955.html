What if your job description were simply "Make tomorrow better?" &nbsp That's the essence of roles within our Cortana Team. Every day, we bring an insatiable curiosity to the table, challenging ourselves to reimagine what is and what can be. We build on what's come before to create what's next. We drive machine intelligence. &nbsp We help shape the future. &nbsp We empower billions of people around the globe.

Our mission is to understand, recombine, and utilize data having geospatial location essential to millions of users, especially, in enabling a cutting-edge personal assistant.

The ideal candidate would have sound understanding of data science, experience in machine learning, and strong S/W development skills. The candidate should enjoy working in a small startup environment, agile prototyping, and facing new ideas. We are looking for a technical background in some of the following areas: data mining, machine learning, search, mathematics, and geo data.

Job Responsibilities

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Develop predictive algorithms and produce data insights

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Suggest, specify, and prototype original components and help with their productizing

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Provide technical leadership, learn, and partner with other Microsoft teams

Qualifications

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Ph.D. in computer science, machine learning, mathematics, AI, or a related field

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Ability to formalize real-life problems and to focus on business objectives

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Good understanding of predictive analytics process from data validation to model assessment, stability, and deployment monitoring

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp 1+ years of programming and/or development skills using C#/C++/Java/Python

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Good contingency planning skills, ability to anticipate and to overcome difficulties

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Strong communication skills and ability to work with multi-disciplinary teams
