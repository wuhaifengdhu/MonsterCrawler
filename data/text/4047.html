We are seeking a Data Science Analyst to join the Business Intelligence Division of Support Business Application Department within Information Technology Admin Area of Saudi Aramco.

Saudi Aramco Business Intelligence Division (BID) is responsible for gathering, storing, analyzing, and providing access to corporate data that enables users to gain insights and make better fact-based business decisions. BID will provide a framework for implementing self-service business intelligence to distribute reporting, dash boarding, and data exploration. BID will enable advanced analytics to deliver business analytical solutions using predictive analytics, data mining (text analytics), and geo-spatial analytics.

The Data Science candidate primary role is to act as a Data Science Architect and will be required to develop and investigate hypotheses, structure experiments and build mathematical models for predictive and text analytics. The candidate will be involved in all phases of analytics projects including: question formulation, design, research and development, implementation, and testing. Minimum Requirements As the successful candidate, you will hold a Bachelor's degree in one of the following disciplines; Industrial Engineering, Applied Mathematics, Statistics, or Computer Science from a recognized and approved program. An advanced degree is preferred. You will have five plus years of hands-on experience in data science including at least three plus years in statistical methods, such as machine learning (ML), information retrieval, data mining, text Mining, predictive analytics, and geo-spatial analytics.

The following are other requirements to perform:

You will have experience in predictive analytics and machine learning using R, Python.

You will be able to demonstrate experience in applying ML to real-world problems.

You must have deep exposure in statistics, statistical analytics.

You must have Knowledge on SQL and database querying language.

Data visualization experience is a requirement.

Data mining, data cleanup, and data massaging experience is a requirement.

You will be able to analyze data sets (structured/unstructured) and build sophisticated mathematical/statistical models.

Knowledge in math like linear Algebra, calculus and probability is a requirement.

You will be able to demonstrate project work with evidence of creative and critical thinking.

You will also have superior analytical, problem solving and organizational skills.

Excellent English language communication skills (verbal, presentation and written), including the ability to collaborate and influence effectively across all levels of a matrix organization is also necessary.

You will have the ability to show Knowledge of big data tools like Hadoop, Hive and Pig, and cloud tools such as Amazon. Duties & Responsibilities You will be required to perform the following:

Work with Saudi Aramco business users to gather and refine specific business scenarios and document analytical solution requirements.

Create and maintain documentation of technical requirements including use cases.

Define the data requirements for specific business scenario and Identify sources of data in consultation with business clients and technical team.

Identify and resolve data/information gaps for meeting business objectives/goals including data quality issues and sources (data feeds, the master data, etc.), and identify training and validation datasets as appropriate.

Build analytical models to execute specific business scenarios using all available tool sets.

Evaluate and prototype innovative solutions using the latest technology trends.
