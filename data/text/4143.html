Company Overview

At Proofpoint, we have a passion for protecting people, data, and brands from today's advanced threats and compliance risks. We hire the best people in the business to:

* Build and enhance our proven security platform

* Blend innovation and speed in a constantly evolving cloud architecture

* Analyze new threats and offer deep insight through data-driven intel

* Collaborate with customers to help solve their toughest security challenges

We are singularly devoted to helping our customers protect what matters most. That's why we're a leader in next-generation cybersecurity—and why more than half of the Fortune 100 trust us as a security partner.

The Role

The engineers in the tool & data science group are responsible for creating tools to help our internal customers analyze large sets of challenging real-world data in order to understand what types of malware are affecting our customers.

Your day-to-day

* You'll be working with analysts, product marketing, sales engineers and other developers to understand their problems and workflows, and propose solutions.

* We use rapid, iterative development processes to create UI-based and command-line tools for internal users, both client and server side. While working on new tools, you'll be demo'ing your work every couple of weeks to get feedback and refine requirements.

* You'll be part of a team working on making the most effective malware detection products in the market. Your suggestions may be the driving factor in a new way to look at threat data, or in getting data in front of threat analysts faster than we've ever been able to do before.

* You'll be helping to establish tooling, frameworks and procedures for next generation tool development.

What you bring to the team

* BS degree in a technical discipline

* 6+ years experience

* Expertise in building high performance web applications

* Experience with many or all of ReactJS, Redux, AngularJS, JavaScript(ES6), Node.js, Python, GO

* Passionate about making tools self-service, data-driven, and reliable

* Familiarity with NPM dependency package management

* Familiarity with Unix Shell Script, SQL, NoSQL ( previous experience with ElasticSearch , Cassandra, and/or MongoDB great but not required)

* Familiarity with many tools for secure app development (oauth protocols, AWS security groups, certificates)

* Experience with Amazon tool set (ELB, VPCs, etc) great but not required

* Familiarity with agile development
