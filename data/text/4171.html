Want to join a company on the cutting edge of technology and travel? Want to be part of a fantastic and fun company that's revolutionizing the online travel advertising space?

Sojern is travel's leading data-driven performance marketing engine. Through its Sojern Traveler Platform and billions of traveler intent signals across online and mobile channels, Sojern puts more heads in beds and travelers in town for its clients worldwide. Currently one of the fastest growing travel tech companies, Sojern works with top travel brands and independent hotels in North America, Latin America, Europe, MEA and APAC. The company is headquartered in San Francisco, with key offices in Dubai, London, New York, Omaha and Singapore. For more information, please visit www.sojern.com.

Join us in our airy loft centrally located in SOMA. In SF we are close to all transportation. Our campus is SF and that can't be beat. We offer competitive salary, equity, benefits, unlimited vacation days, equipment of your choice, fantastic coworkers, well-stocked kitchen, and team/community events.

You:

* Think in patterns at scale and how to apply them to the domain of travel.

* Know how to translate human insight into machine scaled systems.

* Keep on top of the latest research, papers, contests, systems and their practical application.

* Know that the right ensemble makes sweet music.

* Dream of finding latent novel business domain knowledge in creative places.

* Have proven experience shipping ML software, or insatiable desire to do so!

* The ability to express your ideas, absorb other people's, influence the team, and grow.

* Are an algo fiend and know the best player is the one that makes everyone better.

Responsibilities:

* Hands on model building, application, instrumentation, data science engineering position.

* Build, tune, tweak the core learning prediction system to achieve the conversion probabilities that drive Sojern audience satisfaction.

* Apply domain expertise to find latent features within our data sets.

* Apply multiple techniques to optimize performance: logistic regression, stochastic gradient descent, decision trees...measure, salt to taste, iterate.

* Optimize our bidding systems to maximize budget spend and minimize cost.

* Follow best practices and procedures in the team for achieving day to day objectives.

* Ability to work independently, learn from and mentor other team members.

Required:

* BS/MS/PhD CS, Machine Learning, Applied Math, Applied Stats, Physics or practical equivalent

* 3+ years practical experience building: customer segmentation, predictive models, bid optimization, budget allocation in an online delivery system.

* 3+ years practical application of ML tools and techniques to large sets.

* 3+ years production experience in one of: Java, Scala, R, Python(SciPy/Numeric/Pandas/SKLearn)

* 3+ years production experience with one of MapReduce/Hive/Pig on hadoop

* Expertise in regression techniques, information gain, sampling

Desired Skills:

* Knowledge of advertising technologies, techniques, pitfalls, pratfalls, nooks and crannies

* Practical experience applying predictive modeling in an online digital marketing system

Other:

* Location, SF, SoMa.

* Culture: Our team loves a good time

* Snacks: Well-stocked kitchen to fill your breakfast, lunch, and snack needs

* Opportunities: Be part of a growing team with training and support to help you grow

* Give Back: We give 40 hours a year to volunteer and organize office volunteer programs with local organizations

* Ownership: Lead creative and challenging projects

* Meals: Monthly catered lunches & happy hours

* UNLIMITED vacation policy

* Wealth: Stock options
