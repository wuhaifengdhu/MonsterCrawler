Who we are: EMD Serono is the North American biopharma brand of Merck KGaA, Darmstadt, Germany - a leading science and technology company - focused exclusively on specialty care. For more than 40 years, the brand has integrated cutting-edge science, innovative products and industry-leading patient support and access programs. EMD Serono has deep expertise in neurology, fertility and endocrinology, as well as a robust pipeline of potential therapies in oncology, immuno-oncology and immunology as R&D focus areas.

Your role: Support the design, implementation and operation of the Healthcare Insights Engine

* Design and implement a data infrastructure that supports both quantitative and qualitative analyses of large datasets

* Drive automation and operational excellence in our environment.

* Ensuring the security, scalability and uptime of our distributed environment.

* Design and implement secure authentication methods between a variety of applications (SAML, OpenID, ADFS)

* Collaborate and partner with the Data Science team and business teams.

* Monitor technological trends and apply them to existing or new architectures to improve operational efficiency.

* Implement robust interfaces to various enterprise systems and applications.

Who you are: Experience working with and automating the AWS ecosystem (EC2, RDS, Redshift, S3, CloudFormation)

* AWS Solution Architect certified or similar certification/proof of experience

* Proficiency with developer tools and workflows (git, bash)

* Experience with BI, data warehouse and analytical systems, preferably Redshift, Qlik/Tableau

* Experience with RDBMS and NoSQL, preferably PostgreSQL and DynamoDB

* Hands-on experience with the design and architecture of a big data pipeline and infrastructure

* Proven, experienced architect and technical leader in data engineering

* Experiences in a mid-to-large sized team in a dynamic organization with a mature process.

* Experience with cloud data infrastructure such as AWS EMR, S3 is a strong plus

* Experience developing server-side JavaScript (NodeJS). R and python are a plus.

* Bachelors or Master's degree in relevant major along with equivalent experience.

* Technology-oriented, keeping up with the most cutting-edge relevant data technology direction

* Known for smart, logical thinking and being detail-oriented.

* Excellent oral and written communication skills;

* Flexible with the ability to work well in a global, team-oriented culture and environment.

* Ability to travel up to 15%

What we offer:

Your possibilities to develop in our growing company are just as manifold as our product portfolio. We offer a modern and international working environment to unleash your innovative potential in diverse teams, and take responsibility for our employees, products, the environment and society.
