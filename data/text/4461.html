Data Scientist from Corporate Consulting Services, Inc.

Boston, MA

Report this job

About the Job

The candidates need to be methodology and process driven, detail oriented, hands on, fast-learners and passionate about technology

Responsibilities:

· Apply tools and develop strategies/software to discover actionable insight from big video marketing data.

· Develop tools/models to expose the actionable insights in the course of a media campaign so they can be used to optimize marketing strategy and execution.

· Write white papers and other material to communicate general insights and other findings form data science work.

Requirements:

· Experience in several of the following: Python, R, machine learning, natural language processing, business intelligence tools, online marketing analytics tools, advanced Microsoft Excel.

· Expertise in predictive analytics/statistical modeling/data mining algorithms. Must have knowledge/experience in some/all of the following: Multivariate Regression, Logistic Regression, Support Vector Machines, Bagging, Boosting, Decision Trees, Lifetime analysis, common clustering algorithms, Optimization, Stochastic Processes.

· Demonstrable ability to provide compelling insights based on large data sets.

· Ability to make intelligent approximations of mathematical models in order to make them practical and scalable.

· Exceptional organizational skills, meticulous attention to detail, and ability to maintain focus in fast paced environment.

· Solid understanding of marketing platforms (emphasis on Display/Ads) and experience using several consumer oriented apps.

· Knowledge of Unix/Linux and ability to learn, understand and analyze data and other information generated by the server platform.

· Strong initiative, flexibility, and ability to learn quickly.

· Self-motivated and self-managed with a high degree of dependability.

· Analytical, problem solving skills, ability to learn and apply new tools & applications.

· Ability to communicate effectively in writing and verbally.

Nice to Have:
Experience with SQL and NoSQL databases is a plus.

Ideal Candidate:

· 4-6 years of experience in similar role analyzing large amounts of data and generating actionable business value

· BA/BS in Data Science, Data analytics, Computer Science or related field

· Interested in working in a dynamic and fast paced environment

· Located in the Boston area and would be able to be present at our headquarters.
