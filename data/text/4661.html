Accelerate data science by building the free software platform for verifiable large scale computation.

JOB DESCRIPTION

At Curoverse, you'll work on a small team of senior software developers to build the Arvados platform and the Curoverse products that are based on Arvados.

RESPONSIBILITIES

* Program in Golang, Javascript, Python, Ruby

* Work on hard distributed computing problems

* Use scrum in an agile, self-managed team of peers

* Develop big-data storage and analytical systems

* Help create a massive, in-memory, real-time query engine

* Contribute to a free/open source platform and community

WE'RE LOOKING FOR SOMEONE WHO...

* Has a solid background in Golang, Python, or both

* Loves writing and improving code

* Works well in a small team of peers

* Has used multiple programming languages to build production systems

* Has substantial experience contributing to free software projects

* Has experience with big data and distributed computing systems

* Can work in a fast-paced startup environment

OUR STACK

Some of the technologies we use: Debian, Ubuntu, CentOS, Docker, Git, Golang, Javascript, Jenkins, jQuery, Nginx, PostgreSQL, Python, Puppet, Rails, Ruby, Twitter Bootstrap.

WHY WORK HERE

* Work that matters - Build software that will be the backbone of modern medicine.

* Hard problems - Solve software engineering problems in distributed computing and big data.

* Diverse tasks - We have a small team with a big project, so you won't be stuck working in some corner.

* Free software - Your code will be released under AGPLv3 / Apache 2.0.

* Sustainable lifestyle - Do great work and have time and energy for the other things life has to offer.

* Convenient office location - Our office is right in Davis Square, Somerville.

COMPENSATION

We offer competitive salaries, stock options, health/dental/vision, flexible time off, and other benefits.

LOCATION

We prefer candidates who are based in the Boston area or can relocate, but we are open to remote employees.
