Overview

Criteo is in search of a passionate, highly motivated Account Strategist to join our Mid-Market Strategic Accounts team in Boston, MA supporting our top clients. You will be working directly with our largest Mid-Market client advertisers to optimize their campaigns, communicate their needs internally and lead performance reviews. We are very excited to be in the Americas as a profitable and growing company, but most of all, being positioned as a global leader in personalized retargeting solutions!

You will be responsible for managing, maintaining, and up-selling post sale optimizations with our large, and most strategic Mid-Market client partners. This position will be responsible for analyzing an assortment of metrics for all ads served, communicating issues/concerns internally to all parties involved and being the client's "Go-To" person for any questions. You will monitor and optimize existing campaigns to help grow and cultivate strong, long-lasting relationships with clients.

What you will be doing?

* Managing the day to day relationships with our largest, most strategic Mid-Market clients to ensure we exceed their expectations.

* Leading client business reviews and communicating performance optimizations recommended along with the planned roadmap.

* Analyzing advertising campaign metrics and make sure client goals are reached (ROI, ROAS, COS, CPO, Revenue…).

* Escalating internally any issues and concerns raised by clients.

* Partner closely with the support teams (Data Science, Tech Solutions, Creative Services) to ensure optimal campaign setup.

* Some travel is required.

What we need?

* Must have at least 4+ years of direct or digital advertising/marketing experience, managing top tiered clients, with quarterly/annual goals or revenue accountability.

* Proficient knowledge and experience in display, performance marketing (retargeting) or other digital advertising, with full-funnel account strategy initiatives.

* Must be PASSIONATE about performance marketing or digital advertising, enjoys thinking outside the box, and love's driving results for their clients!

* Proven experience in managing and growing top tiered clients in a fast-paced, innovative, and goal-oriented or revenue driven environment.

* Experience building and delivering strategic quarterly business reviews. Some travel will be required quarterly to meet with top clients.

* Proven track record of analytical, problem-solving and decision-making skills.

* Self-driven, highly motivated, and confident to provide outstanding service and optimal performance of campaigns for top clients.

* Experience working with e-commerce such as Retail, Travel, and Financial clients a plus.

* Must love working collaboratively across multiple operational teams.

* Must be authorized to work for any employer in the USA without restriction.
