Req ID: 39901BR Position Summary: Aetna's Consumer Health Services - Clinical Analytics team is seeking a Data Science Analyst to develop analytic solutions to optimize care management interventions, improve health outcomes, and reduce health care costs. Solutions will leverage behavioral economic and analytic insights regarding consumers preferences and needs. The Data Science Analyst will develop, implement and measure the impact of solutions spanning all available touch points with health care consumers. Fundamental Components: The position will work with a team of analysts and business partners to deliver solutions to important health care challenges. The Data Science Analyst will be responsible for extraction and manipulation of structured and unstructured data from multiple, large data sources, using advanced statistical techniques and software to develop predictive models and actionable analytic insights. Applies analytical rigor and statistical methods to analyze large amounts of data, using advanced statistical techniques. Develops, validates and executes algorithms and predictive models to investigate problems, detect patterns and recommend solutions. Performs analyses of structured and unstructured data to solve moderately complex business problems utilizing advanced statistical techniques and mathematical analyses.. Develops data structures and pipelines to organize, collect and standardize data that helps generate insights and addresses reporting needs Uses data visualization techniques to effectively communicate analytical results and support business decisions. Creates and evaluates the data needs of assigned projects and assures the data integrity. Explores existing data and recommends additional sources of data for improvements. Documents projects including business objectives, data gathering and processing, detailed set of results and analytical metrics. Background/Experience: 2 or more years of experience in data management , reporting, and analytics. Demonstrates proficiency in machine learning, statistical analyses, and predictive modeling. Experience using SQL, SAS , R, and Python to manipulate large datasets and develop statistical models. Knowledge of data management in a Hadoop environment, including use of HIVE. Demonstrates strong ability to communicate technical concepts and implications to business partners.
