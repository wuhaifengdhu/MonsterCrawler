Acquia is transforming the strategies of companies all over the world with our Digital Experience Platform. We are passionate and relentlessly committed to helping our clients create journeys and experiences that are more relevant, personalized, and built for a fast-changing, always-connected, customer centric world. Headquartered in Boston with offices around the globe, we have been named as one of North America’s fastest growing software companies as reported by Deloitte and Inc. Magazine, and have been rated a leader by the analyst community and named one of the Best Places to Work by the Boston Business Journal. We are Acquia. We are building for the future of the web, and we want you to be a part of it.

Responsibilities:

  • Work with data scientists to architect and build systems that use Machine Learning techniques to provide services via Rest APIs
  • Develop reliable data pipelines that convert data into features that can be used to train machine learning models
  • Analyze and understand data sources, participate in design discussions and provide guidance about database technology best practices
  • Write tested, high quality code that can be quickly moved into production

Qualifications and skills:

  • B.S. or M.Sc. in Computer Science or Software Engineering, or equivalent experience
  • Experience building highly-performant data heavy applications
  • Expert knowledge of SQL and NoSQL tools
  • Working knowledge of Redshift, DynamoDB, Kinesis, Apache Spark or other big data technologies
  • 3+ years experience with AWS services
  • Strong knowledge of Linux / UNIX systems
  • Proficiency in high level language such as Java, Scala, C++, Go or Python
  • Knowledge of how to assess the performance of data solutions, how to diagnose performance problems, and tools used to monitor and tune performance
  • Understanding of software quality assurance principles

Nice to have:

  • Experience with data mining, machine learning, statistical modeling tools or underlying algorithms
