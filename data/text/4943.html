Altamira is an Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action employer. All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, disability, or protected veteran status.

Altamira Technologies is searching for talented Software Developers to support the US Army's 513th Military Intelligence Brigade functions as an Echelon-Above-Corps multi-discipline intelligence unit. The GETS mission is to deliver GEOINT toolsets (the development of software tools and tooling that reside on the GETS platform) through an enterprise-grade, Software as a Service (SaaS) platform. GETS supports many agencies and organizations on multiple networks, including every Combatant Command (CCMD) and military branch. All Information Technology (IT) administrative operations, development operations, and end-user support operations reside at Fort Gordon, GA. GETS hardware is currently located in Hawaii, Germany, and Georgia, with other instances planned. From those locations, GETS services are available via network connections worldwide. The specific mission focus pertaining to this performance work statement involves daily operation of GETS IT hardware, integration activities with GEOINT processes, direct end-user support, and development of new automated technical capabilities to satisfy the supported commanders' stated requirements.

Job Responsibilities

* Provides analysis related to the design, development, and implementation of hardware for products

* Develops test strategies, devices, and systems

* Performs stress and performance tests on a variety of computer hardware including circuit boards, processors and wiring

* Web/widget-based applications for accessing geospatial data

Develop using Java, Javascript, Matlab, IDL, CSS, C++, HTML, Python and other programming languages approved by the government. Web-services shall be compliant with OGC standards.

Active TS/SCI clearance required

Required Experience

* Bachelor's Degree in computer science, engineering, or related science field

* Experience with GEOINT Enterprise TPED System (GETS) preferred

* Ability to work independently or on a team to solve engineering problems.
