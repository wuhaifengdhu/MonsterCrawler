The Job

Our Research team is critical because they prove we can do what we say we can. They analyze a massive number of audio files, create algorithms to extract interesting forensic information, and work on real problems of phone-based fraud. In addition to sticking it to the bad guys, you have the opportunity to be part of an environment that feeds innovation, supports learning, recognizes style, and rewards effort. We are looking to add an Computer Vision Research Scientist to work on research and development of future Pindrop product offerings.

Who You Are

  • PhD in Computer Science or related field
  • Deep knowledge and understanding of pattern recognition, applied machine learning, computer vision and biometrics.
  • Good publication record in the field of computer vision and pattern recognition
  • Experience with different deep neural network architectures
  • Strong communication skills

Tech Experience

  • You are fluent with Linux command line (Ubuntu preferred)
  • Fluent with a scripting language (Python preferred)
  • Competent in at least one database system (MySQL preferred)
  • You understand basic signal processing concepts
  • You are familiar with applied machine learning concepts
  • You have experience with different DNN architectures and tools
  • You are able to articulate complex technical concepts to non-technical parties in an informal Q&A setting

Experience with these doesn't hurt, either

  • Audio signal analysis
  • Image filtering and segmentation
  • Object detection, recognition and tracking
  • Algorithm implementation in Python and/or C++

Our Bio

Founded in 2011  |  Raised $122 million

Venture backed by Andreessen Horowitz, Google Capital, IVP, Citi Ventures, Felicis Ventures, & GV

Pindrop is the pioneer in voice fraud prevention and authentication. We provide enterprise solutions to reduce fraud losses and authentication expense for some of the largest call centers in the world. Pindrop’s patented Phoneprinting™ technology can identify, locate and authenticate phone devices uniquely just from the call audio thereby detecting fraudulent calls as well as verifying legitimate callers. We have been selected by the world’s largest banks, insurers, brokerages and retailers, detecting over 80% of fraud even for attackers never seen before. Pindrop’s solutions are allowing customers to reduce call time and improve their customers’ experience even while reducing fraud losses.
