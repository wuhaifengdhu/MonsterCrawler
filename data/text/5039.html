Unlock Your Career Potential: Technology at ADP. Do you enjoy exploring, identifying and inspiring the future of the workplace and the lives of millions of people? At ADP, the world's largest B2B cloud company, our Technology team is comprised of brilliant engineers, architects, data scientists, infrastructure experts, and more. We were first in our industry to offer a SaaS solution and continue to push the envelope utilizing the latest operating platforms to deliver the highly automated, intelligent and predictive solutions that are redefining what is possible. Named one of Forbes' "Most Innovative Companies" and one of Computerworld's "100 Best Places to Work in IT", we are committed to leading the way in product development and research, empowering you to bring to life the latest innovations that will forever change the way businesses manage their most vital asset, employees. 

Seeking a Sr .NET/UI developer for a full time position in our Alpharetta, GA location. 

This technical, senior role will write structured, organized and annotated code to best practice standards, and will write test and acceptance criteria for same. S/he will provide business analysis services to turn requirements from business units into functional and technical specifications, define the architectural structure of the eventual solution, prepare project plans with accurate estimates for work to be performed, and meet all agreed deadlines. The selected candidate will provide technical support to user groups and resolve system problems efficiently. The candidate will produce full documentation for all development projects completed. 

As a senior member of the development team, the successful candidate will be expected to be able to work autonomously, with minimal supervision, mentor junior members of the team, and assist where needed. S/he will stay up to date on industry standards and emerging technologies, and make recommendations on software package evaluations. 

Successful candidate will have at min 5 years' experience at an expert level in the following disciplines: 

 
*  .NET (ASP.NET Web Forms, C#)/MVC 5/Entity Framework 6 
*  Javascript/knockout.js  

And will be proficient in the following areas: 

 
*  JSON (json.net, specifically) and jquery-ui 
*  CSS3, Bootstrap, Angular JS 
*  MS SQL Server / Linq 
*  Version Control platforms, such as TFS, BitBucket, Subversion and/or GitHub  

Candidates who also possess the following knowledge will be highly preferred: 

 
*  Pricing Engines 
*  State Machines  

ADP, LLC is a highly collaborative environment. This senior role requires a candidate who can critically analyze requirements from the business partners, and extract appropriate questions to determine end goals of the requirement, providing out-of-the-box solutions where necessary. 

The successful candidate will be familiar with and have experience working within an Agile/SCRUM framework, will possess the ability to effectively manage time between competing priorities and closely collaborate/contribute to successful releases with associates who may be located anywhere in the world, requiring a mature communication style. 

BS in Mathematics, Computer Science or a related discipline, or commensurate combination of education and experience will be required.
