PositionTitle: AssistantManager, Trade Strategy

Location: Various

Reportsto: Senior Manager, Trade Strategy

The Trade Strategy – Assistant Manager (TSAM) willwork closely with the Senior Manager Trade Strategy and cross functional teams inthe development of sub-category strategies focusing on driving growth andexpansion.

With analysis of syndicated data, category andindustry trend information, and internal performance metrics, the TSAM will assistin the development of comprehensive strategies focused around enabling greatersell-in, sell-through, and market share gains. The TSAM will prepare reportsthat will inform Sales and Channel Marketing Teams as they pursue opportunitieswith customers. This position will contribute to the development of strategic plansincorporating category knowledge to drive profitable business results.

Specific Responsibilities

Specific responsibilities will include but are not limited to:

Broad Sub-Category Understanding

* Research trends in assigned category and providereports to inform internal constituents as well as external customers.

* Wield data tools such as NPD, IRI, andinternal data to advise the development of strategies and tactics for marketexpansion

* Offer category insight and analysis foron customer meeting presentations

Business Management

* Assist in development of promotional guidelinerecommendations by sub-category or product line

* Track and report on business performanceand sales results.

Multi-Channel Knowledge

* Provide analysis of the various trends onassigned categories.

* Monitor channel shifting and theassociated impacts reflected in the marketplace.

* Perform competitive analyses during keyperiods to inform new programming.

* Offer insight into market pricingpatterns and consumer purchasing behavior.

Manage Innovation Pipeline

* Partner with Brand Development tomanage pipeline of innovation, bringing both a customer and consumerperspective to the development process

* Use knowledge of customer and consumerto recommend optimal assortments and channel exclusives

* Build in-store and .com requirementsfor a successful go-to-market into launch development plans

Project Management

* Manage flow of information and requestsfrom Sr. Management, CDO, business segments, etc.

* Prioritize and resource projectseffectively.

* Ability to manage multiple projectssimultaneously under tight deadlines

* Ability to prioritize and resourceprojects effectively

* Bachelor'sdegree (emphasis in business, marketing, finance or related field)

* 1 years of experience working with a leading CPG company (Trade or ChannelMarketing, Consulting, Data Science, Category Management, Finance) preferred

* Stronganalytical skills and computer software proficiency (e.g. Excel, PowerPoint,SharePoint, etc.)

* Strategicthinking, self-starter with ability to manage multiple projects simultaneouslywith aggressive deadlines

* Must beable to travel 10-20% of the time.

Additional Skills

* Build strong sustainable businessrelationships with customers and customer teams

* Excellent communication skills – bothwritten and verbal

* Direct and perform analysis across awide variety of category business issues and make fact-based recommendations

* Convey complex issues with strong communicationand presentation skills
