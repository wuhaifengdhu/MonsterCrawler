The Country Solution Manager for is responsible for overall product delivery for the US. Responsibility is focused on the activities related to defining and managing roadmap priorities through collaboration with internal (and sometimes external) business partners; functional analysis and design; and integrating all aspects of product development and delivery into a coherent solution, The role uses defined processes and guidance to ensure priorities are managed appropriately to meet broad and specific business objectives. This role requires significant collaboration with various internal and external business partners.  

This role requires significant functional expertise of SAP payroll delivery for the US, as well as practical knowledge of GlobalView® processes and standards and US specific services (printing, money movement, benefits, etc). Technical SAP expertise is required, but not at a very granular level.  

This role is expected to function independently based on information gathered from multiple sources including internal and external business partners as well as Country Solutions & Localization team leadership. The role is expected to manage all aspects of product solution delivery and related communications. The role is responsible for providing regular status reports regarding key issues following defined processes and guidelines to internal business partners and team leadership. 

 Reporting to the Manager -- Application Development, the role interacts heavily with senior level managers and consultants throughout the organization and may be required to interact with executive-level management from time to time.   
  

 
*  To provide functional analysis and design of solutions for identified problems and suggested enhancements for the US solution and Interfaces. 
*  To manage overall solution delivery to ensure committed priorities are delivered and all stakeholders are kept current regarding future or proposed issue resolutions or enhancements. 
*  To oversee and personally adhere to standards for process and methodology; analysis and design; and documentation and communication in all work activities performed. 
*  To collaborate with internal and external business partners to ensure that functionality is optimized between being feature-rich, easily implemented and maintained, highly usable and intuitive, and meets committed timelines, while remaining compliant with local regulations and best practices. In the absence of a legislative consultant, the Country Solution Manager is responsible for interacting with legal services (if available) or other resources to make every reasonable attempt to keep the solution compliant with local regulations. 
*  To communicate status of projects regularly to ensure no surprises and to proactively address potential roadblocks. 
*  To educate internal resources on the functional aspects of developments and fixes -- through formal training, documentation, and ad hoc communications.  

    
  

 
*  The role of Country Solution Manager does not have any direct reports.    
  

 
*  SAP functional expertise in analysis and design for the US. 
*  Working knowledge of SAP technical skills in analysis and design as well as configuration and testing. 
*  Strong knowledge of US Product Interfaces (PSS, CAPS, Garnishments, Benefits, eTime etc.)    
  

 
*  Effective written and verbal communication skills in English. 
*  Significant appreciation of cultural nuances differences and their impact on effective communication.    
  

 
*  Ability to adapt to evolving processes, tools, and team structure 
*  Ability to adhere to processes and guidance and make sound decisions with little guidance under sometimes ambiguous circumstances 
*  Learning agility 
*  Resourceful and proven ability to solve technical and functional problems without significant, specific prior knowledge or experience  

  

 
*  Self starter with strong initiative 
*  Attention to detail and methodical 
*  Ability to work under stress in a dynamic environment 
*  Ability to work flexible hours to accommodate different time zones. 
*  Agile team player.  

    
    
  

 
*  Practical working experience with SAP-based country payroll from a functional perspective (design and analysis, at a minimum) for the US. 
*  Proven ability to manage a portfolio of projects of varying sizes, effectively and concurrently with little or no direction 
*  Experience with US Benefits or other Payroll related services (printing, money movement, Time & Labor, etc).  

    
  

 
*  Good knowledge of GlobalView® model 
*  Practical working experience with one or more GlobalView® country templates 
*  Prior experience working within a geographically diverse, global organization would be advantageous  

    
    
    
  

 
*  Minimum of five years of experience in functional delivery of solutions for the US via SAP payroll (preferably delivery of product template OR in implementing or maintaining client solutions based on a template)
