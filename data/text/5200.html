 We strive for every interaction to be driven by our CORE values: Insightful Expertise, Integrity is Everything, Service Excellence, Inspiring Innovation, Each Person Counts, Results-Driven, - Social Responsibility. 

The Infrastructure & Operations Group (I&O) is an organization that designs, implements, and supports Infrastructure and Platform solutions in support of business need. I&O established a PLAN, BUILD, RUN Operating Model based on the ITIL framework. Associated with the Operating Model are industry wide roles such as Architect, Service Manager, Project Manager, Design Engineer, Implementation Engineer, IT Operations Control, Tech & App Management, Service Desk, and Client Product Support. 

I&O Technical areas include: Solutions/Product Engineering, Data Network, Voice, Distributed Systems, Mainframe, Midrange, Middleware, Web, Database, End User Computing, Service Technology, Disaster Recovery, or Data Center Site Management. 
 I&O associates are expected to become familiar with any processes, tools or technologies in the areas they are working in.  This posiiton is will be located witin the Tech Cafe in Augusta.

RESPONSIBILITIES: 

 
*   Desktop and Mobile Device Support issue resolution 
*  Work within the Tech Cafe interfacing with internal & external clients 
*  Ability to work and navigate in different Operating Systems such as Windows and Mac OSx 
*  Configuration, installation, troubleshooting, analysis and maintenance of all systems and software applications. Includes desktops, laptops, printers, servers, virtual machines, routers, CDROM technologies, Security systems and all other associated systems. 
*  Deployment, support, troubleshooting and analysis of all hardware and software applications within our scope. 
*  Configuration, installation, troubleshooting and analysis and maintenance for Mac and Windows Operating Systems. 
*  Configuration, installation, troubleshooting and analysis and maintenance for iOS and Droid devices. 
*  Recommends and drives process enhancements; identifies common issues in systems and processes. 
*  Creates, updates and maintains all documentation for processes and procedures including Service level agreements. 
*  Participates in evaluating, testing and documenting new software and hardware changes and upgrades. 
*  Assists with business unit/location specific Business Continuity Plans
