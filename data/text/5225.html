Description

Basic Qualifications:


  • Bachelors Degree in Computer Science or related discipline
  • Minimum of 5 years of experience in Software leadership, product management and/or strategy; specifically software areas of expertise related to Enterprise Software, SaaS, Data Sciences, Cloud and Mobility, IoT


Additional Qualifications:


  • Strong Knowledge of technologies and use cases for Big Data, Cloud-native SaaS, IoT, Analytics/Machine Learning and related areas
  • Machine learning and visualization solutions preferred.
  • Agile, DevOps, continuous integration and continuous delivery tools
  • Multiple cloud computing platforms: Microsoft Azure, Amazon AWS, CloudFoundry and related areas. Plus/Preferred:
  • Microsoft Azure IoT, Amazon IoT, and related solutions
  • Experience with more than one data streaming and batch processing technologies, and distributed messaging technologies
  • Combination of the following technologies: Hadoop, Map Reduce, Pig, Hive, Impala, Spark, Kafka, Storm, SQL and in-memory NoSQL databases such as Hbase and Cassandra.
  • Experience working with and evaluating open source technologies and demonstrated ability to make objective choices
  • Experience architecting highly scalable, highly concurrent and low latency systems
  • Demonstrated interpersonal and communication skills including the ability to effectively collaborate across time zones and cultures
  • Ability to travel up to 40%, including international travel, on a regular basis
