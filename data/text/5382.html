About the Job

SPARKS DEVELOPER


This onsite position is for our client in downtown PA.


DESCRIPTION:

The ideal candidate will be passionate about data-driven decision making and able to do analysis on large unstructured data sets and quickly grasp new technologies. NEED EXCELLENT COMMUNICATION SKILLS

RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Conducts ad hoc data analysis and data quality investigations.
  • Analyze granular data for segment and trend analysis
  • Experience applying regression, classification, and clustering algorithms to varied types of data •
  • Experience with data science languages like R, Python • Experience with building and testing predictive Models


BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Minimum of a Bachelor’s degree in analytical field (Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics); Master’s degree preferred
  • Experience with SPARKS, HADOOP, R, PYTHON
  • Experience working with very large data sets
  • Working knowledge of statistical methodologies a plus
  • Analytical, creative, and innovative approach to solving problems
  • Strong written and verbal communication
  • Positive, people-oriented, and energetic attitude
  • Ability to work in a rapidly changing  and fast paced environment
  • Self-motivated, highly organized, and able to prioritize and manage multiple tasks
  • Ability to establish and maintain strong working relationships and achieve results by working collaboratively with others



We are celebrating over 15 years of service to our national client base for both permanent placement and consulting opportunities. For consulting positions, we offer a variety of benefit options including but not limited to health & dental insurance, paid vacation, timely payment via direct deposit in addition to transferring H1B Visa sponsorship. Please inquire about our referral program if you would like to submit a candidate for any of our open or future job opportunities. We are an EEO employer. We encourage Veterans to apply.
