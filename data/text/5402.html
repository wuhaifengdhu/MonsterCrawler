Industrial Industry Analytics Technical Sales ManagerCandidate MUST be located in the following area's to be eligibleWest Coast, Mountain Time or Texas As the Industrial Analytics Technical Sales Leader, you will be expected to drive the Industrial Market Analytics strategy, evangelize our mission across the client, technical team and with the sales community. You will be asked to lead the growth of intellectual capital and support development of the technical sales specialists via mentoring, coaching or enablement. In this role, you will be part of the global Analytics team providing expertise to our clients including: industry aligned use case and business value development for Big Data and Analytics solutions; solution outline and design of Analytics architectures. You will work closely with the Analytics Sales Manager peers and Analytics Sellers driving Analytics software results. We are looking for experienced Industry Technical Sales Managers that understand the trends, the challenges, and the typical use cases in that Industry as they relate to Analytics. This person will be responsible for ensuring all the Technical Sales Specialists and Analytics Architects aligned to the Industry understand the specific Industry standards, and what types of technologies/approaches would be used to solve common business problems in that Industry. The role requires technical expertise of IBM Analytics (Information Management, Business Analytics, Enterprise Content Management, Cloud, Information Integration, Open Source, Data Science and Machine Learning), with the ability to lead the client and IBM team in developing solutions. Team leadership and interpersonal proficiency are a requirement. Experience in field technical sales is preferred. Criteria for success include growth of IBM Analytics software revenue and signings, establishment of client relationships, client satisfaction, client adoption of IBM strategic platforms, opportunity identification, development of innovative client solutions, and cross-IBM teaming. As an IBM Analytics Technical Sales Manager, you will be a key catalyst for the advancement of analytics client engagements from client exploration to active opportunity to client success. You will spend 40-60% of your time interfacing directly with the clients and IBM field teams. Your knowledge will be essential to deliver new and complex high quality solutions in response to varying business objectives. You will incorporate in-depth knowledge of the Analytics products and solutions in conjunction with industry and business results. You will collaborate on innovative ideas using proven approaches to deliver a winning solution. + At least 3-5 years of experience in Analytics + At least 3-5 years of experience in Industry + At least 1 year experience in performing architectural leadership + At least 1 year experience in lead strategy/design/implementation of solutions + Readiness to travel up to 50% travel annually + English: Fluent Analytic SW Sales
