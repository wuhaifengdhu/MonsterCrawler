Designs, develops and programs methods, processes, and systems to consolidate and analyze unstructured, diverse "big data" sources to generate actionable insights and solutions for client services and product enhancement.

Interacts with product and service teams to identify questions and issues for data analysis and experiments. Develops and codes software programs, algorithms and automated processes to cleanse, integrate and evaluate large datasets from multiple disparate sources. Identifies meaningful insights from large data and metadata sources; interprets and communicates insights and findings from analysis and experiments to product, service, and business managers.

Leading contributor individually and as a team member, providing direction and mentoring to others. Work is non-routine and very complex, involving the application of advanced technical/business skills in area of specialization. 8 years relevant work experience. BS/BA preferred.

Oracle provides a range of Data Science technologies on Oracle Database and Hadoop/Spark platforms, both on-premises and in the Cloud. Technologies include R and SQL language-based analytics as well as a visual pipeline tool supporting analytical workflows. In support of data scientists, the R language and environment enables machine learning-focused customers to leverage this powerful and widely used statistical programming language with Oracle Database, Hadoop, and Oracle engineered systems. The group designs, develops, and tests the software and use case code temlates, engages with internal and external customers to leverage the software in both pre- and post-sales situations, and evangelizes/promotes the use of R through publications and conferences.

Data Scientist position. Oracle is looking for a results-oriented data scientist to work with internal and external customers enabling the use of Oracle machine learning technologies on Big Data problems, and to develop notebook templates illustrating the use of Oracle technology. The Data Scientist will apply and develop techniques for solving statistical and predictive analytics business problems principally with R, SQL, Oracle's R technologies, Hadoop, Hive, MapReduce, Spark, Oracle Database, and Oracle Big Data Appliance.

Required Experience and Skills:

* 6 years advanced analytics experience including applied techniques in data mining, machine learning, and graph mining on big data sets

* Deep knowledge of analytical methodologies (e.g., machine learning, statistical modeling, predictive analytics, information retrieval, graph analytics, etc.)

* 3 years experience with R, other statistical packages a plus (Python, SAS, SPSS, MatLab, etc.)

* Experience with Spark, Hadoop, and Hive

* Experience with relational databases and SQL (Oracle preferred)

* Advanced degree in a quantitative field (e.g., Statistics, Engineering, Mathematics, Physics, etc.)

* Strong verbal and written communication skills, as well as presentation skills

Primary responsibilities:

* Characterize and quantify customer business problems and assess appropriate Oracle technology to address

* Document, implement, test, and benchmark solutions to big data analytics problems

* Use appropriate social media (blogs, tweets, etc.) to promote Oracle machine learning and R technologies

* Work closely with cross-functional teams to design, test, and use new product features
