About the Company

Lincoln Financial Group provides advice and solutions that help empower Americans to take charge of their financial lives with confidence and optimism. Today, more than 17 million customers trust our retirement, insurance and wealth protection expertise to help address their lifestyle, savings and income goals, as well as to guard against long-term care expenses. Headquartered in Radnor, Pennsylvania, Lincoln Financial Group is the marketing name for Lincoln National Corporation (NYSE:LNC) and its affiliates. The company had $222 billion in assets under management as of March 31, 2015.

The Role

The Application Architect, Digital Application Architecture works as part of Digital Business Technology and is responsible for design, technical specifications, and implementation of digital solutions.

Responsibilities

Execution

* Work with business and IT partners to successfully implement projects that advance our digital strategy.

* Stay up to date on the latest industry techniques and capabilities.

* Drive technical designs to consensus and approval by development teams and Enterprise Architecture, providing framework, interfaces and services that can be extended and reused in future strategic initiatives.

* Ensure high-quality output including: project design documents, reference architectures and decision documents.

* Work closely with Enterprise Architecture, Enterprise Data Management and Security Assurance.

* Evaluate vendor software and offerings from a financial, technical and requirements viewpoint. Drive conversations with vendors and participate in proof of concepts.

* Work with project teams to coordinate environment availability and scheduling.

Technical

* Assist in troubleshooting of production issues.

* Identify and mitigate risks in process and technology.

* Assist in defining best practices for development and implementation and refine repeatable practices.

* Enforce architectural and development guidelines and best practices.

* Lead role in providing technical solutions/directions to ensure systems/applications are highly available and meet SLAs.

* Strategic thinking and experience with presenting to varying audience sizes a must.

* Engage IT partners as necessary.

* Project estimation for annual planning and various Tier estimates through the life of the project.

Education

* BS Degree in Computer Science (or equivalent experience)

Experience

* 7+ years Application Development experience

* 5+ years' experience in design and development of Web and Mobile applications

* Experience in, or exposure to the following technologies is required:

J2EE

Javascript, jQuery and AngularJS

HTML 5/CSS

Service Oriented Architecture (SOAP, REST, ESB)

Mobile (ios/android) application development

Relational Databases

Hadoop and Spark big data technologies

Identity Management

Web Portals and Content Management

Environment Configuration and Management

AWS Cloud services

* Capable of creating or reviewing/updating technical design and strategic documentation related to Digital Architecture.

* Excellent communication and articulation skills to engage all stakeholders effectively.

* Ability to evaluate problems from multiple viewpoints and understanding the needs of all parties involved to help drive to a mutually beneficial consensus.

* Data Science and Machine Learning skills and experience

* Knowledge and use of Scikit-Learn, NLTK, NLP, and Deep Learning frameworks (preferred)

* Visualization tools experience using Tableau, Python, and/or R (preferred)

* Feature analysis and engineering skills (preferred) #LI-KM1
