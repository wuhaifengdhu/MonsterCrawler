Anyone who has redeemed a coupon, filled a prescription or returned a product, has touched Inmar. We apply technology and data science to improve outcomes for consumers and those who serve them. As a trusted intermediary for over 35 years, we have unmatched access to billions of consumer and business transactions in real time. Position Summary:

Under the minimal supervision of the Senior Manager, Client Relations , the Client Relations Manager will oversee the day to day service deliverables for assigned clients while ensuring processes and procedures are clearly documented and followed.  The position will ensure delivery of consistent high quality service to clients. The position also provides training to assigned team as needed, manages performance of assigned associates, responds to Clients as appropriate, supervises day-to-day work of service teams and uses six sigma and lean processes to identify process improvement opportunities.

Major Responsibilities/Essential Functions:

  1. Manages day-to-day operations:
  • Implements standard operating procedures and develops required process documentation
  • Ensures clients are properly serviced by assigned team
  • Ensures standard service deliverables are performed as defined by management team
  • Facilitates getting client questions and issues addressed in an accurate and timely manner and assures client satisfaction from a service delivery perspective.
  • Collaborates with other departments as needed to resolve identified issues and develops new procedures/controls when needed
  • Provides weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly reporting to manager to show status of expected service delivery.
  1. Tracks and Monitors performance against expected weekly deliverables and quarterly scorecard metrics:
  • Ensures department metrics are measured and goals are met.
  • Ensures contractual processing commitments are met.
  • Assists with identifying collections and other value added opportunities for our clients.
  • Communicates concerns with manager in a timely manner.

3. Ensures satisfaction and quality of client deliverables:

  • Manages Client projects with assistance from the team in accurate and timely manner.
  • Keeps Senior Manager updated on satisfaction level of clients.
  • Ensures all Client contractual/SLA commitments are met.

4. Leads and develops associates:

  • Ensures staff is properly enabled in their jobs.
  • Assists with analytical and operational issues.
  • Develops associates and addresses performance issues on a timely basis, works closely with Great Teams! in situations warranting formal discipline or termination
  • Conducts regular meetings with direct reports to discuss current performance, future goals, development needs, and ongoing projects

Minimum Requirements:

  • A bachelor’s degree from a four year college or university with a degree in business or related field, or an equivalent combination of experience and training that provides the required knowledge, skills and abilities needed to complete the job responsibilities.
  • Demonstrated ability to manage and lead others.
  • Ability to build productive working relationships internally and externally
  • Demonstrates hands-on mindset and be self-motivated
  • Excellent written, verbal, telephone, presentation and organizational skills
  • Strong/proven skill with all Microsoft Office products (Excel, Word, PowerPoint).
  • Ability to represent the Company in a professional manner to outside Clients, prospects, vendors, and agencies
  • Ability to deal effectively with individuals at all business levels and a variety of situations requiring tact, judgment, and composure.
