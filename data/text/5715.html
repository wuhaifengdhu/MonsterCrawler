* Position Description

Overview of the Position

The Data Science team supports the Claims, Service, andSales operations organization through predictive modeling and operationsresearch.

This role will be responsible for developing text miningrules against large claim and operations datasets in our NLP-based software;projects will span Auto, Property, GL, WC and Disability lines of business. This role will also act as a pioneerin developing similar capabilities for Underwriting and Risk Engineeringthrough text mining new sources such as documents, emails and social media. This role requires close collaborationwith various stakeholders within the enterprise such as data science, smallcommercial, middle market and external data vendors.

We seek candidates with a solid data background as well asstrong analytical skills, communication, interpersonal skills and businessacumen. The ideal candidate will also possessan investigative nature and be extremely detailed oriented. While some technical skills are required, thecandidate should expect more emphasis on the research aspect of the role asopposed to development.

Responsibilities:

The primary responsibilities for the position will includethe following:

* Establishes andmaintains partnerships with internal customers, guiding partners from conceptideation through project completion.

* Collects hypothesisand business requirements, then translates to work plan and executes on workplan.

* Follows a publishedrelease calendar, controlling customer expectations around scope and planningwork such that the end-to-end process is completed within one release cycle.

* Creates corpora foruse in text mining projects; employs working knowledge of SAS, SQL and Oracle.

* Employs knowledge ofnatural language concepts such as named entity recognition, part of speechtagging, parsing, relationship extraction & word sense disambiguation tointeract with NLP software for the creation of text flags from unstructureddata.

* Train data scienceteam on new data assets.

* Sustain focus and pace through extended and repetitive cycles of research, development and rigorous testing to ensure high quality results.

* Support development oftraining materials and perform end-user training to ensure a high degree ofcustomer engagement.

* Partner with IT tooversee implementation of work efforts.

* Support documentation,metadata and data visualization of the assets.

* Participate in thedevelopment of the implementation plan as a subject matter expert.

* Designs, develops andimplements data mining tools and analyses to sift through large amounts of datastored in a data warehouse or data mart to find relationships and patterns.

* Requires in-depthknowledge and experience. Solves complex problems; takes a new perspectiveusing existing solutions. Works independently; receives minimal guidance.

* Acts as a resource forcolleagues with less experience. Represents the level at which career maystabilize for many years or even until retirement

* Experience & Skills

* Bachelor's Degree or higher in Statistics, Business, Computer Science, Actuarial Science, MIS or related field.

* Knowledge of SAS, SQL or other data querying tools.

* 3-5 years of experience in the P&C insurance industry (Actuarial, Underwriting, Product Management, IT)

Competencies

* Strong strategic thinking and exceptional analytical capability

* Strong verbal/written communication skills; Must be able to communicate effectively at all levels across the organization.

* Results-oriented. Demonstrates a sense of urgency and accountability for business issues.

* Customer focused. Builds effective relationships with internal partners, including actuarial, underwriting, operations, and information technology.

* Innovative. Seeks new and creative ways to approach analytical exercises and development projects.

* Strong influencing skills

* The ideal candidate is a technically-minded self-starter who can work well under pressure and balance competing priorities in fast-paced, dynamic work environment.
