About the team and/or client

NBCU TV is one of the world's leading media and entertainment companies. It develops, produces, and markets popular entertainment, news and sports content to a global market, and its business units include NBC, NBC News, NBC Owned Stations, NBC Sports, NBC Sports Network, Golf Channel, USA, Bravo, Oxygen, Esquire, Syfy, MSNBC, CNBC, Chiller, Sprout and E!.

The Role Objective: The Media Supervisor on the NBCU TV team in LA is one of daily interaction with the client, internal planning team members in senior and junior positions alike, and implementation team counterparts.

Job responsibilities include the oversight of plan development and its delivery against KPIs for Television (Local, National, Addressable), Print, Out of Home and Radio. The Supervisor is also responsible for the development and growth of Planners and Assistant Planners by ensuring their exposure to all necessary facets of the planning process. Must have strong familiarity with plan options, areas that have been pursued on behalf of a client's business, and the critical list of areas to explore.

Primary (day-to-day) Responsibilities:

* Develop, execute and present media plans from inception to completion

* Delegate appropriately to Media Planners and Assistant Media Planners to support plan development and execution

* Ensure media plans deliver on client objectives, are strategic, and meet efficiency goals

* Embrace the Channel Neutral approach to media and framework for thinking

* Effectively use and guide team on use of RM tools

* Present plan recommendations to client teams

* Oversee plan negotiations and media team activities

* Ensure plans are executed flawlessly

* Build and maintain a comprehensive media knowledge base and synthesize information into ideas

* Maintain status reports to ensure efficient workflow to meet deadlines and write reports of key meetings

* Act as key liaison with internal implementation teams (Print, Broadcast, OOH) and Digital counterparts to ensure seamless integration of ideas into media plans ensuring deliverables align with overall plan strategies, timelines are met and programs/results are coordinated and presented holistically to client

* Maintain client contact/manage client requests – i.e. plan changes, budget changes, provide marketplace information

* Staff supervision, training and development as well as serving as a positive role model with a strong work ethic and vigorously reviewing all work to ensure high quality standard

Secondary Responsibilities:

* Attend client specific and departmental meetings

* Attend mandatory Agency Training

* Participate in creative brainstorm sessions

* Proactively keep abreast of industry trends and share knowledge with team
