As part of the Marketing team you will be a key player in the Analytics and Data Science team, one of your main responsibilities is to analyze data, find insights, and present your analyses to improve performance.

Here's what you'll do:


* Work with data scientists to create algorithms based on your insights
* Partner with the different marketing teams to deliver in depth analyses and drive performance
* Create visually appealing and strategic dashboards using our Data Warehouse and BI platform
* Analyze visitor level data to measure the impact of the different marketing channels and devices through the consumer decision journey
* Help with all web analytics tracking initiatives using Google Analytics and Omniture

Here's what you'll bring to the table:


* Expert knowledge in turning complex data into actionable insights
* 5-7 years of digital marketing analytics experience
* Strong communication and PowerPoint skills to present to client executives
* Strong background in Google Analytics and other analytics platforms
* High level of comfort analyzing large datasets
* Expert in Excel
* Experience with Data Warehouses and BI Tools (Cognos, Business Objects, etc.)
* Experience using statistical programs (R, Python, SAS, etc.) a plus
* Experience with attribution models a plus
* Bachelor's in Marketing, Finance, Economics, Statistics, or Applied Mathematics
