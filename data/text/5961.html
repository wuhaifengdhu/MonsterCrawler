Snap Inc. is a camera company. We believe that reinventing the camera represents our greatest opportunity to improve the way people live and communicate. Our products empower people to express themselves, live in the moment, learn about the world, and have fun together.

We're looking for a Senior Business Intelligence Analyst on the Financial Planning and Analysis team to join Snap Inc! Working closely with the Engineering and Finance teams, you'll analyze and break down large data sets to identify causes of unexpected changes and trends. Working from our Venice, CA headquarters, you'll collaborate with existing Engineering leads, partners on the Finance team, and colleagues throughout the company to drive new insights and results.

What you'll do:

* Analyze large data sets to identify current and past trends

* Identify trends and anomalies with an exceptional attention to detail

* Build and expand reporting tools that help provide senior management key insights into data and metrics

* Report key metrics regularly

* Develop data visualizations that provide in-depth analysis that will be used at the CEO/CFO level

* Use your knowledge of standard control methodologies to produce auditable results, and serve as a liaison to auditors for these results

* Design and implement new processes while constantly evaluating and refining existing processes

* Analyze complex information and reports to provide accurate and timely recommendations to management, for decision-making purposes

* Prepare presentations for the Board of Directors and Senior Management team, alongside other ad-hoc reporting and analysis

What we're looking for:

* BS in Computer Science, Math, Statistics, Engineering, Operations Research, or related field

* 5+ years experience in data science, business analytics, and business intelligence (BI)

* Experience driving business insights through large and complex data sets

* Someone skilled at gathering requirements and formulating new business reporting metrics for the Senior Management team

* In-depth knowledge of SQL and Excel

* Strong verbal/written communication and data presentation skills

* Ability to effectively communicate with both business and technical teams
