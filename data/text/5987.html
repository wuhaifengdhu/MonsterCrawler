AdColony, the world's premium mobile video ad network and an innovation leader in HD mobile video advertising, is looking for a motivated Analyst to join AdColony's Growth team in Los Angeles. This unique opportunity will focus building data tools that help accelerate revenue growth.

Top candidates will demonstrate a passion for innovation with and driving actionable insights from data. The qualified candidate will have a demonstrated interest or experience in data analysis, business intelligence, marketing, analytics, monetization, and performance advertising, demonstrating a passion for all things quantitative and data driven.

This position will be based in Los Angeles and report to a key stakeholder on our Publishing team. Position may require occasional West Coast travel to San Francisco.

Key Responsibilities

* Combing through massive, real-time datasets to extract meaningful insights that drive revenue growth

* Consistently building and iterating on data tools to enable anyone in the company to access the precise data she needs

* Uncovering new opportunities for product development

* Coordinating with cross-functional teams from Data Science, Product, Business Intelligence, QA, Creative, and Sales to ensure optimal network performance and growth

* Scope of work may also include working on processes that gather and scrub data to improve the accuracy of key reports and perform more refined analysis.

* You will ensure timely and accurate data analysis in support of our company's strategic goals

Desired Skills and Experience

* Bachelor's degree in Math, Statistics, Business, Economics, or a related field preferred

* 2-4 years of relevant experience

* Curious to a fault, never settling for the first answer data may offer

* Exceptional quantitative skills and experience in Excel, or SQL and a solid understanding of coding and logical thinking with regard to relational databases

* Experience with BI and Visualization tools like Qlikview, Microstrategy and Microsoft PowerBI and/or Tableau preferred

* Experience with statistical packages (e.g., SPSS, STATA, etc) and statistics/programming preferred

* Strong project management skills with the ability to handle multiple projects simultaneously and meet tight deadlines in a dynamic environment.

* Understanding of existing mobile advertising and monetization ecosystem

* Passion for mobile technology, iOS and Android apps, gaming, and advertising

* Self-starter, positive attitude, energetic with sense of humor

* Enjoys playing mobile apps/games preferred
