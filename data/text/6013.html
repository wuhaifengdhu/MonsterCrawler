Whisper is excited to hire a summer intern to work directly with our data science team on various machine learning efforts. You will be exposed to everything from natural language processing to computer vision. The internship is a 10 week program where housing will be provided.  This position has potential to become a full time position. Re

Responsibilities

  • Work in a cross-functional team to build machine learning solutions at scale
  • Execute end-to-end experimentation and statistical analysis projects in close collaborations with Product team
  • Define new metrics, methods and techniques using your expertise in statistics and related disciplines
  • Diagnose, develop and maintain complex distributed systems

Qualifications

  • Pursuing a Bachelors, Masters or PhD in Computer Science, Engineering, Statistics, or Math
  • Experience with machine learning techniques and advanced analytics, including regression, classification, clustering, time series
  • Advanced SQL skills and comfortable working with very large data sets
  • Familiarity with distributed systems like Hadoop, HBase, Storm, Kafka, Spark, Redshift, Hive, or Pig
  • Significant experience with a programming language (Python/Ruby/C++/Java)
  • Ability to deep dive into data to understand and apply patterns, while maintaining a sense of the big picture
