We are looking for a Lead Data Scientist to join our Business Intelligence team to help us pull actionable insights out of our data. Our Business Intelligence team serves the data needs of all major business units at Ring, including Product, Marketing, Finance and our Executive team. The BI team is responsible for ensuring the optimal setup for rapid and actionable reporting, insights and analyses.

The candidate must thrive on innovating in a fast-paced environment and be willing to run with projects to completion.

* Measure, report and analyze user, device, app, system, marketing and sales data across multiple data sources and performance segments

* Collaborate with various functional teams to test hypothesis and design/execute experiments to continually be improving our products and strategies

* Work with various business unit leaders to ensure that our systems are meeting their data needs and implementing solutions when necessary

* Help design, import, process and maintain tables in our data warehouse

* Work with database, hardware and software engineers to ensure the integrity of the data in our warehouse

* Work with the BI team to build dashboards, alert systems, and models to drive actionable insights

Requirements

* 3+ years of experience in a Data Scientist role for a technology company

* Exceptional quantitative, analytical and problem solving skills with the ability to draw insights and recommendations from analysis and present proposals to internal stakeholders

* Bachelor's or graduate degree in engineering or other highly technical field (e.g. mathematics statistics, or computer science) and from a top program

* Strong programming capabilities (SQL, Python/R, Kibana/Splunk, Java) and a hunger to learn new languages and frameworks

* Hungry self-starter and active learner with a relentless work ethic who doesn't want or need to be micromanaged

* Experience with AWS data infrastructure and ETL processes (e.g. data pipeline and Spark jobs)

* Experience working directly with database engineers

Nice to have

* Understanding of the unique data challenges that face connected hardware, IoT, e-commerce and/or retail companies

* MS or PhD degree in data science, mathematics statistics, or computer science

About Ring:

Ring's mission is to reduce crime in neighborhoods and empower consumers by creating a Ring of Security around homes and communities with its suite of smart home security products: Ring Video Doorbell, Ring Video Doorbell Pro (HomeKit-enabled), Ring Stick Up Cam and the new Ring Floodlight Cam. With these security devices, Ring has created the neighborhood watch for the digital age and continues to keep homes around the world safe. For more information, visit www.ring.com. With Ring, you're always home.
