Responsibilities

As a Data Scientist in HDR's Predictive Analytics team you will work with a team of Data Scientists, Data Engineers and Data Analysts on a combination of projects for HDR's clients, as a consultant, and on internal analytics initiatives. You will work in a fast paced environment on interesting problems in a wide range of domains including healthcare, marketing, architecture, engineering, remote sensors, education, forecasting, demographics, GIS, network analysis, and many others. You will use your excellent communication skills to present your work to executive level decision makers.

This position will report to the Director of Predictive Analytics and be key member of the Predictive Analytics team.

Additional duties include:

* Data analysis and predictive modeling using state of the art methodology and algorithms;

* Data visualization, dashboard
