Who we want

* Are you seeking a career where you can work in a highly engaged environment and have the autonomy to tackle difficult technical challenges?

* Are you innovative and practical?

* Do your original insights into data infrastructures put you at the forefront of the big data world?

* Can you see big-picture solutions as well as the detailed steps to get there?

* Are you logical and systematic, yet creative, when you approach data collection and problem-solving?

* When you analyze data, do you easily uncover stories that lead to applicable solutions?

* Do you strive for perfection down to the last detail as you develop, test and document your work?

* Are you creative and analytical, with a passion for all things data?

What you will do

Gallup is looking for a data engineer to help us build an effective data infrastructure that will generate insights for Gallup's analytics. In this role, you will design and manage Gallup's big data cloud platform; partner with data scientists to build an infrastructure/pipeline to collect and analyze all necessary data, including social media data sets; build tools for data scientists to access and use the platform; work with Gallup's unique, proprietary data sets; and have the opportunity to analyze large amounts of data and help derive insights and value from them.

What you need

* Bachelor's degree in computer science, mathematics, statistics, data sciences or engineering is required

* Five years of programming language experience required, preferably in Java, R or Python

* Three years of corporate experience designing, building and operating in-production big data/stream processing and/or enterprise data warehouse is required

* Three years of experience using data management tools, including SQL/DBMS and NoSQL is required

* Experience handling data with relational databases is preferred

* Proficiency with HDFS and MapReduce is preferred

* One year of experience with Apache Spark/Storm and NoSQL storage is preferred

* Experience with big data platforms is preferred

* Knowledge of machine learning/distributed systems is preferred
