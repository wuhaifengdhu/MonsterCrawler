Monsanto is seeking a highly motivated individual for our Trait Analytics and Decision Science team under our Biotechnology Trait Testing organization. This position will play a key role in shaping, optimizing, and helping with strategic decisions across emerging technologies. The Data Analytics / Statistical Scientist will engage with multi-disciplinary areas across Technology providing expertise in experiment strategy, design, data analytics and decision making. In particular, they will be responsible for modeling of high-dimensional and multi-layered geo-spatial data, improving process efficiencies, teaching statistical concepts to scientists, and collaborating with IT analytics teams for delivery of enterprise-wide analytical solutions. This position is also expected to identify and implement emerging methodologies that accelerate analytical research objectives in Monsanto Biotech.

Required Education & Skills/Experience:

* Minimum of a Master's Degree in Statistics, Biostatistics, Bioinformatics or a Statistics related discipline

* Background and experience in spatial statistics and modeling of multi-layered geospatial data

* Demonstrated computational skills and experience in R and SAS

* Strong statistical skills in experimental design, general and generalized linear mixed models, and multivariate statistics

* Strong problem solving and critical thinking skills

* Experience presenting results to varied audiences & influencing project direction/strategy

* Ability to actively engage with internal partners & external collaborators to align strategies & success measures in analytics

* Proven consulting skills in a research environment

Desired Education & Skills/Experience:

* PhD in Statistics, Biostatistics, Bioinformatics or a Statistics related discipline

* One or more years of experience after graduate degree

* Background and experience in predictive analytics/machine learning

* Background and experience in image analytics

* Background and experience in computational statistics, computational mathematics, Bayesian statistics, and non-linear mixed models

* Programming skills in MATLAB

* Programming skills in Python

* Knowledge and experience in plant biology, plant breeding, agronomy, and/or soil science

* Knowledge and experience with multi-environment agricultural field trials

Who is Monsanto?

Monsanto is an agriculture company with more than 20,000 employees focused on making a balanced meal accessible to everyone. We work to help farmers produce food in a more sustainable way. We think about how our food is grown so farmers have the tools they need to have better harvests.

We believe that bringing diverse perspectives together is the most effective way to develop creative solutions to some of the world's biggest challenges. Career opportunities at Monsanto range from farming, plant breeding and engineering to marketing, environmental science, IT and many other areas. We're always looking for exceptional professionals interested in developing a strong career while collaborating to develop solutions for sustainable agriculture. Sound like a job for you?
