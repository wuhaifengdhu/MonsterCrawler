Description

Emerson is looking for a Senior Agile Engineer who is passionate and enjoys developing code. We're looking for someone that can code and dive into infrastructure as necessary. This person is responsible for the entire software lifecycle.

Responsibilities

* Assist in all phases of the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC)

* Develop automated UAT and Unit Tests

Technical Qualifications

* Knows at least a couple languages well

* An expert in at least one technical stack

* Could function as a 'go-to' person for several technologies (e.g. Java, PostgreSQL, Ansible, etc…)

* Has some familiarity with operating systems other than their primary

* Comfortable automating some parts of the product

General Qualifications

* Enjoys paired programming

* Comfortable translating business requirements into a design

* Has shipped at least one commercially used software product

* Has participated on several software projects

* Participates in product support (e.g. on call if necessary)

* Has a decent approach between getting the job done vs technical debt

* Understands what agile development is, could teach the ideas

* Can explain and communicate technically complicated and abstract topics to business and product teams effectively

* Willingness to put the needs of the team over their own

* Values collaboration

* Willing to work onsite

* Willingness to experiment with development processes with an open mind

Preferred Qualifications

* Has shipped multiple commercialized products

* Experience in related fields, e.g. customer support or data science

* Experience with a functional programming language

* Has an interest in the business side of things, e.g. product viability and marketing

Work Authorization

No calls or agencies please. Emerson will only employ those who are legally authorized to work in the United States. Emerson will consider candidates that may require sponsorship for work authorization in the USA.
