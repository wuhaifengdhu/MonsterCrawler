The Aggregation Operations Specialist will be responsible for coordinating the implementation and ongoing maintenance of AEP Energys municipal aggregation programs. These responsibilities include requesting and acquiring needed data from utilities, coordinating data processing with internal and external stakeholders, coordinating marketing materials and legal approvals with internal stakeholders and assisting communities and brokers throughout the governance process.

Key Duties & Responsibilities:

Ongoing Activities

* Monitor PUC websites and other news outlets for current and relevant information related to municipal aggregations.

* Foster positive relations with community leaders and brokers to build positive relationships and build a robust lead funnel.

* Support/backup the Director, as needed, at community/social functions.

* Complete post-sale win/loss analysis.

Pre-Sale Functions

* Review RFPs and Plan of Operation and Governance documents sent from brokers and Community Partners.

* Create deal summary sheets for upcoming new/renewal municipal aggregations, including refreshes.

* Conduct deal review sessions with internal stakeholders and draw consensus around timelines necessary to achieve agreed upon deliverables.

* Obtain needed account and usage data from utilities.

* Arrange for the utility files to be sent over to the Customer Data Sciences team.

* Interact from time to time with internal pricing team as it relates to deal inputs and changes.

Post-Sale Execution

* Create and formalize a process for municipal aggregations that is comprehensive, anticipates unique needs and leads to flawless execution.

* Serve as the internal point-person for closed municipal aggregation deals.

* Involve internal stakeholders about key elements of the deal and lead the process as referenced above.

* Coordinate with Marketing, Legal, Operations, and other stakeholders to see the aggregation process through to full execution, ensuring Director is appropriately informed.

* Communicate with brokers and community leaders about the deal status throughout the deal execution phase.

Knowledge & Skill Requirements:

* Quick learner who grasps concepts and processes quickly and intuitively.

* Ability to communicate information (email & verbally) accurately, professionally and clearly with an authentically enthusiastic cadence.

* Highly organized.

* Consistently prioritize/reprioritize multiple deliverables as needed.

* Good project management skills

* Good composition skills-- ability to compose a grammatically correct, concise, and accurate written response.

* Work successfully in a team environment as well as independently.

* Ability to learn and utilize propriety software in order to perform day to day duties.

* Must have a high level of integrity and confidentiality as well as the highest caliber of judgment.

* Strong communication skills with the ability to listen and express ideas and thoughts effectively.

* Goal oriented with a passion for success.

Education & Experience Requirements:

* Bachelors Degree from 4 year accredited university required.

* This position is located Columbus, Ohio

* Advanced proficiency in MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Project and Dynamics.

* Energy experience a plus.

Attributes:

* Embodies AEP Energy values.

* Entrepreneurial drive and high level of initiative.

* Presents and maintains a positive attitude.

* Strong business acumen.

* Enthusiastic learner, eager to meet challenges and quickly assimilate new concepts.

* Displays confidence in ability and performance.

* High EQ-- superior interpersonal skills, diplomacy, win-win mind-set, relationship-builder, able to work with diverse group of colleagues.

* Able to bear high levels of responsibility: making decisions, being accountable.

* Communicates honestly, openly, and consistently. Shares information appropriately and involves internal stakeholders in solving problems.

* Able to evaluate information quickly, identify key issues and formulate conclusions based on sound, practical judgment, experience, and common sense.

* Proactive thinking. Ability to foresee outcomes and plan against possible shortcomings.

* Understand the strategic business drivers and able to map market trends.

* Excellent communication skills written, spoken, presentation and listening with ability to explain and interpret complex issues clearly, mindful of audience.

* Well organized; ability to prioritize, plan proactively and execute properly while managing conflicting priorities and multi-task effectively.

* Innovative and resourceful.

* Acts with a sense of urgency.

* Ability to handle stress in a professional and productive manner.
