Job Description

BI is one of the leading technology companies in Colorado and the largest provider of innovative electronic monitoring solutions in the United States. We excel at providing technological solutions to over 1,000 agencies nationwide and allowing those agencies to fulfill their missions without worrying about the technical complexities.

About the Role

As the expert in all things security you will work directly with the entire technology team to ensure BI retains a solid and secure environment. You#8217;ll be the one who incorporates industry best practices into all that we do while maintaining your expertise through hands-on execution. In this role you will also work directly with our key customers including the Department of Homeland Security and a wide variety of State and Local government agencies.

What You#8217;ll Be Doing

  • Developing, implementing, and overseeing policies, procedures and associated plans for system security administration and system access based on industry standard best practices.
  • Deploying, managing, and maintaining network security systems and their corresponding or associated software, including firewalls, intrusion detection systems, cryptography systems, and anti-virus software.
  • Troubleshooting problems with network and security infrastructure and taking corrective actions as necessary.
  • Overseeing testing of systems in order to identify system vulnerabilities using internal tools or in conjunction with outside vendors.
  • Working hand-in-hand with System Administration to monitor logs and network traffic for unusual or suspicious activity, interpreting any threats, and taking steps to rectify the situation.
  • Continually assessing the state of the environment and leading the team in any changes needed.
  • Providing top level expertise and guidance to other team members in the resolution of issues on individual PC#8217;s or servers.
  • Keeping current with emerging security alerts and trends ensuring that BI is able to respond quickly and appropriately.
  • Using electronic tools to document all aspects of our network systems, allowing your fellow engineers and architects to work within the boundaries defined.
  • Proving your expertise as a central figure in planning and implementing new or upgraded hardware and software systems.#160;

What You#8217;ll Bring to this Position

  • Bachelor#8217;s Degree in Information Systems, Computer Science or related field or equivalent experience (security related certifications are a bonus!).
  • Four to five years of experience in the following areas:
    • Computer networking and IT security
    • Computer systems administration on Windows operating systems including Windows 2012/2016, Windows 7 and 10.#160; Knowledge of Linux a plus.
    • PC/server and hardware configuration and maintenance.
    • Jupiter Netscreen firewall and VPN security appliances.
    • Sonicwall SSL VPN and/or IPSEC VPN experience
    • Enterasys and Dell Networking Equipment
  • In depth knowledge of network and security principles, systems, and hardware.
  • In depth knowledge of SSL and encryption and secure communications, DNS systems and the ability to manage both external and internal DNS, UNIX/Linux, Windows - including 2008/2012/2016 server, Microsoft SQL Server (2012 and higher), heterogeneous routing hardware, networking and security equipment from vendors including Dell, Cisco, Enterasys, and Sonicwall, TCP/IP, and client server technologies.
  • Ability to communicate technical issues to all levels of management and diverse internal and external contacts at all levels in comprehensible terms.
  • Ability to share knowledge and experience and to provide helpful advice and mentoring to other team members.
  • Observational skills to validate data obtained via log files and network scanning and come to reasonable conclusions.
  • Ability to quickly diagnose and resolve complex hardware and software conflicts and issues in a 24 x 7 uptime environment.
