We strive for every interaction to be driven by our CORE values: Insightful Expertise, Integrity is Everything, Service Excellence, Inspiring Innovation, Each Person Counts, Results-Driven, & Social Responsibility. 

  

This Chief Architect will be responsible for the following: 

  


JOB RESPONSIBILITIES
* Strong technical leadership, including hands-on approach, covering the entire pipeline from architecture and design to deployment, security and performance
* Work with executive leadership and solution architects for alignment of business and technology objectives
* Provide architectural direction to Enterprise Architects, Business Architects and Solution Architects
* Define standards and best practices across the organization
* Ensure architectural integrity and consistency across the entire product
* Develop and maintain an aspirational architectural road map for the enterprise
* Research and recommend emerging technologies in support of business needs 

  


Required Skills: 

  

* Java enterprise technology stack
* Databases such as Oracle or NoSQL
* Application servers or Servlet Containers like WebSphere, Tomcat
* Spring, Spring Boot, ReactJS or Angular JS, Node.js
* In-memory databases - e.g. Gemfire, Redis, Couchbase, XAP or similar platforms
* Containerization and cluster compute tools like Docker, UCP, Compose, Kube, and Marathon 
* Public cloud platforms - AWS or Azure
* Code quality tools like Sonar, BlackDuck, Blue Optima, and FindBug
* Source control tools -- SVN or Bitbucket/GITHub 

  

Required Qualifications: 

  

Bachelor or Master's Degree in Computer Science
Strong hands on experience and Implementation knowledge of modern WEB 2.0 applications
