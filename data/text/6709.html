Job Description

Title: Data Mining Lead

Location: Pennington, NJ - (Lawrenceville, NJ or Hopewell, NJ)

Duration: 6 Months plus

Candidate must be willing to be on site Lawrenceville, NJ or Hopewell, NJ

Description:

  • Scientific data mining consultant is needed for translation medicine analytics capabilities.
  • Use existing clinical and preclinical datasets to answer key exploratory questions that will support external interactions (publications, KOL/Prescriber interactions etc.) and internal decision-making (study design and new asset positioning) for an asset.
  • To better understand and define what is needed in order to mine integrated datasets across trials and programs routinely and in a timely manner.
  • To demonstrate/evaluate the value of such a cross-disciplinary data mining capability.

Responsibilities – Data Mining lead

  • Work with physicians, scientists, stats programmers, statisticians and the data mining team to identify patient cohorts that may be required for a particular analysis.
  • Perform data filtering, querying and integration on SAS and R datasets across trials.
  • Formulate analysis strategy and seek stakeholder engagement at appropriate time during the analysis cycle.
  • Perform analyses (simple charts to multivariate analysis, supervised/unsupervised) to generate insights from integrated datasets.
  • Deliver insights in a presentable format to senior stakeholders on the drug project team.
  • Support the advanced analytics lead with creation and maintenance of analytics and decision logs pertaining to various data mining activities on the product across the organization.
  • Assist with facilitation of biweekly quantitative network forum meetings.


Qualifications

Skills Required

  • Expertise in integration and multivariate analysis of clinical trial datasets required.
  • Advanced expertise in R or SAS is a must
  • Proficiency in Spotfire, MATLAB or similar tools preferred.
  • Scientific training and prior experience in Cardiovascular and Oncology disease areas preferred.
  • Ability to analyze biomarker (IHC, Imaging, flow cytometry, NGS) datasets desirable.
  • Excellent interpersonal, communication and reporting skills expected.
  • Candidate must be willing to be on site Lawrenceville or Hopewell
  • Master's degree in Statistics or related fields.
  • 6-10 yrs experience in Statistical Analysis
  • 6-10 yrs experience in data science


Additional Information

  • Candidate must be willing to be on site Lawrenceville or Hopewell
