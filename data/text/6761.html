Job Description

The Senior Data Engineer will serve as a key resource in building andexecuting on Chubb's Advanced Analytics Strategy. This role is in direct alignment with theChubb goal of continuing to act as an Information-Driven organization throughthe use of advanced analytics to provide business insights.

This role directly supports the goal of becoming anInformation-Driven organization by aligning analytics with business informationneeds. By answering core businessquestions through advanced analytics techniques such as data mining, predictivemodelling, pattern detection, graphical or statistical analysis, as well as byutilizing innovative solutions and capabilities for rapid delivery of businessinsights.

* Full stack engineering experience across AWS,Azure and/or Google Cloud technologies

* Experienced in testing and validation of datasystems

* Ability to quickly assimilate to new softwarepackages (both open source and off the shelf) in the data and analytics domain

Responsibilities
