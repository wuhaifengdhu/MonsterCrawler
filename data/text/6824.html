Overview:

* A challenging environment and opportunity to work with a diverse array of Fortune 500 companies in clinical life sciences projects.

* Chance to work with relevant and cutting-edge technologies to analyze and solve our customer's complex business problems with extremely passionate team members

* A growing company with a culture that highly values using a disciplined approach to take calculated risks, executes with speed, and treats employees and customers with integrity

Responsibilities:

Consulting Leadership

* Excellent communication skills to interact with business leaders

* Expert level Program management experience, managing multiple business and IT facing workstreams

* Very strong communications and proficient at presentations, leading workshops and executive facilitation. Proficiency at Powerpoint.

* Very strong problem solving and analytical skills, able to structure complex questions and lead workstreams to deliver business insights

* Responsible for actively managing project communication, project risks, and team dynamics

* Strong leadership abilities, confidence and ability to work with a client and Saama teams (very important) to deliver value to clients

* Leadership managing all phases of program / project delivery such as requirements analysis, design/build, quality assurance, user acceptance testing, and release management

* Business facilitation for data and metrics definition, data ownership and governance

* Provide support in setting strategies, process design and workflow.

Program and Project Management

* Accountable for the overall planning, tracking, and execution of an Enterprise DW/BI program using an iterative, multi-phase approach

* Leads and directs project teams to meet project objectives. Reviews, evaluates, baselines and executes project plans, schedules and budgets

* Work closely with internal and external project teams to support the estimation and delivery of reporting requirements, data architecture and design solutions

* Collaborate with Business and IT leadership teams to ensure that the delivered solution meets agreed upon requirements

* Identifies and negotiates schedules, milestones and resource required to meet project objectives and success criteria

Managing Clients

* Responsible for monitoring and increasing the satisfaction scores across all relevant stakeholders and sponsors within their accounts.

* Drives periodic surveys and discussion groups designed to garner feedback and input as to Saama's perceived value and contribution to these organizations

* Collaborates proactively with the delivery organization to ensure services are being executed up to the client's expectations and represents the client's interests on all project deliverables.

* Provides continual feedback to the delivery organization regarding client sentiment as well as specific feedback on all Saama consultants who are engaged within these accounts.

Qualifications:

* Bachelor's degree or equivalent and at least 10 years of experience required; Masters degree preferred

* Life Sciences Clinical Operations domain knowledge

* Strong knowledge and working experience on clinical trial process, data and systems is required.

* Understanding of clinical operations metrics, risk based management and drug lifecycle

* Experience working with and understanding of the stakeholders involved in clinical development, including working with them to optimize their operations using targeted clinical operations reporting and analytics solutions

* Experience working with and owning relationships with senior clients at large corporations is a must

* Proven experience in managing business intelligence and analytics project delivery

* Experience with data governance, business data and metrics definition is a big plus

* Understanding of statistics and data science is a plus

* Travel to client sites of up to 50% may be expected.
