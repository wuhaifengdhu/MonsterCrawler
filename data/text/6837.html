Job Title: Senior Business Insights and Data Analyst

Reports to:  Global Director, Sales/Revenue Management

This position is responsible for supporting a global analytics capability and reports into the Global Director of IGS Revenue Management. The Senior Business Insights and Data Analyst is part of a core team that provides global analytics support focused on optimal pricing, mix management and trade effectiveness across multiple geographies, categories and customers. This individual will contribute to best-in-class analytic models and practices. They will be responsible for contributing to and implementing a cohesive advanced analytics plan that captures multiple aspects of technology, people capability and process excellence. This individual must be a strong communicator and passionate about generating actionable, insightful analytics that drive business value.

Responsibilities:

  • Contribute to strategic pricing plans to deliver financial and marketplace objectives.
  • Develop insights that contribute to trade strategy to drive effectiveness of portfolio price architecture.
  • Find game changing insights through rigorous quantitative analysis facilitated by accurate experimental design.
  • Create and deliver advanced predictive analytics vision that will advance our revenue management capabilities
  • Manage priorities of revenue management / analytics HUB; ensure accurate and timely delivery of business unit needs and analytics
  • Conduct analysis around price positioning and target price gaps, channel, pack size relationships and product portfolio roles to optimize returns.
  • Proactively identify, prioritize, and conduct analysis that drives pricing insights.
  • Join together large, disparate data sets to uncover actionable insights.
  • Promote and evangelize a culture of excellence, data driven discussions, healthy skepticism, intellectual curiosity, knowledge sharing and teamwork.
  • Contribute to effective presentations and selling stories to influence business results (succinct documents summarizing findings / insights and action).
  • Assist the implementation of process improvements that significantly improve quality across the team, department and/or business unit for area(s) of responsibility.
  • Keeps abreast of new analytics approaches, ideas and technologies in external environment and brings in appropriate analytic / data science innovation into Campbell’s IGS
  • Works on complex assignments, projects, and/or initiatives to build and enhance the capability of his/her assigned area(s) of responsibility.
  • Manage multiple complex analytics projects, manage priorities to deliver timely results.

Education:

4 Year/Bachelor’s Degree preferred concentration in Statistics, Psychology, Engineering, Computer Science,Mathematics, Marketing, Economics, or Business Administration. Master’s Degree is strongly preferred or relevant certification (i.e. Udacity, Edx, Coursera)

Experience: 4 Years of Experience

  • 2 Years of driving insights from data, experience with tools and concepts related to data and analytics, such as dimensional modeling, ETL, reporting tools, data governance, data warehousing, structured and unstructured data.
  • Knowledge of CPG industry, customers and marketplace pricing and trade dynamics
  • Time management skills, ability to work multiple projects in parallel
  • 2 Years of Experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau, D3.js, Qlikview, etc.
  • Experience utilizing predictive models and machine learning algorithms to drive actionable business insight.
  • Experience using advanced statistical software (Alteryx, SPSS Modeler, SAS/STAT, SAS/MACRO, E-Miner)
  • Experience in SQL is a plus
  • Advanced Experience in data mining and data wrangling tools
  • Skill in identifying data issues and anomalies during analysis
  • Demonstrated creativity in identifying the "so what" from multiple and disparate sources of information
  • Passion for learning (new problem domains, algorithms, tools etc) and for analyzing data
  • Ability to grasp complex analytic principles and techniques
  • Strong business acumen & demonstrated aptitude for analytics that incite action
  • Strong problem solving and critical thinking skills
  • Effective communication skills ability to "tell the story"
  • Ability to work effectively across functions, disciplines and levels
