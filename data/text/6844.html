In this role, you will be responsible for building big data platform using a combination of integration framework and Big Data processing technologies. 8+ years of experience is required.

B.S or M.S in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics or equivalent experience.

Demonstrated expertise designing and coding high performance big data applications and servicesProficiency in Hadoop, Kafka, Spark, Hbase and No SQL databases in a large scale environmentExperience administering Big data eco system on HDP or CDHExperience implementing failover architectureEffectively performs - ETL on large data volumes, requirements analysis, data profiling Demonstrated expertise with software architecture & design, data structures and algorithmsExperience in programming scripting languages such as Scala, Java, PythonStrong aptitude for learning new technologies related to Data Management and Data Science.Familiarity with BI software platforms like MicroStrategy or Tableau is a big plusDemonstrated ability to work well independently and within a fast-paced, team-oriented environmentKnowledge in engineering machine learning, feature engineering systems is a plus.Excellent debugging, critical thinking and communication skills

Apple is seeking a technical leader in Big Data to join the GDCS (Global Data Center Services) system engineering team. In this role, you will be responsible for building foundational big data platform using a combination of integration framework and Big Data processing technologies. You will also be responsible for developing some of the key components of the framework, collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements and mentor junior members in the team. As a senior member of the team, you will be expected to take ownership of the entire platform components and help set the vision and architecture for it. In the process, you will identify the requirements of new features and propose design and drive the solution.Candidates should have a proven track record of working with engineering teams to communicate data-driven recommendations and the ability to hit the ground running are key to success in this fast-moving and collaborative environment.
