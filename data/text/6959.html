Responsibilities

We are seeking a Sr. Manager to lead a team overseeing our Omni Channel Capability Portfolio.

The Sr. Manager will lead a team responsible for developing and managing the life cycle of digital features and tools including experience strategy, requirements gathering, cost/benefits analysis, scoping, development, roll-out, reporting, optimization & continuous innovation.

Specific product focus areas may include:

  • In Store Pickup
  • Same Day Delivery
  • Store Locator, etc.

The Sr. Manager will set the goals and provide direction to Product Manager who will utilize available data, insights, and voice of the customer/store associates to make informed decisions about product roadmaps and their prioritization. He/she will keep abreast of evolving industry tools and technologies that enhance the website, inside sales & field sales experience to better serve Verizon Wireless’s customers. Given the highly strategic and cross-channel nature of this role, the successful individual must possess a unique combination of structured strategic thinking, passion, and curiosity for technology, as well as strong leadership and collaboration skills.

Responsibilities

Core responsibilities include:

  • Lead a team of 6
  • Own product strategy, roadmap, and delivery
  • Analysis of internal & external customer needs
  • Interpret customer performance metrics and the financial implication
  • Develop the near, mid and long-term holistic roadmap of initiatives aligned to business objectives
  • Research and analyze the key performance indicators across key traffic sources with the goal of maximizing consumer engagement and enhancing the user experience for all of Post Transaction.
  • Monitor key conversion metrics and a/b tests to support continual optimization of the product and business plan
  • Assessment of build vs. partner options with 3rd parties including the industry trends
  • Execution of the in-house or 3rd party vendor solutions including running of various tests/pilots through A/B testing
  • Focus on product performance, data insights, optimization and continuous innovation
  • Help define key performance indicators for each product/tool/capability
  • Develop business case to justify the prioritization and benefits
  • Track and report on the benefits/impact after go-live
  • Drive broad organizational engagement to deliver products on-time and on budget
  • Closely work with merchandizing teams to ensure product reviews platform delivers optimal results for all internal stakeholders
  • Energetic, passionate, and inspired leadership
  • An active networker partnering with cross-functional teams across our lines of business
  • Manage and understand key online analytics
  • Help define key performance indicators (financial and customer)
  • Measure and report on the efficacy of initiatives and facilitate action plans accordingly
  • Identify and prioritize opportunities to improve the user experience leveraging all available insights

Qualifications

Must have:

  • Bachelor’s degree or six or more years of work experience.
  • Six or more years of relevant work experience.
  • eCommerce experience

Ideally, you’ll also have:

  • MBA
  • Six  or more years of eCommerce experience driving top line strategies for large websites including lead
  • Five or more years of  experience in two or more of the following areas:
    • Product management
    • Management/Technology Consulting
    • eCommerce strategy
    • Data science/analytics
    • Project management
  • Six or more years of experience leading high performing teams with proven results.
  • Experience partnering with technical teams to deliver results.
  • Strong strategic, analytical and critical thinking skills; ability to break down complex problems and formulate plans.
  • Customer-centric orientation with proven track-record of developing and acting on customer insights.
  • Strong organizational and project management with attention to detail (must be excellent at excel, power-point).
  • Proven ability to collaborate broadly with flexible, positive attitude and ability to get results.
  • Exceptional oral and written communication skills; ability to communicate to the senior executives.
