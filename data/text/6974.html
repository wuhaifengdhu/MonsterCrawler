Data Science Faculty at Sollers

Menlo Park, NJ 08837

Report this job

About the Job

Sollers invites applications for a Fulltime/ Part-time Faculty position from individuals with expertise in:

• Statistics
• R Programming
• Python Programming
• SAS
• Machine Learning
• Data Mining
• Big Data

The successful candidate will contribute to the Department’s expansion of research and educational initiatives in Data Science.

The candidate will serve as core faculty in the Sollers’ professional graduate and certificate programs and be responsible for training candidates. Applicants should hold advanced degrees in the related discipline. Preference will be given to candidates with five or more years of industry experience in related fields.

Applicants should possess excellent written and communication skills as evidenced by peer-reviewed publications, teaching, and/or presentations at professional meetings.

Please reach out to Program Director Deepa S. at deepas@sollers.edu
