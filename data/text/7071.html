About the Job

Job Title: Bench Scientist - L1



Where: King of Prussia, PA



Contract: 1 year



Position Description




• The position plays a central role in supporting the development of products by generating, interpreting, and summarizing physical properties data.
• These data packages are key to support portfolio progression from early to late phase project development including regulatory contribution to files.
• This position will be laboratory focused, and will require the ability to run a broad range of instrumentation, a subset of which is GMP.


Position Requirements




• Bachelor's or graduate degree in chemistry, pharmaceutical sciences or a related field
• Knowledge and experience in performing laboratory procedures with Standard
• Operating Procedures and in compliance with global pharmaceutical regulations and guidelines.
• Has thorough knowledge of regulatory requirements and drug development process.
• Plan, design and execute work/experimental programmes to further the course of a development project and enable key decisions.
• Proven strong written and oral communication skills.
• Interpersonal and teamwork skills as demonstrated by ability to interact with technical staff, investigators and management to accomplish department objectives.
• General knowledge and experience in pharmaceutical solid state /physical properties analysis including the areas of powder X-Ray diffraction, particle sizing, thermal analysis, microscopy, and surface characterization.
• Work seamlessly with other groups within Product Development as needed.
• Experience in analytical method development, validation and transfer in a QbD environment.
• Accurately record laboratory results within quality guidelines.
• Knowledge and experience in laboratory safety and compliance, occupational health and environmental protection policies and regulations.
• High quality standards in work performance with attention to detail, accuracy, consistency, and laboratory efficiency.
• Demonstrates high standards of personal integrity and performance.
• Responsible for following safety and quality standards within the team.
• Responsible for coordinating the maintenance and service of some instruments.


?Preferred Qualifications




• 3 years industry experience
• Training or experience in one or more specific areas of solid state /physical properties analysis such as diffraction analysis, thermal analysis, surface characterization etc.
• Experience with statistical analysis including design of experiments (DOE).


Benefits




• Medical, Dental, and Vision Insurance
• 401K Plan with Company Match
• Life and AD&D Insurance
