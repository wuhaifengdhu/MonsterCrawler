Duties

Asan IT Program Manager (Data Strategist) within theUSCIS Office of the Chief Information Officer,you will perform the following duties:

* Serveas a leader for USCIS efforts in stewarding the nation's immigration data andproviding tools and support for analysis of data while overseeing OIT's datascience activities and build a culture of technical excellence and analyticrigor around the use of data science principles.

* UtilizeR, Python, and other comparable tools, along with Lean and Agile methodologies,to integrate and cross-reference USCIS data, create data product for use byagency data scientists, select and make available data science tools and helpthe agency understand and make use of its data.

* Provideinsight, advice and technical knowledge to executives and provide counsel tooffice chief and staff on risks, gains and issues on planned actions.

* Representsenior-level officials at departmental policy and planning councils andmultiple IT discipline working groups, leading IT initiatives relating to dataacross the DHS community.

* Act as representative and evangelist for OIT ideas ondata science and analytics, helping to establish USCIS as a leader in thisfield and as an employer of choice for potential employees.
