Eagle Ray is searching for Big Data Science and Analytics Engineers with strong Java programing and data analysis experience to support our analytics and threat modeling projects for its DHS and law enforcement customers.

Our analytics solutions provide our customers with actionable knowledge, insight and intelligence necessary to improve their situational awareness, strengthen investigative capabilities, and support policy enforcement activities through the delivery of high-quality, data-driven quantitative results.

The right candidates will be self-motivated, flexible, excel in a high-pressured, tight-deadline environment and can work well in a collaborative team environment. Candidates MUST be U.S. Citizens and DHS/CBP Public Trust-eligible or already possess a public trust clearance. If you think you have the background, skills, and experience in these areas and you are interested in joining our team, please send your resume.


  • Leads, performs and assists in design, coding, development and testing of operational predictive analytics models that solve complex business problems
  • Performs data collection, organization, advanced statistical analysis and predictive solutions, text and data mining
  • Employs software development (coding) and testing standards and best practices
  • Collaborates with other technical team members on design, architecture and deployment of new features, feature enhancements, and general improvements to knowledge-engineered rules and predictive models
  • Develops plans, system requirements and program/technical specifications
  • Provides technical direction to other programmers and engineers.

Required Experience:

  • Substantive programming/development proficiency with Java (J2EE, JavaScript) and object-oriented programming languages and methods
  • Experience working in an Agile/Scrum software development environment
  • Experience with both relational (e.g., Oracle, mySQL, Postgres) as well as NoSQL (e.g., MongoDB, CouchDB, Cassandra, etc.) database technologies and environments
  • Experience with big data processing, such as Hadoop, HBase, PIG, etc.
  • Experience working with version control tools and environments (e.g., Git, Mercurial, Subversion, etc.)
  • Ability to work effectively both independently as well as part of a team environment

Desired Experience:

  • Broad knowledge of and real-word implementation experience with Homeland and National Security threats
  • Experience with analytics frameworks and statistical software packages such as Weka, Lucene, UIMA, OpenNLP, Mahout, R, SPSS, SAS, etc.
