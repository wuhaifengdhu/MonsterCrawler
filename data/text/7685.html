We are currently looking for a Big Data Architect to join one of our federal engagements. Responsibilities will include:

* Architect, design, prototype, and implement Big Data analytic solutions on a Hadoop–based/NoSQL platform.

* Leverage a broad set of technology skills to provide thought leadership, solution architecting and implementation guidance for robust Big Data solutions, and to grow big data skills and capabilities.

* Create data processing pipelines and assist with data mining / machine learning algorithms to provide understanding of patterns, behavior, insights, and relationships within the data leading to greater depth of knowledge.

* Research, experiment, and utilize leading Big Data methodologies and frameworks to refine data ingestion process focused on unstructured and semi-structured data.

* Deliver proof of concept execution to prove the value of Big Data use cases.

* Perform full lifecycle implementation from requirements analysis, platform selection, technical architecture design, application design and development, testing, and deployment

Required Qualifications:

* BS in Computer Science or related field

* 10+ years of consulting and software development experience including 3+ years of in a combination of relevant Big Data/Analytics areas

* Proven ability to leverage and integrate technology stacks into an overall solution architecture in support of business requirements and use cases

* Hands-on experience with a variety of platforms, languages, frameworks, modeling, and methodologies

* Demonstrate knowledge of data architecture, data warehousing, advanced analytic, or data science implementations.

* Experience presenting technical concepts and solutions (including whitepapers) to business and technology stakeholders.

Technology Skills:

* Experience with Hadoop distributions - MapR, Cloudera (CDH), or Hortonworks (HDP/HDF)

* Hands-on experience with the Hadoop ecosystem (e.g. Hive, Pig, Sqoop, Flume and/or HBase)

* Hands-on experience with analytical tools, languages, or libraries (e.g. SAS, SPSS, R, Pandas, TensorFlow)

* Hands-on experience with real time or micro-batching processing engine (Spark, Storm, Kafka, Flink, etc.)

* Knowledge of NoSQL platforms (e.g. key-value stores, graph or document based databases, RDF triple stores)

* Knowledge of cloud computing infrastructure (e.g. AWS, Azure, GCP)

* Previous experience with high-scale or distributed RDBMS (e.g. Redshift, Snowflake, BigQuery)

* Proficient in shell scripting and object oriented programming languages - Java, Scala, Python
