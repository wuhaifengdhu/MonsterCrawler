About the Job

Director of Research and Analytics

We are assisting a client organization in their search for a Director of Research & Analytics.  This position i s r esponsible for leading and managing the research department of a dynamic organization to support the information, data and analysis needs of the company and its customers . This position is directly responsible for the continued development , growth and operation of the company’s research function and collaborates with senior leaders to identify new areas in which research and analytics can better serve clients, enhance company operations, and contribute to organizational growth. The position also serves as subject matter expert on statisti cal methodology, economic model ing, and data science.

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Direct all aspects of the research function including administrative management and budgeting, research design and implementation, analysis, and communication of findings .
  • Oversee the design and implementation of research projects and data analysis in areas that align with customer objectives, the strategic priorities of the organization , and departmental needs.
  • Research and analyze a wide range of issues and prepare reports on these topics with focus on strategy and strengthening the organization’s credibility with external audiences.
  • Manage large data sets, analyze data, and translate findings for broad audiences with reports and other tools.
  • Support the data, information, and research needs of internal and external clients .
  • Verify accuracy of data and sources for all publications, reports, and presentations to ensure clarity and objectivity.
  • Develop and present oral, written and visual summaries of analyses focused on implications of key findings for practice and action .
  • Collaborate with staff leaders in all departments to communicate trends, issues and research findings that impact their work.
  • Provide leadership for the continued development and growth of the research department, including identification of short and long term goals, priorities, implementation timelines and resource requirements.
  • W ork collaboratively with department leaders to advance the organization’s mission and key initiat ives through data and analysis.
  • Monitor the research services marketplace to identify the company ’s current position , assess advantages or disadvantages and develop strategic solutions to e nhance the company’s competitiveness .

Qualifications

  • Master’s degree in economics, planning, geography, social science, or related field
  • Minimum ten years of related work experience with increasing responsibilities
  • Demonstrated success in executing qualitative and quantitative research
  • Strong analytical and quantitative analysis skills and attention to detail
  • Ability to identify viable business opportunities related to research offerings and build long-term relationships with prospects and clients in order to increase company revenue
  • S trong communication skills with ability to present, assert , influence and speak to different stakeholders , using tact and diplomacy
  • Evidence of effective team building , conflict management and leadership skills to foster a collaborative work environment
  • Proven leadership in administrative /project management, budgetary responsibility and coordinating interdisciplinary research activities
  • Disciplined, accountable, consensus builder and passionate leader
  • Quick learner who is meticulously detail-oriented, with excellent writing skills and ability to produce high-quality work
  • Self-starter who takes ownership and drives activities to completion, organizes time and priorities to meet deadlines
  • Ability to travel as required

This is an excellent opportunity to advance your career and work with a great team on interesting and challenging projects!

We offer very competitive pay and outstanding benefits.
