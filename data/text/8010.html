Job Description Pizza Hut Enterprise Data and Business Intelligence team is looking for a motivated, creative and enthusiastic technology professional to join our team as an Enterprise Data Architect. In this highly pivotal role, the ideal candidate will develop a solid understanding of Pizza Hut's business strategies and help lead the transformation of our data management and business intelligence capabilities to position Pizza Hut for growth. He/she must have a proven track record of working with complex inter-related systems across digital and retail, and bringing that content together on integrated data platforms. The individual will possess end-to-end vision of the enterprise data strategy, provide thought leadership to a diverse team and take a hands-on approach to drive the modernization of the architecture and design of the data platform, including traditional and modern Data stores, MDM and analytical/reporting capabilities.

This position will have proven collaboration and negotiation skills, and will consult with multiple business teams across Digital, Data Science, Finance and Operations as well as with technology development leaders to deliver key business objectives. They will understand how data flows across the enterprise and how businesses extract the most value from the data in the most effective way. The ability to demonstrate strong leadership and influencing skills in this setting will position this candidate for future career growth across Pizza Hut.

Minimum Requirements

* 12+ years implementing enterprise data warehousing, data integration, reporting & analytical solutions in a complex environment across a variety of data platforms, including but not limited to, traditional and modern data architectures deployed on premised or in the cloud

* 5+ years hands on experience working as a Solution Architect with depth in data integration and data architectures (conceptual, logical, physical & dimensional models)

* Experience with implementing MDM solutions, ideally in a B2C setting.

* Experience implementing traditional data warehouses leveraging appliances like Vertica, Teradata, etc., and Big Data solutions using open source technologies such as Hadoop, EMR, Hive, Scala, Spark, Pig, etc.

* Experience in implementing enterprise data management processes and governance is needed.

* Solid understanding of traditional data reporting, data aggregation and visualization tools

* Solid understanding of service-oriented and cloud-based architectural principles and patterns is required.

* Good understanding of Agile project management and the software development lifecycle are needed.

* Strong Leadership skills and ability to mentor developers and engineers is required.

* Strong communication skills with the ability to present solutions to the "C" level as well as the technical level.
