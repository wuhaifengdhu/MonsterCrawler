Come join our world-class in-house legal team!

Position is for a passionate data scientist or analyst to turn data into information, information into insight and insight into enablement of business decisions.

Data scientist responsibilities include conducting a full lifecycle of activities from scoping business needs, designing metrics, developing analysis and reporting capabilities and perform continuous monitoring of operational areas to identify improvements.

Key Responsibilities:

* Interpret data, analyze results using statistical techniques and provide on-going reporting

* Identify, analyze and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets

* Triangulate information across various data sets to provide meaningful insight

* Partner cross functionally with Dell IT and analytics teams to identify and acquire data and maintain databases where necessary

* Transform data and insights into effective visualizations for executive presentations

* Work closely with Legal teams and management to prioritize business and information needs

* Locate and define new process improvement opportunities

* Proficiency with Tableau Software and other business intelligence software

Preferred Skills / Experience:

* Proven work experience as a data scientist or analyst

* Strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, analyze and disseminate significant amounts of information with attention to detail and accuracy

* Technical expertise in creating data models, data mining and segmentation

* Strong knowledge of and experience with reporting packages and databases

* Knowledge of statistics and experience using statistical packages

* Strong verbal communication skills and adept in presenting findings visually

* Proficiency with Excel and PowerPoint

* BS or higher in Mathematics, Information Management or Statistics
