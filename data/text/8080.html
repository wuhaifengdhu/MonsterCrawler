Job Summary

The Business Operations Intern supports analysis and resolution of a wide variety of operational issues and needs. He/she assists the Business Operations team to ensure attainment of financial and operational goals within the department and in support of PROS' strategic objectives. The intern takes over dedicated roles in meetings or at other events. He/she will receive valuable insight into IT consulting and Cloud operations during the assignment, with the goal to own, improve, and report on specific operational processes with a minimum of guidance and supervision.

Primary Objectives:

* Support the Senior Manager, Business Operations in tracking and reporting on the strategic progress towards departmental objectives

* Support weekly meetings and other activities and follow up on outstanding actions to drive departmental performance (project status calls, account reviews, staffing calls, weekly operational meetings)

* Coordinate departmental communication strategies (via Outlook, Skype messaging, virtual meetings)

* Work closely with other departments or external groups to align expectations and meet timelines

* Assisting in the allocation of resources for the overall department with Resource Management Office team

* Provide operational support to corporate administrative functions: Accounting/Finance, Human Resources, Legal, Information Technology, etc.

Knowledge, Skills and Abilities

* Good written and verbal communication skills (MS Office, Power Point)

* Demonstrated ability to interpret operational metrics and apply statistical analysis of such (advanced Excel knowledge required, project management knowledge is a plus)

* Ability to understand strategic objectives

* Ability to think creatively to develop/improve processes

* Ability to work effectively with cross functional teams and to understand the specific departmental functions and purpose

* Team player with a positive mindset who enjoys working in an international and multi-cultural environment and embraces PROS values

* Willingness to deal with ambiguity and to work with a great deal of independency towards set goals

Education and Experience

Working towards a Bachelor's degree in Management/other Business discipline, Engineering, Computer Sciences or similar

Work Environment

Most work activities are performed in an office environment and require little to moderate physical exertion. Work activities may require periods of extended hours, critical deadlines and stressful situations. To successfully complete the tasks of this position, individuals must be able to communicate clearly (in writing and orally), comprehend complex business terminology, interpret numerical data, hear effectively, use hands and fingers to type, write, grasp/hold, and similar tasks.
