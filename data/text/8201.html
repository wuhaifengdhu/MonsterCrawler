Job Description

We are looking for a Professional Services Consultant to join our growing team!

In this role, you will develop and deliver detailed IT solutions through consulting project activities.

Responsibilities include:

Client identification through final invoicing for engagements requiring varied interpersonal and technical skills.

Technical responsibilities include problem identification, system architecture definition, hardware/software specification and/or design, implementation, testing, client training, and solution deployment.

Performance is typically evaluated based on utilization, i.e., billable hours.

Project management activities include interaction with company and client managers and cost/schedule monitoring.

May have some financial responsibilities including project cost estimating, proposal generation, and invoicing.

May participate in sales and proposal presentations in addition to completing ongoing team account activities. Identifies additional product/services opportunities in customer organization.

Performance is typically measured by the capture of the consulting engagement and/or delivery of agreed solutions within budgeted hours.

This is a commissioned position located in San Antonio, TX

Qualifications

Required Qualifications:

Bachelor's Degree in Software Engineering, Computer Science, Data Science, Electrical Engineering and 3-5 years of related experience.

Demonstrated development skills in SQL and JavaScript or Python.

Additional Qualifications:

Excellent communication and interpersonal skills

Able to work well individually as well as in a highly collaborative team

Demonstrated interest in learning new technologies

Self-motivated and autonomous.

Skilled in problem solving, troubleshooting, and analysis of algorithms

Ability to work in client facing roles and be the single point of contact for technical activities.

Ability to switch among multiple projects in short periods of time and sometimes support multiple simultaneous projects.

Software engineering experience, and have client-facing experience with software proof of concept, pilot and implementation activities.

Strong background in data analytics.

This position requires close collaboration with geographically diverse teams to ensure successful delivery of projects.

Candidate must have necessary skills to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.

Desirable Skills and Experience one or more of the following:

Data Science background and understanding

Experience in multiple roles of the software development life-cycle architecture, requirements, development, integration and/or system test, support for commercial, enterprise and/or SaaS production deployments.

Development/Test experience with Java and/or C++

ETL experience, e.g., Pentaho, Talend and/or commercial ETL products

Production implementation of one or more of RDBMS-based OLTP, RDBMS-based Data Warehouse or NoSQL-based systems

MapReduce or Hadoop-based analytic systems

Hardware and Server management and deployment

Virtualization technologies and management

Developer-level experience with modern Linux distributions

Application and system performance analysis and optimization

Application and system-level security

Team project management.

Experience reading/writing CSV, JSON, XML, and Unstructured data

Experience with NLP

Inside this Business Group

Intel Labs, the New Business Initiatives group (NBI), the Perceptual Computing Group and the New Devices Group (NDG). Additionally, New Devices Group and the New Business Initiatives group were combined into the New Business Group (NBG) which nests into NTG.
