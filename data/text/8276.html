We live in an era of remarkable change and opportunity. Data and technology are transforming industries, society, and even the workplace by creating professions that didn't exist before the emergence of cognitive computing, cloud, analytics, social, mobile and security. As the largest technology and consulting employer in the world, IBM is a leader in this global transformation and we encourage you to consider being part of this new team. In the role of the Cognitive Systems Architect, you will be responsible for the following: + Leading discovery workshops with selected clients to discover and shape Cognitive infrastructure opportunity + Leading solution design of infrastructure to support Cognitive High Performance Data Analytics (HPDA) and High Performance Computing (HPC) solutions + Being recognized as a subject matter expert on infrastructure for the specific cognitive workload + Providing deep skills in support of opportunities + Developing ecosystem around the specific solution workload + Leading our social presence and point of view for the workload + Teaming with and leveraging the Cognitive solutions units, offerings teams, R&D and global infrastructure If you are excited about Big Data & Analytics, High Performance Computing, Artificial Intelligence, with experience in areas such as Kinetica, NVIDIA, GPUs, Genomics, or Tensorflow, we want to talk to you. + At least 3 years' experience in infrastructure architecture and data science + At least 3 years' experience in Cognitive High Performance Data Analytics or High Performance Computing, Big Data & Analytics industry solution workloads or Machine Learning (deep learning and knowledge of associated frameworks and tools) + At least 3 years' experience in technical sales and architectural thinking using design or agile methods Sys HW Sales
