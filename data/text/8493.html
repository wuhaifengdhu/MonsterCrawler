Facebook's mission is to give people the power to share, and make the world more open and connected. Through our growing family of apps and services, we're building a different kind of company that helps billions of people around the world connect and share what matters most to them. Whether we're creating new products or helping a small business expand its reach, people at Facebook are builders at heart. Our global teams are constantly iterating, solving problems, and working together to make the world more open and accessible. Connecting the world takes every one of us—and we're just getting started.

The Business Integrity team protects users and ensures safe connections between users and businesses. We ensure our platform is a high quality ecosystem for users, developers, page admins, advertisers and businesses to create and promote digital content. Our focus on data analysis, machine learning and a robust infrastructure of back-end systems allows us to work effectively with our engineering and product teams to build proprietary tools and techniques to enforce the quality of all promoted content at scale.

Data Operations is the technical analytics team within Business Integrity and is looking for a candidate who enjoys the combination of analytics and program management. As a Technical Program Manager, you will play a key role with all of our cross-functional Business Integrity organizations, driving down key risk metrics and owning a critical part of managing our business through the right KPIs. This position is located in our Austin, Texas office.

Responsibilities

* Manage and move cross-functional Business Integrity metrics across all lines of the business

* Understand current abuse trends in a malicious environment

* Drive metrics discussions in the Business Integrity ecosystem

* Provide hands-on program management and operate in a nimble manner

* Perform risk management and change management on projects

* Provide day-to-day coordination and quality assurance for projects and tasks

* Drive internal process improvements across multiple teams and functions

* Interface with Engineering, Data Science and other cross-functional partners

Minimum Qualifications

* BA/BS in a technical or quantitative discipline and 7+ years of work experience in a related field

* Ability to influence data, business and engineering teams through an understanding of business and ecosystem needs

* Analytical and problem-solving skills

* Experience with performing analytics work and manipulating data sets

* Experience in program management and being a "connector" across teams

* Communication skills and experience working with technical teams

* Organizational and coordination skills along with multi-tasking capabilities

Preferred Qualifications

* Experience with leading programs for data/analytics teams
