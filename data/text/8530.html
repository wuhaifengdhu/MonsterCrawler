Outstanding opportunity for an engaging Data Analytics Leader to join a growing, global company whose innovation and strong execution has helped shape the dynamic Pet Health sector and drive fantastic shareholder returns! This leader will join an accomplished team of entrepreneurs driving continual advancements in pet health and IDEXX's data science platform.

Position summary:

We are building a world-class data business to harness the power of IDEXX's big data derived from a market-leading global portfolio of veterinary software, diagnostic labs, and IoT connected devices. We are seeking an entrepreneurial leader for our Analytics Team to help develop and deliver on our big data strategy. In this role, you will also extend your influence across the broader IDEXX organization via formal and informal connections that you will help build. As a direct report to the IDEXX big data business General Manager (GM), you will work as a member of the Leadership Team to help shape the strategy, set ambitious goals and drive execution to realize our vision. You will call upon your experience, drive, leadership skills, analytical capabilities and collaborative style to execute your responsibilities.

Specific duties:

* Develop and execute an Analytics strategy aligned with the overall business strategy to deliver insights, develop cross-organization processes and build capabilities.

* Identify and own clear KPIs and metrics to assess, communicate and guide analytics success.

* Lead your team to design and conduct advanced analytics, develop predictive models and create insightful visualizations to deliver actionable business and clinical insights for internal and external customers.

* Contribute individually in the execution of the above.

* Lead, motivate, manage, coach, develop and reward your team to achieve goals and attain ever-higher performance.

* Develop connections with other analytical leaders and their teams across IDEXX to extend your influence, strengthen coordination and drive cross-organization execution.

* Develop criteria and processes and gain needed buy-in to sharpen prioritization and improve analytical coordination within your team and with other analytical teams.

* Serve as a member of the IDEXX big data business Leadership Team contributing to business strategy and executional success.

* Work with the GM and Finance to develop and manage department budget including developing business cases to secure resources.

* Exhibit and inspire continual learning: Guide team to develop new techniques to identify and test hypotheses, work with Data Science R&D leaders to identify new internal and external data sources, stay current on business and technology trends to continuously evolve the IDEXX data science platform and the tools to support analytical needs.

* Maintain knowledge of Company products and services as well as developments in the industry. Participate in outside industry groups/forums related to business activities if/as needed.

Education:

* Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Economics, Mathematics, Econometrics or Social Sciences - Marketing, Psychology, Sociology.

* Master's Degree in related discipline or MBA preferred.

Experience/Competencies:

* 10 years business analytics experience, preferably in quantitative research including data mining, primary quantitative studies, statistical analysis and predictive modeling

* Deep knowledge on a broad range data science technologies and tools that include data access, statistical programming, modeling and data-visualization software with experience in tools like SAS, SQL, R, SPSS, Spark, ElasticSearch, BigQuery, Lavastorm and Tableau.

* Domain Experience in one or more areas including healthcare, insurance, finance, sales and marketing

* Strong people leadership skills and proven track record of leading an analytics team to generate actionable insights from multiple information sources while also developing team members and building capabilities.

* Excellent communication skills (presentations, correspondence, and 1-on-1) with well-developed interpersonal and listening skills able to collaborate effectively and professionally at all levels of the organization and build alignment.

* Proven ability to work and, when needed, lead collaboratively to create prioritization and coordination processes that are embraced across the organization.

* Excited by the possibilities of new data connections and addressing complex problems to distill insight.

* Financial literacy and business acumen

IDEXX Laboratories, Inc. is the leader in pet healthcare innovation, serving practicing veterinarians around the world with a broad range of diagnostic, software and data offerings. IDEXX is also a worldwide leader in livestock and poultry diagnostics as well as water and milk quality and safety tests. Headquartered in Maine, IDEXX Laboratories employs more than six thousand people, offers products to customers in over 175 countries, and recently joined the S&P 500 and the NASDAQ 100.

IDEXX attracts, rewards and inspires exceptionally talented and motivated people who are eager to participate in two of the most important technological revolutions of our time - biotechnology and information technology. We offer outstanding opportunities for personal growth and success, and support excellence, world-class standards, and continuous improvement.

IDEXX has a new state of the art, LEED certified, world campus which fosters an innovative and collaborative work environment and promotes wellness; offering an impressive onsite fitness center, health clinic, and many healthy dining choices! In support of health and wellness, IDEXX is also a tobacco free campus.

If you are looking for a challenging work environment that unleashes creativity and energy, is refreshingly honest and comfortably casual, and offers opportunities for unmatched personal growth, we want to hear from you!

About the Area: Portland, Maine recently ranked number 6 on Forbes magazine's list of America's Best Cities for Young Professionals, was ranked by Bloomberg Businessweek as one of the best places to raise kids, was listed by Forbes.com as one of America's Best Hipster Neighborhoods, was named the Foodiest city in the US by Bonappetit magazine, was ranked by Yahoo.com as having one of the healthiest and most affordable housing markets, made the top 10 list of healthiest cities for women by Women's Health Magazine, and has recently been nominated for "Best Town Ever" by Outside Magazine; the magazine chooses its top 10 places to live based on a balance of great culture, breathtaking scenery, reasonable home prices, low-stress living, and, of course, easy access to the outdoors!
